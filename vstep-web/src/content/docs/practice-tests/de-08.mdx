## Speaking Part 1: Interview

### Topic A — Festivals

**1. Do you like celebrations?**

> Yes, I **really enjoy** celebrations. I think they are a great time to **get together** with my family and friends. Celebrations make me feel **happy and excited**. I always look forward to holidays.

**2. What is your favorite festival?**

> My favorite festival is **Tet**, which is the Vietnamese New Year. During Tet, my whole family **comes together** to eat **traditional food** like bánh chưng. Children receive **lucky money**, and everyone is happy. **It is the most special time** of the year for me.

**3. Are there many celebrations in your hometown?**

> Yes, there are **quite a few** celebrations in my hometown. **Besides** Tet, we also have the **Mid-Autumn Festival**, **National Day**, and some **local festivals**. People in my hometown really enjoy celebrating and they always **prepare carefully** for every event.

**4. What do you usually celebrate?**

> I usually celebrate **Tet**, **birthdays**, and **Teacher's Day**. On Tet, I visit my grandparents and enjoy family meals. On birthdays, I have a small party with my friends. **On Teacher's Day**, I give flowers to my teachers to **show my gratitude**.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **get together** | Tụ họp | *A great time to get together with family* |
| **look forward to + N/Ving** | Mong chờ... | *I always look forward to holidays* |
| **It is the most + adj + N** | Đó là... nhất | *It is the most special time of the year* |
| **Besides + N, ...** | Ngoài... ra... | *Besides Tet, we also have Mid-Autumn Festival* |
| **show gratitude** | Thể hiện lòng biết ơn | *Give flowers to show my gratitude* |
:::

### Topic B — Museum

**1. Are there many museums in your hometown?**

> There are about **two or three museums** in my hometown. The biggest one is the **city museum**, which has many **historical artifacts**. It is not very big, but I think it is **interesting** and worth visiting.

**2. Do you think museums are useful for visitors?**

> Yes, I think museums are **very useful** for visitors. They help visitors **learn about** the history and culture of a place. **For example**, when tourists visit the Vietnam History Museum, they can understand more about **Vietnamese traditions** and the country's past.

**3. Do you often visit museums?**

> **To be honest**, I don't visit museums very often. I usually go about **once or twice a year**, usually when my school organizes a field trip. **However**, every time I go, I learn something new and feel **more interested** in history.

**4. Did you go to museums as a child?**

> Yes, when I was a child, my parents **took me to** the museum every summer. I remember feeling very **curious** about the old objects and pictures. Those visits **helped me develop** a love for learning about the past.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **learn about + N** | Học về... | *Learn about the history and culture* |
| **For example, ...** | Ví dụ... | *For example, when tourists visit...* |
| **To be honest, ...** | Thành thật mà nói... | *To be honest, I don't visit often* |
| **took me to + nơi** | Đưa tôi đến... | *My parents took me to the museum* |
| **help sb develop + N** | Giúp ai phát triển... | *Helped me develop a love for learning* |
:::

## Speaking Part 2: Discussion

> You are having a birthday party and your friends are invited. Where is the best place: **at home**, **at a restaurant**, or **in a park**?

### Bài mẫu:

> **I would choose a restaurant** for my birthday party. I think it is the **best place** to celebrate with my friends.
>
> **The first reason is that** a restaurant is very **convenient**. We don't need to cook food or clean up after the party. The restaurant will **prepare everything** for us — from the food to the drinks and decorations. So we can just enjoy the party and **have a good time**.
>
> **The second reason is that** restaurants usually have **delicious food** and a nice **atmosphere**. We can choose from many dishes, and there is enough space for everyone to sit and talk. **Besides**, we can also **take nice photos** at the restaurant to keep as memories.
>
> **I would not choose having a party at home** because my house is **not big enough** for many friends. I would also have to cook and clean, which is very **tiring**. **As for a park**, the weather might be **bad** — if it rains, the whole plan will be ruined. Also, there are no tables and chairs in the park.
>
> **In short**, a restaurant is the best option because it is convenient, comfortable, and everyone can enjoy the food and the party.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 2]
**Bước 1 — Chọn:** *I would choose + lựa chọn*

**Bước 2 — Lý do 1:** *The first reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 3 — Lý do 2:** *The second reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 4 — Loại bỏ:** *I would not choose... because...* / *As for..., it is...*

**Bước 5 — Kết:** *In short, ...*

| Từ vựng quan trọng | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **convenient** | tiện lợi |
| **atmosphere** | không khí, bầu không khí |
| **have a good time** | có khoảng thời gian vui vẻ |
| **not big enough** | không đủ lớn |
:::

## Speaking Part 3: Topic Development (nói 2 phút)

> **Studying abroad brings many benefits:** enhance knowledge and experience, promote career prospects, foster personal growth, develop a wider network.

### Bài mẫu:

> Today, I would like to talk about **why studying abroad brings many benefits**. I strongly agree with this idea, and I have several reasons.
>
> **Firstly**, studying abroad helps students **enhance their knowledge and experience**. Foreign universities often have **advanced programs** and **modern facilities**. Students can learn from **experienced professors** and use the latest technology. This helps them gain **high-quality education** that they may not find at home.
>
> **Secondly**, studying abroad can **promote career prospects**. When students graduate from a foreign university, they have an **international degree**. This helps them **stand out** when they apply for jobs. Many companies prefer to hire people who have **studied overseas** because they have more experience and skills.
>
> **Thirdly**, living in another country helps **foster personal growth**. Students learn to **be independent** — they cook, clean, and manage their money by themselves. They also learn to **solve problems** on their own. **As a result**, they become more **mature and confident**.
>
> **Finally**, studying abroad helps students **develop a wider network**. They meet people from many different countries and **build friendships**. These international connections can be very **valuable** for their future career and personal life.
>
> **In conclusion**, I believe that studying abroad is a **wonderful experience** that helps young people grow in many ways.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 3]
**Mở bài:** *Today, I would like to talk about... I strongly agree with this idea.*

**Thân bài:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Thirdly, ... Finally, ...*

**Kết:** *In conclusion, I believe...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **enhance + N** | Nâng cao... | *Enhance their knowledge and experience* |
| **stand out** | Nổi bật | *This helps them stand out when applying for jobs* |
| **be independent** | Tự lập | *Students learn to be independent* |
| **As a result, ...** | Kết quả là... | *As a result, they become more mature* |
| **build friendships** | Xây dựng tình bạn | *Build friendships with people from many countries* |
| **promote career prospects** | Thúc đẩy triển vọng nghề nghiệp | *Studying abroad promotes career prospects* |
:::

## Writing Task 1: Informal Letter

> Your friend has won 2 contests and decided to travel for 2 weeks. Write a letter about: summer weather in your country, where to go, and interesting activities.

### Bài mẫu:

> Dear Sarah,
>
> **Congratulations on** winning two contests! I'm so **happy and proud** of you. I heard you want to travel to Vietnam for two weeks — that's a great idea! **Let me help you** plan your trip.
>
> **First, about the weather.** Summer in Vietnam is very **hot and humid**. The temperature is usually about **30 to 38 degrees**. It can also rain suddenly in the afternoon, so you should bring a **light raincoat** and **sunscreen**.
>
> **Next, about where to go.** I **would suggest** going to **Da Nang** and **Hoi An**. Da Nang has **beautiful beaches** where you can swim and relax. Hoi An is an **ancient town** with colorful lanterns — it is very **romantic** and perfect for taking photos. If you have more time, you can also visit **Phu Quoc Island**.
>
> **Finally, about activities.** You should **try snorkeling** to see the beautiful coral reefs under the sea. You should also visit **local markets** and eat **fresh seafood** — it is very cheap and delicious. **Don't forget** to try **Vietnamese coffee** — I'm sure you will love it!
>
> I'm sure you will **have an amazing time** in Vietnam! **Write back** and tell me your schedule so I can help you more.
>
> **Love,**
> Mai

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Thư thân mật]
**Mở thư:** *Dear + tên, Congratulations on...! / Let me help you...*

**Thân thư:** Trả lời theo 3 ý: thời tiết → nơi đi → hoạt động

**Kết thư:** *I'm sure you will have... / Write back. Love, + tên*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **Congratulations on + Ving/N** | Chúc mừng về... |
| **I would suggest + Ving** | Tôi gợi ý nên... |
| **Don't forget to + V** | Đừng quên... |
| **have an amazing time** | Có khoảng thời gian tuyệt vời |
| **You should try + Ving** | Bạn nên thử... |

**Lưu ý:** Thư thân mật được dùng **short forms** (I'm, It's, don't) và giọng văn **vui vẻ, gần gũi**. Đếm đủ **120+ từ**.
:::

## Writing Task 2: Informal Letter

> Your foreign friend loves Vietnam. Write a letter about 3 beautiful cities in Vietnam and suggest one city to visit with activities.

### Bài mẫu:

> Dear Anna,
>
> I'm so **glad to hear** that you love Vietnam! **Let me tell you** about three beautiful cities that you should know about.
>
> **The first city is Hanoi**, the capital of Vietnam. It is **rich in history** and culture. There are many old **temples** and the famous **Hoan Kiem Lake**. The food here is also very special, like **phở** and **bún chả**.
>
> **The second city is Da Nang.** It is a modern city with **stunning beaches** and the famous **Golden Bridge** at Ba Na Hills. Many tourists love Da Nang because it is **clean and beautiful**.
>
> **The third city is Ho Chi Minh City**, the biggest city in Vietnam. It is very **vibrant and exciting**. You can visit **Ben Thanh Market**, **Notre Dame Cathedral**, and enjoy the **nightlife**.
>
> **If I had to suggest one city**, I would **recommend Da Nang**. The beaches like **My Khe Beach** are perfect for swimming. You can also take a short trip to **Hoi An ancient town** to see beautiful lanterns. **Moreover**, you should try **mì Quảng** — a famous local dish. And if you love nature, you can go **snorkeling** to see **coral reefs**.
>
> I **highly recommend** Da Nang — you will love it! **Let me know** when you plan to come so I can help you.
>
> **Love,**
> Trang

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Thư thân mật]
**Mở thư:** *Dear + tên, I'm glad to hear... / Let me tell you about...*

**Thân thư:** Giới thiệu 3 thành phố → gợi ý 1 thành phố + hoạt động

**Kết thư:** *I highly recommend... / Let me know when... Love, + tên*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **rich in history** | Giàu lịch sử |
| **It is famous for + N** | Nơi đó nổi tiếng về... |
| **I highly recommend + N/Ving** | Tôi rất khuyên nên... |
| **If I had to suggest one, I would...** | Nếu phải gợi ý một nơi, tôi sẽ... |
| **vibrant and exciting** | Sôi động và thú vị |

**Lưu ý:** Thư thân mật được dùng **short forms** (I'm, It's, don't) và giọng văn **vui vẻ, gần gũi**. Đếm đủ **120+ từ**.
:::
