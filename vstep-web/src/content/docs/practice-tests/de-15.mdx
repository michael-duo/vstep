## Speaking Part 1: Interview

### Topic A — Water

**1. Do you drink a lot of water daily?**

> Yes, I try to **drink a lot of water** every day. I usually drink about **2 liters** of water per day. I always carry a **water bottle** with me so that I don't forget to drink. I think drinking water is very **important for my health**.

**2. How much water is enough per day?**

> **According to** doctors, people should drink about **2 liters** of water per day, which is about **8 glasses**. However, if you **exercise** a lot or the weather is **hot**, you may need to drink more. Drinking enough water helps our body **stay hydrated** and keeps our **skin** healthy and fresh.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **I try to + V** | Tôi cố gắng... | *I try to drink a lot of water every day* |
| **According to + N** | Theo... | *According to doctors, people should drink about 2 liters* |
| **stay hydrated** | Giữ đủ nước cho cơ thể | *Drinking water helps our body stay hydrated* |
:::

### Topic B — Clothes

**1. What do you usually wear to work?**

> I usually wear **casual clothes** to work, like a **T-shirt** and jeans. I prefer clothes that are **comfortable** because I have to sit and work at my desk for many hours. On special days like **meetings**, I wear a **formal shirt** and trousers.

**2. Do you enjoy buying clothes?**

> Yes, I quite enjoy **shopping for clothes**. I usually go shopping **once a month** with my friends. I like to look for clothes that are **fashionable** but also **affordable**. **However**, I don't spend too much money on clothes because I need to **save money** for other things.

**3. Are you willing to wear a uniform?**

> Yes, I think wearing a uniform is a **good idea**. **Firstly**, it helps me **save time** in the morning because I don't need to choose what to wear. **Secondly**, uniforms create **equality** among people — nobody can show off with expensive clothes. **However**, I think uniforms should be **comfortable** and not too strict.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **I prefer + N + that + V** | Tôi thích... mà... | *I prefer clothes that are comfortable* |
| **save time** | Tiết kiệm thời gian | *It helps me save time in the morning* |
| **create equality** | Tạo ra sự bình đẳng | *Uniforms create equality among people* |
| **However, ...** | Tuy nhiên... | *However, I think uniforms should be comfortable* |
:::

## Speaking Part 2: Discussion

> If you had a lot of money, what would you do: **start a business (start-up project)**, **put money in the bank**, or **buy gold**?

### Bài mẫu:

> **I would choose to put money in the bank.** I think it is the **safest and smartest** option.
>
> **The first reason is that** putting money in the bank is very **safe** and has **low risk**. My money is **protected** by the bank, so I don't have to worry about losing it. Even if something bad happens, the bank will **guarantee** my savings.
>
> **The second reason is that** I can earn **interest** every month. The bank will pay me a small amount of money for keeping my savings there. **In this way**, my money **grows** slowly without me having to do anything. I can also **withdraw** money easily whenever I need it.
>
> **I would not choose starting a business** because it is very **risky**. Many start-up projects **fail**, and I could **lose all my money**. I don't have enough experience to run a business. **As for buying gold**, the price of gold **fluctuates** a lot. Sometimes the price goes up, but sometimes it drops **dramatically**. This is too **unpredictable** for me.
>
> **In short**, putting money in the bank is the best choice because it is safe, earns interest, and I can use it anytime.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 2]
**Bước 1 — Chọn:** *I would choose + lựa chọn*

**Bước 2 — Lý do 1:** *The first reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 3 — Lý do 2:** *The second reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 4 — Loại bỏ:** *I would not choose... because...* / *As for..., ...*

**Bước 5 — Kết:** *In short, ...*

| Từ vựng quan trọng | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **low risk** | rủi ro thấp |
| **interest** (tài chính) | lãi suất |
| **withdraw money** | rút tiền |
| **fluctuate** | dao động, lên xuống |
| **unpredictable** | không thể dự đoán |
| **lose all money** | mất hết tiền |
:::

## Speaking Part 3: Topic Development (nói 2 phút)

> **Factors to consider when choosing a job:** interests, salary, working environment.

### Bài mẫu:

> Today, I would like to talk about **the factors to consider when choosing a job**. I think choosing a job is one of the **most important decisions** in our lives, and there are three main factors we should think about.
>
> **Firstly**, we should consider our **interests**. If we choose a job that we **enjoy**, we will feel **motivated** and happy every day. We will not feel bored or want to quit. **On the other hand**, if we do a job we don't like, we will feel **miserable** even if the salary is high. **Therefore**, interests should be the first thing to think about.
>
> **Secondly**, **salary** is also an important factor. We need a salary that is **high enough** to **cover our living expenses** like rent, food, and transportation. **Moreover**, a good salary allows us to **save money** for the future and support our families. Nobody wants to work hard but still **struggle financially**.
>
> **Thirdly**, a **friendly working environment** is very important. When we have **supportive colleagues** and a **kind boss**, we feel **comfortable** and can work more **effectively**. A bad working environment with unfriendly people can cause a lot of **stress** and make us want to leave.
>
> **In conclusion**, when choosing a job, we should carefully consider our interests, salary, and working environment to find the **best fit** for ourselves.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 3]
**Mở bài:** *Today, I would like to talk about...*

**Thân bài:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Thirdly, ...*

**Kết:** *In conclusion, ...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **consider + N** | Cân nhắc... | *We should consider our interests* |
| **cover living expenses** | Trang trải chi phí sinh hoạt | *A salary high enough to cover living expenses* |
| **supportive colleagues** | Đồng nghiệp hay hỗ trợ | *Supportive colleagues and a kind boss* |
| **On the other hand, ...** | Mặt khác... | *On the other hand, if we do a job we don't like* |
| **Therefore, ...** | Vì vậy... | *Therefore, interests should be the first thing* |
| **struggle financially** | Khó khăn về tài chính | *Nobody wants to struggle financially* |
:::

## Writing Task 1: Informal Letter

> You have just received a puppy. Write a letter to your friend to tell: **when and who** gave it to you, **what name** you gave it, and **why** you chose that name.

### Bài mẫu:

> Dear Minh,
>
> **Guess what?** I just got a **puppy**! I'm so **excited** and I can't wait to tell you about it.
>
> **My parents gave** it to me **last Sunday** as a **birthday present**. When I came home from school, I saw a small box in the living room. I opened it and found the **cutest little dog** inside! I was so **surprised** and happy that I almost cried.
>
> It is a very **small dog** with **golden fur** and big brown eyes. It looks like a little **teddy bear**! I decided to name it **Lucky**. I chose this name because I feel very **lucky** to have such a wonderful pet. **Also**, the day I got Lucky was my birthday, which is a very **special day** for me. So the name Lucky **reminds me of** this happy moment.
>
> Lucky is very **playful and friendly**. He loves to run around the house and **chew on** everything! I'm learning how to **take care of** him — feeding him, bathing him, and taking him for **walks** every evening.
>
> **You should come** and meet Lucky sometime! I'm sure you will **fall in love** with him.
>
> **Love,**
> Huy

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Thư thân mật]
**Mở thư:** *Dear + tên, Guess what? I just got a...*

**Thân thư:** Kể theo thời gian: ai tặng, khi nào → mô tả → đặt tên và lý do

**Kết thư:** *You should come and... Love, + tên*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **Guess what?** | Đoán xem! (mở đầu hào hứng) |
| **I decided to name it + tên** | Mình quyết định đặt tên là... |
| **reminds me of + N** | Nhắc mình nhớ đến... |
| **take care of + N** | Chăm sóc... |
| **fall in love with + N** | Yêu thích... |

**Lưu ý:** Thư thân mật dùng **short forms** (I'm, can't, it's) và giọng văn **vui vẻ, hào hứng**. Đếm đủ **120+ từ**.
:::

## Writing Task 2: Essay — Discuss both views

> Some people say we should **think twice before eating fast food** because of health problems. Others believe **fast food is good** for busy people. Which opinion do you agree with?

### Bài mẫu:

> **Nowadays**, fast food is very **popular** around the world. **Some people say** that we should think carefully before eating fast food because it is **bad for our health**. **Others believe** that fast food is **convenient** for busy people. **In my opinion**, I agree that we should **be careful** about eating fast food, and I will explain my reasons.
>
> **Firstly**, fast food is **harmful to our health**. Most fast food contains a lot of **fat, sugar, and salt**, which can **lead to** serious diseases like **obesity**, **heart disease**, and **diabetes**. **For example**, eating hamburgers and fried chicken every day can make us gain weight very quickly. **Moreover**, fast food usually lacks important **vitamins and nutrients** that our body needs to stay healthy.
>
> **It is true that** fast food is **convenient and saves time** for busy people. Many workers and students don't have time to cook, so they buy fast food for lunch. **However**, there are **healthier alternatives** that are also quick and easy. **For instance**, people can prepare **simple meals** at home like sandwiches or salads, or they can buy **healthy lunch boxes** from restaurants. These options are just as **fast** but much **healthier** than fast food.
>
> **In conclusion**, although fast food is convenient, it can cause many **health problems**. I believe that **health should always come first**. We should try to eat **healthy food** as much as possible, even when we are busy.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Agree or Disagree (chọn 1 phía)]
**Mở bài:** *Some people say... Others believe... In my opinion, I agree that...*

**Thân bài 1 (Lý do chính):** *Firstly, ... For example, ... Moreover, ...*

**Thân bài 2 (Phản biện ý kia):** *It is true that... However, ... For instance, ...*

**Kết luận:** *In conclusion, ... I believe that...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **be harmful to + N** | Có hại cho... |
| **lead to + N** | Dẫn đến... |
| **It is true that... However, ...** | Đúng là... Tuy nhiên... |
| **healthier alternatives** | Lựa chọn lành mạnh hơn |
| **For instance, ...** | Ví dụ... |
| **Health should always come first** | Sức khoẻ luôn phải được ưu tiên |

**Lưu ý:** Bài luận cần **ít nhất 150 từ**. Khi đề bài hỏi "Which opinion do you agree with?", chọn 1 phía rồi bảo vệ. Nên nhượng bộ ý kiến đối lập ở thân bài 2 rồi phản biện lại.
:::
