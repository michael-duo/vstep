## Speaking Part 1: Interview

### Topic A — Feelings

**1. What things make you happy?**

> Many things **make me happy**. I feel happy when I **spend time with** my family, especially when we eat dinner together. I also feel happy when I **listen to music** or **go out** with my friends. **Simple things** in life make me the happiest.

**2. Do you think people with more talent are happier?**

> No, I **don't think so**. I believe happiness **does not depend on** talent. Some people are very talented but still feel **stressed and lonely**. **In my opinion**, happiness comes from having **good relationships**, good health, and being **grateful** for what we have.

**3. Are you a happy person?**

> Yes, I think I am a **happy person**. I try to **stay positive** every day. **Of course**, sometimes I feel sad or worried, but I always try to look at the **bright side** of things. My family and friends also **make me feel** happy and loved.

**4. Does the weather affect your feelings?**

> Yes, the weather **definitely affects** my feelings. When it is **sunny**, I feel more **energetic** and want to go outside. But when it is **rainy and cloudy**, I feel a bit **lazy and sleepy**. I think many people feel the same way.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **make me happy** | Khiến tôi vui | *Many things make me happy* |
| **does not depend on + N** | Không phụ thuộc vào... | *Happiness does not depend on talent* |
| **In my opinion, ...** | Theo ý kiến tôi... | *In my opinion, happiness comes from...* |
| **stay positive** | Giữ thái độ tích cực | *I try to stay positive every day* |
| **look at the bright side** | Nhìn vào mặt tốt | *I try to look at the bright side of things* |
:::

### Topic B — Colors

**1. What is your favorite color?**

> My favorite color is **blue**. I like blue because it makes me feel **calm and peaceful**. When I look at the **blue sky** or the **blue sea**, I feel very relaxed. I also like to wear **blue clothes** because they look nice on me.

**2. Is it the same color you liked as a child?**

> No, it is **different**. When I was a child, I liked **red** because it was very **bright and eye-catching**. But as I grew up, I started to prefer **softer colors** like blue. I think our taste in colors **changes as we get older**.

**3. What colors are popular among your friends?**

> I think **black and white** are the most popular colors among my friends. Most of them like to wear black or white clothes because they are **easy to match** with other clothes. Some of my friends also like **pastel colors** like pink and light blue.

**4. Are there any colors you don't like?**

> Yes, I don't really like **bright yellow**. I think it is too **strong** for my eyes and it doesn't look good on my skin. I also don't like **dark brown** because it looks a bit **dull and boring** to me.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **make me feel + adj** | Khiến tôi cảm thấy... | *Blue makes me feel calm and peaceful* |
| **as I grew up** | Khi tôi lớn lên | *As I grew up, I started to prefer softer colors* |
| **easy to match with** | Dễ phối với... | *Black is easy to match with other clothes* |
| **change as we get older** | Thay đổi khi ta lớn lên | *Our taste in colors changes as we get older* |
| **too + adj + for** | Quá... cho... | *Too strong for my eyes* |
:::

## Speaking Part 2: Discussion

> Your hometown holds a festival and foreign visitors come. Which role would you choose: **tour guide**, **security MC**, or **traffic instructor**?

### Bài mẫu:

> **I would choose to be a tour guide.** I think this is the **best role** for me during the festival.
>
> **The first reason is that** as a tour guide, I can **introduce** our culture, food, and history to foreign visitors. I can tell them about the **meaning** of the festival, the **traditional activities**, and the local dishes. This helps visitors **understand and enjoy** the festival more.
>
> **The second reason is that** being a tour guide gives me a chance to **practice my English**. I can talk to foreign visitors and **make friends** with them. This is a great **opportunity** for me to improve my communication skills and learn about their cultures too.
>
> **I would not choose to be a security MC** because this job needs **professional training** and special skills. I am just a student, so I am not qualified for this role. **As for being a traffic instructor**, it is quite **boring** because I would only direct traffic and have **no chance** to interact with the visitors or introduce our culture.
>
> **In short**, being a tour guide is the best choice because I can share my culture and also practice my English.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 2]
**Bước 1 — Chọn:** *I would choose + lựa chọn*

**Bước 2 — Lý do 1:** *The first reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 3 — Lý do 2:** *The second reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 4 — Loại bỏ:** *I would not choose... because...* / *As for..., it is...*

**Bước 5 — Kết:** *In short, ...*

| Từ vựng quan trọng | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **introduce** | giới thiệu |
| **opportunity** | cơ hội |
| **professional training** | đào tạo chuyên nghiệp |
| **interact with** | giao tiếp, tương tác với |
:::

## Speaking Part 3: Topic Development (nói 2 phút)

> **It is necessary to study a language:** better communication, job opportunities, understanding about culture.

### Bài mẫu:

> Today, I would like to talk about **why it is necessary to study a foreign language**. I strongly agree with this idea, and I have three reasons.
>
> **Firstly**, studying a language helps us have **better communication**. In today's world, people travel and work in many countries. If we know a foreign language like English, we can **talk to people** from different countries easily. **For example**, when I travel to another country, I can ask for directions, order food, and **make friends** with local people.
>
> **Secondly**, knowing a foreign language gives us more **job opportunities**. Many companies in Vietnam need employees who can **speak English well**. People who know a foreign language often have a **higher salary** and more chances to be **promoted**. **Therefore**, studying a language is very **beneficial** for our career.
>
> **Thirdly**, studying a language helps us **understand about culture**. When we learn a language, we also learn about the **customs, traditions**, and way of life of that country. This helps us **respect** other cultures and become more **open-minded**. **For instance**, when I study English, I also learn about Western holidays like Christmas and Halloween.
>
> **In conclusion**, I believe that studying a foreign language is **very important** because it helps us communicate, find better jobs, and understand the world around us.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 3]
**Mở bài:** *Today, I would like to talk about... I strongly agree with this idea.*

**Thân bài:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Thirdly, ...*

**Kết:** *In conclusion, I believe...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **It is necessary for sb to + V** | Ai đó cần phải... | *It is necessary to study a foreign language* |
| **Therefore, ...** | Vì vậy... | *Therefore, studying a language is beneficial* |
| **For instance, ...** | Ví dụ... | *For instance, when I study English...* |
| **be open-minded** | Cởi mở | *Become more open-minded* |
| **be beneficial for + N** | Có lợi cho... | *Studying a language is beneficial for our career* |
| **customs and traditions** | Phong tục và truyền thống | *Learn about the customs and traditions* |
:::

## Writing Task 1: Formal Letter

> You are organizing an IT training course for employees. Write a letter to the instructor about: date, place, and details of the course; number of trainees and equipment available; accommodation options.

### Bài mẫu:

> Dear Mr. Johnson,
>
> **I am writing to** provide you with information about the upcoming IT training course at our company. We are very pleased that you have agreed to be the instructor.
>
> **Regarding the date and place**, the course will **take place** from **June 10th to June 14th**, from 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM each day. It will be held in the **conference room** on the 3rd floor of our main office at 25 Le Loi Street, Da Nang.
>
> **Regarding the trainees and equipment**, there will be **approximately 30 trainees**. They are office staff who need to improve their **computer skills**. Our company will provide **30 laptops**, a **projector**, and **high-speed Wi-Fi**. **Could you please let me know** if you need any additional equipment?
>
> **Regarding accommodation**, we can book a room for you at the **Grand Hotel**, which is only **5 minutes** from our office. **Alternatively**, there is a **guesthouse** nearby if you prefer a quieter place. All **accommodation costs** will be covered by our company. Please let me know your **preference**.
>
> **I look forward to** your confirmation. Please do not hesitate to **contact me** if you have any questions.
>
> **Yours sincerely,**
> Nguyen Van Duc

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Thư trang trọng]
**Mở thư:** *Dear Mr./Ms. + tên, I am writing to provide you with information about...*

**Thân thư:** Trình bày theo 3 ý: ngày/nơi → số lượng/thiết bị → chỗ ở

**Kết thư:** *I look forward to your confirmation. Yours sincerely, + tên*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **I am writing to + V** | Tôi viết thư để... |
| **The course will take place from... to...** | Khoá học diễn ra từ... đến... |
| **There will be approximately + số** | Sẽ có khoảng... |
| **Could you please let me know if...** | Xin vui lòng cho tôi biết nếu... |
| **I look forward to + N/Ving** | Tôi mong chờ... |
| **Alternatively, ...** | Ngoài ra, có thể chọn... |

**Lưu ý:** Thư trang trọng **không dùng short forms** (viết đầy đủ: I am, do not, will not). Giọng văn **lịch sự, chuyên nghiệp**. Đếm đủ **120+ từ**.
:::

## Writing Task 2: Formal Complaint Letter

> You bought a new camera with problems, returned it, and spoke to a company representative a week ago, but it is still not repaired. Write a letter to the company to: introduce yourself, explain the problem, and request action.

### Bài mẫu:

> Dear Sir or Madam,
>
> **I am writing to** express my dissatisfaction with the service I have received regarding my recent purchase of a camera from your company.
>
> **My name is** Tran Minh Anh, and I purchased a **Model X200 camera** on **May 5th** from your store at 12 Nguyen Hue Street, Ho Chi Minh City. The order number is **#45678**. **Unfortunately**, the camera has several problems. The photos are **blurry**, and the **battery drains** very quickly — it only lasts about one hour.
>
> I returned the camera to your store and **spoke to** a representative named Mr. Tuan on **May 20th**. He promised that the camera would be **repaired within three days**. **However**, it has been **one week** and I have not received any update or response from your company. **I am very disappointed** with this situation.
>
> **I would like to request** that you either **repair my camera immediately** or **replace it** with a new one. If neither option is possible, I **expect a full refund**. I hope this matter will be **resolved as soon as possible**.
>
> **I look forward to** your prompt response. You can reach me at 0901-234-567 or email minh.anh@email.com.
>
> **Yours faithfully,**
> Tran Minh Anh

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Thư khiếu nại]
**Mở thư:** *Dear Sir or Madam, I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with...*

**Thân thư:** Giới thiệu → trình bày vấn đề → yêu cầu giải quyết

**Kết thư:** *I look forward to your prompt response. Yours faithfully, + tên*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with + N** | Tôi viết để bày tỏ sự không hài lòng về... |
| **I purchased + N + on + ngày** | Tôi đã mua... vào ngày... |
| **However, ...** | Tuy nhiên... |
| **I would like to request + N/that** | Tôi muốn yêu cầu... |
| **expect a full refund** | Mong đợi hoàn tiền đầy đủ |
| **resolved as soon as possible** | Giải quyết sớm nhất có thể |

**Lưu ý:** Thư khiếu nại cần giọng văn **lịch sự nhưng cương quyết**. Nêu rõ **ngày mua, mã đơn hàng, tên sản phẩm** để công ty dễ xác minh. Đếm đủ **150+ từ**.
:::
