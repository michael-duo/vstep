## Speaking Part 1: Interview

### Topic A — Summer Holidays

**1. Where do you go for your summer holiday?**

> Every summer, I usually go to the **beach** with my family. We often visit **Nha Trang** or **Vung Tau** because the beaches there are very **beautiful and clean**. Sometimes, we also go back to our **hometown** in the countryside to visit our grandparents.

**2. How do you enjoy your summer holiday?**

> I enjoy my summer holiday by doing many **fun activities**. I love **swimming** in the sea, eating **fresh seafood**, and **taking photos** of the sunset. I also **spend time with my family** — we cook together, play games, and just relax. **In addition**, I like to **read books** that I don't have time to read during the school year.

**3. Is summer the best holiday?**

> Yes, I think summer is the **best holiday** in a year. **Firstly**, it is the **longest holiday**, so I have enough time to travel and rest. **Secondly**, the weather is great for **outdoor activities** like swimming and hiking. **Moreover**, after a hard school year, summer is the perfect time to **recharge** my energy and prepare for the next year.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **spend time with + người** | Dành thời gian với... | *I spend time with my family* |
| **In addition, ...** | Ngoài ra... | *In addition, I like to read books* |
| **Moreover, ...** | Hơn nữa... | *Moreover, after a hard school year...* |
| **recharge energy** | Nạp lại năng lượng | *Summer is the perfect time to recharge* |
| **Firstly, ... Secondly, ...** | Thứ nhất... Thứ hai... | *Firstly, it is the longest holiday* |
:::

### Topic B — Cooking

**1. Do you like to cook?**

> Yes, I **enjoy cooking** very much. I usually cook **simple dishes** like fried rice, soup, and stir-fried vegetables. Cooking is very **relaxing** for me. I feel happy when I cook a meal and my family **enjoys eating** it.

**2. Do you prefer cooking at home or eating at a restaurant?**

> I **prefer cooking at home** because it is **healthier** and **cheaper** than eating at a restaurant. When I cook at home, I can choose **fresh ingredients** and control the amount of oil and salt. **However**, I sometimes eat at restaurants on **special occasions** like birthdays.

**3. What cooking programs do you like to watch?**

> I like watching **MasterChef** because it is very **exciting and entertaining**. The chefs are very talented, and I can learn many **new recipes** from them. I also enjoy watching **Vietnamese cooking shows** on YouTube because they teach me how to cook **traditional dishes** step by step.

**4. Would you like to become a chef in the future?**

> No, I **don't want to** become a chef. I think being a chef is very **stressful** because you have to work long hours in a hot kitchen. I just see cooking as a **hobby** — something I do for fun. I would like to have a **different career**, but I will always enjoy cooking at home.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **enjoy + Ving** | Thích làm gì | *I enjoy cooking very much* |
| **prefer + Ving** | Thích... hơn | *I prefer cooking at home* |
| **healthier and cheaper than** | Tốt cho sức khỏe hơn và rẻ hơn | *It is healthier and cheaper than eating out* |
| **on special occasions** | Vào những dịp đặc biệt | *I eat at restaurants on special occasions* |
| **see... as + N** | Coi... như là... | *I see cooking as a hobby* |
:::

## Speaking Part 2: Discussion

> Your English teacher is going to retire. Your class wants to hold a farewell party. Which place: **at school**, **at the teacher's house**, or **in a restaurant**?

### Bài mẫu:

> **I would choose to hold the farewell party in a restaurant.** I think a restaurant is the **best place** for this special event.
>
> **The first reason is that** a restaurant is very **convenient**. We **don't have to cook** or prepare anything — the restaurant staff will **take care of everything**, including food, drinks, and decoration. This means we can **focus on** celebrating with our teacher instead of worrying about preparation.
>
> **The second reason is that** a restaurant has a **beautiful and elegant atmosphere**. This shows our **respect and gratitude** to our teacher. She has taught us for many years, so she deserves a **special celebration**. We can also **take nice photos** together to remember this day.
>
> **I would not choose having the party at school** because the school environment is **too formal and boring**. It feels like a normal school day, not a celebration. **As for the teacher's house**, it would be very **inconvenient** for her. She would have to **clean and prepare** everything, which is not fair because this party is supposed to be **a gift for her**.
>
> **In short**, a restaurant is the best choice because it is convenient, has a nice atmosphere, and properly shows our love for our teacher.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 2]
**Bước 1 — Chọn:** *I would choose + lựa chọn*

**Bước 2 — Lý do 1:** *The first reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 3 — Lý do 2:** *The second reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 4 — Loại bỏ:** *I would not choose... because...* / *As for..., it would be...*

**Bước 5 — Kết:** *In short, ...*

| Từ vựng quan trọng | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **take care of everything** | Lo liệu mọi thứ |
| **respect and gratitude** | Sự kính trọng và biết ơn |
| **elegant atmosphere** | Không khí trang nhã |
| **inconvenient** | Bất tiện |
| **deserve + N** | Xứng đáng được... |
:::

## Speaking Part 3: Topic Development (nói 2 phút)

> **Living far from home has many disadvantages:** homesickness, high living costs, and dangers.

### Bài mẫu:

> Today, I would like to talk about **the disadvantages of living far from home**. Many young people in Vietnam move to big cities for study or work, but I think living far from home has several problems.
>
> **Firstly**, one of the biggest disadvantages is **homesickness**. When young people live far from their family, they often feel **lonely and sad**, especially during **holidays and festivals** like Tet. They miss their parents' cooking, their old friends, and the **familiar environment** at home. When they are **sick or in trouble**, there is no family member nearby to help them. This can lead to **stress and depression**.
>
> **Secondly**, the **cost of living** in big cities is much **higher** than in small towns or the countryside. Young people have to pay for **rent, food, transportation**, and many other things. Many students only receive a **small allowance** from their parents, so they feel a lot of **financial pressure**. Some students even have to work **part-time jobs** to earn extra money, which affects their studies.
>
> **Thirdly**, living alone in a big city can be **dangerous**. Young people may face **unsafe situations** such as theft, scams, or traffic accidents. They are not familiar with the area, and they **don't know** the people around them. **Without family protection**, they are more **vulnerable** to these risks.
>
> **In conclusion**, living far from home has many disadvantages such as homesickness, high living costs, and dangers. Young people should **think carefully** before making this decision and always **keep in touch** with their family.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 3]
**Mở bài:** *Today, I would like to talk about... I think living far from home has several problems.*

**Thân bài:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Thirdly, ...*

**Kết:** *In conclusion, ... Young people should...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **One of the biggest disadvantages is + N** | Một trong những nhược điểm lớn nhất là... | *One of the biggest disadvantages is homesickness* |
| **lead to + N** | Dẫn đến... | *This can lead to stress and depression* |
| **cost of living** | Chi phí sinh hoạt | *The cost of living is much higher* |
| **financial pressure** | Áp lực tài chính | *They feel a lot of financial pressure* |
| **keep in touch with + sb** | Giữ liên lạc với ai | *Always keep in touch with their family* |
| **Without + N, ...** | Không có... | *Without family protection, they are more vulnerable* |
:::

## Writing Task 1: Informal Letter

> Your Australian friend wants to visit Danang in Vietnam. You have been there before. Write a letter to tell him how to get there, what activities to do, and why you chose Danang.

### Bài mẫu:

> Dear Tom,
>
> **I'm so happy** to hear that you want to visit Danang! It is one of the **most beautiful cities** in Vietnam, and I'm sure you will **love it**. **Let me tell you** everything you need to know.
>
> **First**, about **how to get there**. The best way is to fly from Sydney to **Ho Chi Minh City** or **Hanoi**, and then take a **domestic flight** to Danang. The flight from Ho Chi Minh City to Danang takes only about **1 hour and 20 minutes**. You can also find some **direct flights** from Australia to Danang, which is even more convenient.
>
> **Second**, there are many **exciting activities** in Danang. You should definitely visit **My Khe Beach** — it is one of the **most beautiful beaches** in Asia. You can **swim**, **surf**, and enjoy **fresh seafood** right on the beach. **Don't forget** to visit **Ba Na Hills** and take photos at the famous **Golden Bridge**. I also recommend trying the local food like **mi Quang** and **banh xeo** — they are **incredibly delicious**!
>
> **Finally**, I chose Danang because the weather is **beautiful**, the people are very **friendly**, and the food is **cheap and delicious**. It is a perfect place for a holiday. **You won't regret it!**
>
> **Let me know** when you plan to go so I can help you more. **Take care!**
>
> **Best wishes,**
> Linh

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Thư thân mật]
**Mở thư:** *Dear + tên, I'm so happy to hear that... Let me tell you everything you need to know.*

**Thân thư:** Chia 3 phần: cách đi + hoạt động + lý do chọn Đà Nẵng

**Kết thư:** *Let me know when you plan to go. Take care! Best wishes, + tên*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **The best way is to + V** | Cách tốt nhất là... |
| **You should definitely + V** | Bạn nhất định nên... |
| **Don't forget to + V** | Đừng quên... |
| **I also recommend + Ving** | Tôi cũng gợi ý... |
| **You won't regret it** | Bạn sẽ không hối hận đâu |
| **Let me know + N** | Cho tôi biết... |

**Lưu ý:** Thư thân mật dùng **short forms** (I'm, don't, won't, you'll) và giọng văn **vui vẻ, nhiệt tình**. Đếm đủ **120+ từ**.
:::

## Writing Task 2: Essay — Advantages

> What are the advantages of having new experiences? Give your own opinion.

### Bài mẫu:

> Life is full of **opportunities** to try new things. **In my opinion**, having new experiences brings many **important advantages** that help us grow as individuals. **In this essay**, I will discuss three main benefits.
>
> **Firstly**, new experiences help us **gain valuable knowledge**. When we travel to a new country, we learn about different **languages**, **cultures**, and **ways of living**. When we try a new job, we learn new **skills** and gain practical experience. **For example**, when I visited Thailand last year, I learned many things about Thai food and culture that I could never learn from books. **Real-life experience** is always the best teacher.
>
> **Secondly**, new experiences can **enrich our lives** and make them more **meaningful**. When we try new hobbies like painting, cooking, or playing a sport, we **discover new passions** that make us happier. Life would be very **boring** if we did the same things every day. **Having new experiences** adds color and excitement to our daily routine.
>
> **Thirdly**, trying new things helps us **broaden our social circle**. When we travel, join a new class, or start a new activity, we have the chance to **meet new people** who share the same interests. These connections can become **lasting friendships** and even open up new **opportunities** in our career and personal life.
>
> **In conclusion**, having new experiences helps us gain knowledge, enrich our lives, and make new friends. I believe everyone should **step out of their comfort zone** and try something new whenever they have the chance.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Advantages]
**Mở bài:** *In my opinion, having new experiences brings many advantages. In this essay, I will discuss three main benefits.*

**Thân bài:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Thirdly, ...*

**Kết luận:** *In conclusion, ... I believe everyone should...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **gain valuable knowledge** | Thu được kiến thức quý giá |
| **enrich our lives** | Làm phong phú cuộc sống |
| **discover new passions** | Khám phá đam mê mới |
| **broaden our social circle** | Mở rộng mối quan hệ xã hội |
| **step out of one's comfort zone** | Bước ra khỏi vùng an toàn |
| **lasting friendships** | Tình bạn bền lâu |
| **For example, ...** | Ví dụ... |
| **In conclusion, ...** | Kết luận... |

**Lưu ý:** Bài luận cần **ít nhất 150 từ**. Mỗi đoạn nên có **1 lợi ích + giải thích + ví dụ**. Dùng **linking words** ở đầu mỗi câu để bài mạch lạc.
:::
