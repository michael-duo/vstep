## Speaking Part 1: Interview

### Topic — Flowers

**1. Do you like flowers?**

> Yes, I **really like** flowers. I think flowers are very **beautiful** and they make me feel **happy**. I often buy flowers to put in my room because they make my room look **brighter** and smell **nicer**.

**2. What is your favorite flower?**

> My favorite flower is the **lotus**. It is the **national flower** of Vietnam. I love the lotus because it is very **elegant** and has a **gentle smell**. **Moreover**, the lotus has a special meaning — it grows from mud but is still **clean and beautiful**, just like a person with good character.

**3. Do you think flowers are important?**

> Yes, I think flowers are **very important** in our lives. Flowers help people **express their feelings**. **For example**, we give **red roses** to show love, and **white flowers** to show respect. **Besides**, flowers are also used to **decorate** houses, offices, and events, making them more **beautiful**.

**4. On what occasions do people give or receive flowers?**

> People give and receive flowers on many **special occasions**. **For example**, on **Valentine's Day**, men often give roses to their girlfriends. On **Women's Day** (March 8th), women receive flowers from their families and friends. **In addition**, people also give flowers at **weddings**, **graduations**, and **funerals**.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **I really like + N** | Tôi thực sự thích... | *I really like flowers* |
| **express their feelings** | Thể hiện cảm xúc | *Flowers help people express their feelings* |
| **For example, ...** | Ví dụ... | *For example, we give red roses to show love* |
| **Besides, ...** | Ngoài ra... | *Besides, flowers are also used to decorate* |
| **In addition, ...** | Thêm vào đó... | *In addition, people also give flowers at weddings* |

## Speaking Part 2: Discussion

> You want to lose weight. Which would you choose: **playing sports**, **skipping breakfast**, or **going to the gym**?

### Bài mẫu:

> **I would choose playing sports** to lose weight. I think it is the **healthiest and most enjoyable** way.
>
> **The first reason is that** playing sports helps me **burn calories** effectively and **improve my overall health**. When I play sports like badminton or football, my whole body is moving, so I can lose weight **naturally**. It also makes my heart and muscles **stronger**.
>
> **The second reason is that** I can **play with my friends**, so it is much more **fun** than exercising alone. When we play together, we **motivate each other** and don't feel bored. This makes it **easier to continue** for a long time.
>
> **I would not choose skipping breakfast** because it is very **unhealthy**. Our body needs energy in the morning, and if we skip breakfast, we may feel **tired** and get **stomachaches**. **As for going to the gym**, it can be effective, but we have to pay a **membership fee** every month. Many people also **give up** after a few weeks because it is boring to exercise alone.
>
> **In short**, playing sports is the best way to lose weight because it is healthy, fun, and free.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 2]
**Bước 1 — Chọn:** *I would choose + lựa chọn*

**Bước 2 — Lý do 1:** *The first reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 3 — Lý do 2:** *The second reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 4 — Loại bỏ:** *I would not choose... because...* / *As for..., ...*

**Bước 5 — Kết:** *In short, ...*

| Từ vựng quan trọng | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **burn calories** | đốt cháy calo |
| **improve overall health** | cải thiện sức khoẻ toàn diện |
| **motivate each other** | động viên lẫn nhau |
| **membership fee** | phí thành viên |
| **give up** | bỏ cuộc |
| **unhealthy** | không tốt cho sức khoẻ |
:::

## Speaking Part 3: Topic Development (nói 2 phút)

> **Ways to overcome stress.**

### Bài mẫu:

> Today, I would like to talk about **ways to overcome stress**. In modern life, stress is very **common**, but there are several **effective ways** to deal with it.
>
> **Firstly**, one of the best ways to reduce stress is to **exercise regularly**. When we exercise, our body releases **happy hormones** that make us feel **relaxed and positive**. Simple activities like **jogging**, **swimming**, or even **walking** in the park for 30 minutes a day can help a lot.
>
> **Secondly**, **talking to someone** is also a great way to overcome stress. When we share our problems with **family members** or **close friends**, we feel **much better**. They can give us **advice** and **support**, and we know that we are **not alone**. Sometimes, just having someone to listen is enough.
>
> **Thirdly**, we should learn to **manage our time wisely**. Many people feel stressed because they have **too much work** and not enough time. By making a **schedule** and **breaking big tasks** into smaller ones, we can finish our work **step by step** without feeling **overwhelmed**.
>
> **In conclusion**, exercising regularly, talking to others, and managing time wisely are three simple but **effective ways** to overcome stress. I think everyone should try these methods to have a **happier life**.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 3]
**Mở bài:** *Today, I would like to talk about...*

**Thân bài:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Thirdly, ...*

**Kết:** *In conclusion, ...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **exercise regularly** | Tập thể dục đều đặn | *One of the best ways is to exercise regularly* |
| **release happy hormones** | Tiết ra hormone hạnh phúc | *Our body releases happy hormones* |
| **share problems with + sb** | Chia sẻ vấn đề với ai | *Share our problems with close friends* |
| **manage time wisely** | Quản lý thời gian khôn ngoan | *We should manage our time wisely* |
| **break big tasks into smaller ones** | Chia nhỏ công việc | *Breaking big tasks into smaller ones* |
| **feel overwhelmed** | Cảm thấy quá tải | *Without feeling overwhelmed* |
:::

## Writing Task 1: Informal Letter

> You are having your first summer camp. Write a letter to your friend Hoa to tell her about: **how you feel**, **who you stay with**, and **what activities** you do.

### Bài mẫu:

> Dear Hoa,
>
> **How are you?** I'm writing to tell you about my **first summer camp**. It's **amazing** here!
>
> **At first**, I felt a bit **nervous and scared** because I didn't know anyone. But now, I'm having **the best time** of my life! Everything here is so **exciting** and new. I wake up every morning feeling **happy and energetic**.
>
> I'm staying in a room with **three other girls**. Their names are Lan, Huong, and Thu. They are all very **friendly and funny**. We became **close friends** after just two days! We talk and laugh a lot before going to sleep every night.
>
> **About the activities**, we do so many **fun things** here. In the morning, we do **outdoor activities** like hiking, swimming in the lake, and **playing team games**. In the afternoon, we have **workshops** where we learn to paint and make crafts. My favorite part is the **campfire** at night — we sing songs, tell stories, and **roast marshmallows**. It's so much fun!
>
> **I wish you were here!** I'll show you all the photos when I get back. **Write to me soon!**
>
> **Love,**
> Linh

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Thư thân mật]
**Mở thư:** *Dear + tên, How are you? I'm writing to tell you about...*

**Thân thư:** Kể theo chủ đề: cảm xúc → bạn cùng phòng → hoạt động

**Kết thư:** *I wish you were here! Write to me soon! Love, + tên*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **At first, I felt... but now...** | Lúc đầu mình cảm thấy... nhưng giờ... |
| **I'm having the best time of my life** | Mình đang có khoảng thời gian tuyệt vời nhất |
| **became close friends** | Trở thành bạn thân |
| **My favorite part is + N** | Phần mình thích nhất là... |
| **I wish you were here!** | Ước gì bạn ở đây! |

**Lưu ý:** Thư thân mật dùng **short forms** (I'm, don't, it's) và giọng văn **vui vẻ, hào hứng**. Đếm đủ **120+ từ**.
:::

## Writing Task 2: Essay — Benefits

> Teenagers take part in unpaid work such as **volunteering** and **teaching poor kids**. Discuss the **benefits** to teenagers and society.

### Bài mẫu:

> **Nowadays**, many teenagers **take part in** unpaid work like volunteering and teaching poor children. I believe that this brings **many benefits** to both teenagers and society.
>
> **Firstly**, unpaid work helps teenagers **develop important skills**. When they volunteer, they learn **communication skills**, **teamwork**, and **leadership**. **For example**, when a teenager teaches poor kids, he or she has to prepare lessons and **speak in front of** a group. These skills will be very **useful** for their future careers. **Moreover**, doing volunteer work also makes teenagers' **CV** look better, which helps them **get into** good universities or find jobs more easily.
>
> **Secondly**, unpaid work brings **great benefits to society**. When teenagers **help disadvantaged people**, the community becomes **stronger and more united**. Poor children who receive **free education** can have a **better future**. Old people in nursing homes feel **less lonely** when young volunteers visit them. **In addition**, when teenagers do volunteer work instead of wasting time on games or social media, **social problems** like youth crime can be **reduced**.
>
> **In conclusion**, unpaid work is **valuable** for both teenagers and society. Teenagers can develop skills and become better people, while society benefits from their **kindness and contribution**. I think the government and schools should **encourage** more teenagers to participate in volunteer activities.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Benefits (2 đối tượng)]
**Mở bài:** *I believe that this brings many benefits to both... and...*

**Thân bài 1 (Benefits cho teenagers):** *Firstly, ... For example, ... Moreover, ...*

**Thân bài 2 (Benefits cho society):** *Secondly, ... In addition, ...*

**Kết luận:** *In conclusion, unpaid work is valuable for both... and...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **take part in + N** | Tham gia vào... |
| **develop important skills** | Phát triển kỹ năng quan trọng |
| **speak in front of + N** | Nói trước... |
| **help disadvantaged people** | Giúp đỡ người thiệt thòi |
| **reduce social problems** | Giảm các vấn đề xã hội |
| **kindness and contribution** | Lòng tốt và sự đóng góp |

**Lưu ý:** Bài luận cần **ít nhất 150 từ**. Khi đề bài yêu cầu benefits cho 2 đối tượng, mỗi đối tượng viết thành 1 đoạn thân bài riêng.
:::
