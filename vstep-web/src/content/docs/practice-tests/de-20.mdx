## Speaking Part 1: Interview

### Topic — Films

**1. Do you like watching films?**

> Yes, I really **enjoy watching films** in my free time. I find them very **entertaining** and relaxing. I usually watch films **on weekends** after a long week of studying. Films help me **forget about** my stress for a while.

**2. What kinds of movies do you like best?**

> I like **comedy** films the best because they **make me laugh** and feel happy. **Moreover**, I also enjoy **action** movies because they are exciting and full of surprises. My favourite comedy movie is "Mr. Bean" because it is very funny.

**3. How often do you go to the cinema?**

> I go to the cinema about **once a month**, usually **with my friends** on weekends. We like to watch **new movies** together and then go out for dinner afterwards. **However**, I also watch films **at home** on my laptop when I don't have time to go to the cinema.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **enjoy + Ving** | Thích làm gì | *I enjoy watching films* |
| **make me laugh** | Khiến tôi cười | *Comedy films make me laugh* |
| **Moreover, ...** | Hơn nữa... | *Moreover, I also enjoy action movies* |
| **However, ...** | Tuy nhiên... | *However, I also watch films at home* |
| **forget about + N** | Quên đi... | *Films help me forget about my stress* |
:::

## Speaking Part 2: Discussion

> You want to open a shop with your friend. Which type of shop do you prefer: **a clothes shop**, **a coffee shop**, or **an internet shop**?

### Bài mẫu:

> **I would choose to open a coffee shop.** I think it is the **best choice** for a business with my friend.
>
> **The first reason is that** there is a **high demand** for coffee in Vietnam. Young people love going to coffee shops to study, work, or **meet friends**. We would always have a lot of customers, so the business can be **profitable**.
>
> **The second reason is that** a coffee shop is **easy to manage**. The menu is simple — just coffee, tea, and some drinks. We do not need many staff members, and the ingredients are not expensive. **Therefore**, it is a good business for beginners like us.
>
> **I would not choose a clothes shop** because the fashion market is very **competitive**. There are too many clothes shops already, and we would have to follow fashion **trends** all the time. **As for an internet shop**, it is a **declining trend** because most people now have their own smartphones and wifi at home. Fewer people need to go to internet shops.
>
> **In short**, a coffee shop is the best option because there is high demand and it is easy to start and manage.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 2]
**Bước 1 — Chọn:** *I would choose to + V*

**Bước 2 — Lý do 1:** *The first reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 3 — Lý do 2:** *The second reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 4 — Loại bỏ:** *I would not choose... because...* / *As for..., it is...*

**Bước 5 — Kết:** *In short, ...*

| Từ vựng quan trọng | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **high demand** | Nhu cầu cao |
| **profitable** | Có lãi, sinh lời |
| **competitive** | Cạnh tranh |
| **declining trend** | Xu hướng giảm |
:::

## Speaking Part 3: Topic Development (nói 2 phút)

> **Benefits of learning English.**

### Bài mẫu:

> Today, I would like to talk about **the benefits of learning English**. I believe that English is very important in our modern life, and learning it brings many advantages.
>
> **Firstly**, English helps us get **better job opportunities**. Many **foreign companies** in Vietnam require their employees to speak English. People who have good English skills often receive **higher salaries** and have more chances to get promoted. **Therefore**, learning English is very important for our career.
>
> **Secondly**, English gives us **access to knowledge**. Most books, research papers, and **online courses** are written in English. If we know English, we can read and learn from the best sources in the world. We can also **study abroad** at famous universities in the US, the UK, or Australia.
>
> **Thirdly**, English is an **international language** that helps us when **travelling**. When we travel to another country, we can use English to **communicate** with local people, ask for directions, and book hotels. This makes our trips much easier and more enjoyable.
>
> **In conclusion**, learning English is very important for our career, our education, and our daily life. I think everyone should **start learning English** as early as possible to enjoy these benefits.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 3]
**Mở bài:** *Today, I would like to talk about... I believe that...*

**Thân bài:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Thirdly, ...*

**Kết:** *In conclusion, ...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **job opportunities** | Cơ hội việc làm | *English helps us get better job opportunities* |
| **access to knowledge** | Tiếp cận kiến thức | *English gives us access to knowledge* |
| **international language** | Ngôn ngữ quốc tế | *English is an international language* |
| **study abroad** | Du học | *We can study abroad at famous universities* |
| **Therefore, ...** | Vì vậy... | *Therefore, learning English is very important* |
| **as early as possible** | Càng sớm càng tốt | *Everyone should start learning English as early as possible* |
:::

## Writing Task 1: Informal Letter

> Your English friend is coming to your hometown for 2 weeks. Write a letter with advice about: accommodation, weather, and activities.

### Bài mẫu:

> Dear Emma,
>
> **I'm so excited** to hear that you're coming to my hometown! **Let me give you** some advice to help you **prepare for** your trip.
>
> **About accommodation**, I recommend staying at a **homestay** in the city centre. It is **cheaper** than a hotel, and you can **experience the local culture** by living with a Vietnamese family. The area is also close to restaurants, markets, and tourist spots, so it is very **convenient**.
>
> **About the weather**, it will be quite **hot and humid** during your visit. The temperature is usually around **30 to 35 degrees**. You should bring **light clothes**, sunscreen, and a hat. **Also**, bring an umbrella because it sometimes **rains in the afternoon**.
>
> **About activities**, there are many things to do here! You can visit **famous places** like the Old Quarter and some beautiful temples. You **must try** our **street food**, especially pho, banh mi, and bun cha — they are delicious and very cheap. I will **take you to** the best food places! We can also go to a **night market** in the evening.
>
> **I can't wait to see you!** If you have any questions, just let me know. **See you soon!**
>
> **Love,**
> Ha

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Thư thân mật]
**Mở thư:** *Dear + tên, I'm so excited to hear that... Let me give you some advice...*

**Thân thư:** Cho lời khuyên lần lượt: chỗ ở → thời tiết → hoạt động

**Kết thư:** *I can't wait to see you! See you soon! Love, + tên*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **I recommend + Ving** | Tôi khuyên nên... |
| **experience the local culture** | Trải nghiệm văn hoá địa phương |
| **You should + V** | Bạn nên... |
| **You must try + N** | Bạn phải thử... |
| **I can't wait to + V** | Tôi nóng lòng muốn... |
| **take sb to + place** | Đưa ai đến... |

**Lưu ý:** Thư thân mật dùng **short forms** (I'm, you're, can't) và giọng văn **vui vẻ, nhiệt tình**. Đếm đủ **120+ từ**.
:::

## Writing Task 2: Essay — Discuss both views

> Some people prefer watching movies at home while others prefer going to the cinema. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

### Bài mẫu:

> Watching movies is one of the most popular forms of **entertainment** today. **Some people prefer** watching movies at home, **while others enjoy** going to the cinema. **In this essay**, I will discuss both views and give my opinion.
>
> **On the one hand**, watching movies at home has several advantages. **Firstly**, it is very **convenient** because people can watch at any time they want. They can also **pause** the movie when they need to do something. **Secondly**, watching at home is much **cheaper** because they do not have to buy cinema tickets or expensive snacks. **Moreover**, people can relax in a **comfortable environment** — they can lie on the sofa, eat their own food, and wear comfortable clothes.
>
> **On the other hand**, going to the cinema also has its **benefits**. **First**, the cinema offers a **better viewing experience** with a big screen and **surround sound**. The picture quality is much better than a small laptop or TV. **Second**, going to the cinema is a great **social activity**. People can go with friends, family, or their partner and **create happy memories** together. **Furthermore**, cinemas show **new movies first**, so people can watch them before they are available online.
>
> **In my opinion**, both options are good for different situations. I usually watch movies at home for **convenience**, but I go to the cinema when there is a **special movie** or when I want to **spend time with** my friends. I think people should choose the option that **suits their needs** and situation.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Discuss Both Views]
**Mở bài:** *Some people prefer... while others enjoy... In this essay, I will discuss both views and give my opinion.*

**Thân bài 1:** *On the one hand, ... Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Moreover, ...*

**Thân bài 2:** *On the other hand, ... First, ... Second, ... Furthermore, ...*

**Kết luận:** *In my opinion, ...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **On the one hand, ... On the other hand, ...** | Một mặt... Mặt khác... |
| **better viewing experience** | Trải nghiệm xem tốt hơn |
| **surround sound** | Âm thanh vòm |
| **social activity** | Hoạt động xã hội |
| **create happy memories** | Tạo kỷ niệm đẹp |
| **suit one's needs** | Phù hợp với nhu cầu của ai |

**Lưu ý:** Bài luận cần **ít nhất 150 từ**. Dùng **linking words** ở đầu mỗi câu để bài mạch lạc. **Không dùng "I think" quá nhiều** — thay bằng "I believe", "In my opinion".
:::
