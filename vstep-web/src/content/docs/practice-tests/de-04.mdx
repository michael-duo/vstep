## Speaking Part 1: Interview

### Topic A — Celebrities

**1. Who is your favorite celebrity?**

> My favorite celebrity is **Son Tung MTP**. He is a very **talented singer** in Vietnam. I admire him because his songs are **catchy** and **meaningful**. He also works very hard and **never gives up** on his dreams.

**2. How do celebrities influence fans?**

> Celebrities can **influence their fans** in many ways. **Firstly**, they can be **good role models**. When fans see their favorite celebrities working hard, they are **inspired to** work hard too. **However**, some celebrities have a **bad lifestyle**, which can negatively affect young people.

**3. Do you like any foreign celebrities?**

> Yes, I like **Taylor Swift** because her songs are very **beautiful and meaningful**. I also like **BTS** because they are talented and their dance performances are **amazing**. I think foreign celebrities help me learn more about **other cultures**.

**4. Would you like to be a celebrity?**

> No, I **would not like** to be a celebrity. I think celebrities have **too much pressure** from the media and the public. They also **lose their private life** because people follow them everywhere. I **prefer to live** a simple and quiet life.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **admire sb because...** | Ngưỡng mộ ai vì... | *I admire him because his songs are catchy* |
| **be inspired to + V** | Được truyền cảm hứng để... | *They are inspired to work hard* |
| **role model** | Hình mẫu, người để noi theo | *They can be good role models* |
| **lose one's private life** | Mất đi cuộc sống riêng tư | *They lose their private life* |
| **prefer to + V** | Thích... hơn | *I prefer to live a simple life* |
:::

### Topic B — Advertisement

**1. Are there many advertisements in your country?**

> Yes, there are **a lot of advertisements** in Vietnam. We can see ads **everywhere** — on TV, on social media, on billboards along the streets, and even on buses. I think the number of ads has **increased a lot** in recent years.

**2. Why are there so many advertisements now?**

> I think there are so many ads because of **competition** between companies. Every company wants to **attract more customers** and **increase their sales**. Advertising is the **best way** to let people know about new products. **Moreover**, social media makes it very easy and cheap to advertise.

**3. Where do we see advertisements?**

> We can see ads in **many places**. The most common places are **television**, **social media** like Facebook and YouTube, and **billboards** on the streets. We also see ads in **newspapers**, on **buses**, and in **shopping malls**. Even our phones show ads when we use **free apps**.

**4. How do you feel about advertisements?**

> I have **mixed feelings** about advertisements. Sometimes they are **useful** because they introduce me to **new products** that I need. **However**, most ads are very **annoying**, especially when they **interrupt** my videos on YouTube. I think there should be **fewer ads** on social media.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **attract more customers** | Thu hút thêm khách hàng | *Companies want to attract more customers* |
| **increase sales** | Tăng doanh số | *Companies want to increase their sales* |
| **have mixed feelings about + N** | Có cảm xúc lẫn lộn về... | *I have mixed feelings about advertisements* |
| **Moreover, ...** | Hơn nữa... | *Moreover, social media makes it easy to advertise* |
| **interrupt + N** | Gián đoạn, cắt ngang | *Ads interrupt my videos* |
:::

## Speaking Part 2: Discussion

> Your mother's birthday is next February. You want to give her a special gift. Which would you choose: **flowers**, **a hand-made card**, or **a notebook**?

### Bài mẫu:

> **I would choose to give my mother a hand-made card.** I think it is the **most meaningful gift** among the three options.
>
> **The first reason is that** a hand-made card shows my **sincerity** and **love**. I will make it myself, so my mother can feel how much I **care about her**. I will decorate it with beautiful drawings and use her favorite colors.
>
> **The second reason is that** I can write a **heartfelt message** inside the card. I will tell her how much I love her and **thank her** for everything she has done for me. My mother can **keep this card** as a **lasting memory**, and she can read it again whenever she misses me.
>
> **I would not choose flowers** because they will **wilt after a few days**. They are beautiful, but they don't last long. My mother would feel sad when the flowers die. **As for a notebook**, it is too **ordinary**. You can buy a notebook anywhere, and it does not carry any **special meaning**.
>
> **In short**, a hand-made card is the best gift because it shows my love and my mother can keep it forever.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 2]
**Bước 1 — Chọn:** *I would choose + lựa chọn*

**Bước 2 — Lý do 1:** *The first reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 3 — Lý do 2:** *The second reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 4 — Loại bỏ:** *I would not choose... because...* / *As for..., it is...*

**Bước 5 — Kết:** *In short, ...*

| Từ vựng quan trọng | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **sincerity** | Sự chân thành |
| **heartfelt message** | Lời nhắn chân thành |
| **lasting memory** | Kỷ niệm lâu dài |
| **wilt** | Héo (nói về hoa) |
| **ordinary** | Bình thường, tầm thường |
:::

## Speaking Part 3: Topic Development (nói 2 phút)

> **Online shopping has many disadvantages:** late delivery, poor quality, and lack of shopping experience.

### Bài mẫu:

> Today, I would like to talk about **the disadvantages of online shopping**. Although online shopping is very popular nowadays, I think it has several problems.
>
> **Firstly**, one of the biggest problems is **late delivery**. When people order products online, they often have to **wait for many days** to receive them. During holidays like Tet or Christmas, there are **too many orders**, so delivery is even slower. Sometimes, products are **delivered to the wrong address** or even get **lost** during shipping. This makes customers very **frustrated**.
>
> **Secondly**, the **quality of products** is often **poor** and different from what customers expect. The pictures on the website look beautiful, but the **real products** may look completely different. Since customers **cannot touch or try** the products before buying, they easily **waste money** on bad items. **Moreover**, returning products is usually very **complicated and time-consuming**.
>
> **Thirdly**, online shopping takes away the **real shopping experience**. When people shop online, they cannot **walk around** the store, try on clothes, or feel the material. They also miss the chance to **spend time with friends** or family while shopping. Shopping is not just about buying things — it is also a **social activity** that people enjoy.
>
> **In conclusion**, although online shopping is convenient, it has many disadvantages such as late delivery, poor quality, and a lack of real shopping experience. People should be **more careful** when shopping online.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 3]
**Mở bài:** *Today, I would like to talk about... Although... is popular, I think it has several problems.*

**Thân bài:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Thirdly, ...*

**Kết:** *In conclusion, although..., it has many disadvantages...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **One of the biggest problems is + N** | Một trong những vấn đề lớn nhất là... | *One of the biggest problems is late delivery* |
| **Although + S + V, ...** | Mặc dù... | *Although online shopping is convenient, it has many disadvantages* |
| **take away + N** | Lấy đi, làm mất... | *Online shopping takes away the real shopping experience* |
| **waste money on + N** | Phí tiền vào... | *They waste money on bad items* |
| **time-consuming** | Tốn thời gian | *Returning products is time-consuming* |
| **cannot + V** | Không thể... | *Customers cannot touch or try the products* |
:::

## Writing Task 1: Informal Letter

> Your American friend is coming to visit you. Write a letter to express your happiness, tell about the weather to prepare, and share your plans (what to do, where to go).

### Bài mẫu:

> Dear John,
>
> **I'm so excited** to hear that you're coming to Vietnam! I **can't wait to see you**. It has been a long time since we last met, so I have **already planned** many fun things for us to do together.
>
> **First**, let me tell you about the **weather** here. You are coming in **July**, so it will be very **hot and humid** — about 35 to 38 degrees. You should bring **light clothes**, **sunscreen**, and a **hat** to protect yourself from the sun. It also rains sometimes in the afternoon, so **don't forget** to bring an umbrella.
>
> **Now, let me share my plans** for your visit. I will take you to **Hoan Kiem Lake** and the **Old Quarter** in Hanoi. These are the most **famous and beautiful places** in the city. We will also try some **delicious Vietnamese food** like **pho**, **bun cha**, and **egg coffee** — I'm sure you will **love them**! If we have time, I want to take you to **Ha Long Bay** for a weekend trip. It is one of the **most beautiful places** in the world.
>
> I'm sure you will have a **wonderful time** in Vietnam. **Let me know** your exact arrival date so I can pick you up at the airport!
>
> **Best wishes,**
> Linh

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Thư thân mật]
**Mở thư:** *Dear + tên, I'm so excited to hear that... / I can't wait to see you.*

**Thân thư:** Chia 2 phần: thời tiết + kế hoạch

**Kết thư:** *I'm sure you will have a wonderful time. Let me know... Best wishes, + tên*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **I'm so excited to + V** | Tôi rất vui khi... |
| **can't wait to + V** | Nóng lòng muốn... |
| **Let me tell you about + N** | Để tôi kể cho bạn về... |
| **Don't forget to + V** | Đừng quên... |
| **I'm sure you will + V** | Tôi chắc bạn sẽ... |
| **Let me know + N** | Cho tôi biết... |

**Lưu ý:** Thư thân mật dùng **short forms** (I'm, can't, don't, you're) và giọng văn **vui vẻ, nhiệt tình**. Đếm đủ **120+ từ**.
:::

## Writing Task 2: Essay — Discuss both views

> Some people think watching TV is bad for children because it harms their health and exposes them to negative images. Others think TV has benefits such as relaxation and boosting imagination. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

### Bài mẫu:

> **Nowadays**, television is one of the most popular forms of **entertainment** for children. **Some people believe** that watching TV is bad for children, **while others think** it has many benefits. **In this essay**, I will discuss both views and give my opinion.
>
> **On the one hand**, watching too much TV can be **harmful to children**. **Firstly**, sitting in front of the TV for many hours can **damage their eyesight** and lead to **obesity** because they do not move or exercise. **Secondly**, some TV programs contain **violence** and **negative images** that can badly **affect children's behavior**. Children may think that fighting or using bad language is normal. **Moreover**, children who watch too much TV spend **less time studying** and playing outside.
>
> **On the other hand**, TV can also bring **many benefits** to children. **Firstly**, there are many **educational programs** on TV that help children **learn new things** about science, history, and nature. **Secondly**, watching cartoons can **boost children's imagination** and creativity. Children can see new worlds and interesting characters that make them think more creatively. **In addition**, watching TV is a good way for children to **relax** after a long day of studying at school.
>
> **In my opinion**, TV is **not completely bad** for children. **However**, parents should **control the time** and **choose the content** that their children watch. **A balance** between studying, playing, and watching TV is the **best solution** for children's development.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Discuss Both Views]
**Mở bài:** *Some people believe that... while others think that... In this essay, I will discuss both views and give my opinion.*

**Thân bài 1:** *On the one hand, ... Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Moreover, ...*

**Thân bài 2:** *On the other hand, ... Firstly, ... Secondly, ... In addition, ...*

**Kết luận:** *In my opinion, ... However, ... A balance between... is the best solution.*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **On the one hand, ... On the other hand, ...** | Một mặt... Mặt khác... |
| **be harmful to + N** | Có hại cho... |
| **damage one's eyesight** | Gây hại cho mắt |
| **affect children's behavior** | Ảnh hưởng đến hành vi trẻ em |
| **boost imagination** | Kích thích trí tưởng tượng |
| **educational programs** | Chương trình giáo dục |
| **A balance between... is the best solution** | Sự cân bằng giữa... là giải pháp tốt nhất |
| **In addition, ...** | Ngoài ra... |

**Lưu ý:** Bài luận cần **ít nhất 150 từ**. Dùng **linking words** ở đầu mỗi câu. **Không dùng "I think" quá nhiều** — thay bằng "I believe", "In my opinion".
:::
