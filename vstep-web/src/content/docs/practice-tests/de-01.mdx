## Speaking Part 1: Interview

### Topic A — Favourite Subject

**1. What is your favorite subject at school?**

> My favorite subject is **English**. I think it is very **useful** because I can use it to **communicate** with people from other countries. I also enjoy learning new words every day.

**2. What do you like about this subject?**

> I like English because the lessons are **interesting**. My teacher often lets us **practice speaking** in class, so I don't feel bored. **Moreover**, I can watch English movies and understand them better.

**3. If you could choose to study another subject, what would you choose?**

> **If I could choose**, I would study **Art** because I enjoy drawing in my free time. I think Art helps me **relax** after a long day of studying.

**4. Will you pursue your favorite subject as your major at university?**

> Yes, I **plan to** major in English at university because I want to **become** an English teacher in the future. I think this is a **good career** in Vietnam.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **I think + S + V** | Tôi nghĩ... | *I think it is very useful* |
| **If I could + V, I would + V** | Nếu tôi có thể... tôi sẽ... (giả định) | *If I could choose, I would study Art* |
| **Moreover, ...** | Hơn nữa... (thêm ý) | *Moreover, I can watch English movies* |
| **plan to + V** | Dự định làm gì | *I plan to major in English* |
| **want to become + N** | Muốn trở thành... | *I want to become an English teacher* |
:::

### Topic B — Public Holidays

**1. How many public holidays are there in your country?**

> In Vietnam, there are about **10 public holidays** in a year. The most important one is **Tet**, which is the Vietnamese New Year. We also have **National Day** on September 2nd.

**2. How do you spend your holidays?**

> During holidays, I usually **spend time with my family**. We often **go back** to our hometown to visit our grandparents. Sometimes, I **go out** with my friends to eat and watch movies.

**3. Do you think that people need more holidays?**

> Yes, I think people **need more holidays** because they work very hard every day. **Having more holidays** helps them **rest** and **recharge**. **As a result**, they can work better after the holiday.

**4. What is your favorite public holiday?**

> My favorite holiday is **Tet** because my whole family **gets together**. We eat **traditional food** like banh chung, and children receive **lucky money**. **It makes me feel** very happy and warm.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **spend time with + người** | Dành thời gian với... | *spend time with my family* |
| **The most important one is...** | Quan trọng nhất là... | *The most important one is Tet* |
| **Having + N + helps sb + V** | Việc có... giúp ai đó... | *Having more holidays helps them rest* |
| **As a result, ...** | Kết quả là... | *As a result, they can work better* |
| **It makes me feel + adj** | Nó khiến tôi cảm thấy... | *It makes me feel very happy* |
:::

## Speaking Part 2: Discussion

> Your English friend is going to sit in an important exam. He asks you for an ideal place for revision. Which would you choose: **at home**, **in the school library**, or **in the coffee shop**?

### Bài mẫu:

> **I would choose the school library.** I think it is the **best place** for my friend to study for his exam.
>
> **The first reason is that** the library is very **quiet**. There are no people talking loudly, so my friend can **concentrate on** his study. This is very important when you prepare for a big exam.
>
> **The second reason is that** there are many **books and materials** in the library. If my friend needs to **look up** some information, he can find it **right away**. He does not need to go anywhere else.
>
> **I would not choose studying at home** because there are **too many distractions**. For example, he might watch TV or play with his phone instead of studying. **As for the coffee shop**, it is usually **noisy** and he has to **spend money** on drinks. That is not a good choice.
>
> **In short**, the school library is the best option because it is quiet and has everything my friend needs.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 2]
**Bước 1 — Chọn:** *I would choose + lựa chọn*

**Bước 2 — Lý do 1:** *The first reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 3 — Lý do 2:** *The second reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 4 — Loại bỏ:** *I would not choose... because...* / *As for..., it is...*

**Bước 5 — Kết:** *In short, ...*

| Từ vựng quan trọng | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **concentrate on** + N/Ving | tập trung vào |
| **distractions** | những thứ gây mất tập trung |
| **look up** information | tra cứu thông tin |
| **right away** | ngay lập tức |
:::

## Speaking Part 3: Topic Development (nói 2 phút)

> **Smoking should be banned in public places:** environmental pollution, health risks, and a bad image for children.

### Bài mẫu:

> Today, I would like to talk about **why smoking should be banned in public places**. I strongly agree with this idea, and I have three reasons.
>
> **Firstly**, smoking causes **environmental pollution**. When people smoke in public places like parks or bus stations, the smoke **pollutes the air** around them. Also, many smokers throw their **cigarette butts** on the ground, which **makes the environment dirty**. This is a big problem in many cities in Vietnam.
>
> **Secondly**, smoking is very **harmful to people's health**. The people nearby have to breathe in **secondhand smoke**, which is even **more dangerous** than smoking directly. **According to** many studies, secondhand smoke can **lead to** lung cancer and heart disease. So, people who do not smoke are also **at risk**.
>
> **Thirdly**, smoking in public places gives children **a bad image**. When children see adults smoking on the street, they may think it is **normal** and **try to imitate** this behavior when they grow up. **As parents**, we should **set a good example** for the young generation.
>
> **In conclusion**, I believe the government should **ban smoking** in public places **in order to** protect the environment, keep people healthy, and create a good image for children.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 3]
**Mở bài:** *Today, I would like to talk about... I strongly agree / disagree with this idea.*

**Thân bài:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Thirdly, ...*

**Kết:** *In conclusion, I believe...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **lead to + N** | Dẫn đến... | *Secondhand smoke can lead to lung cancer* |
| **be harmful to + N** | Có hại cho... | *Smoking is harmful to people's health* |
| **According to + N** | Theo... | *According to many studies* |
| **in order to + V** | Để mà... (mục đích) | *Ban smoking in order to protect the environment* |
| **set a good example for** | Làm gương tốt cho | *We should set a good example for children* |
| **try to + V** | Cố gắng làm gì | *They may try to imitate this behavior* |
:::

## Writing Task 1: Informal Letter

> You've received a letter from your English pen pal asking about your 20-year-old birthday. Write a letter back to tell her about this event. What did you do? How did you feel?

### Bài mẫu:

> Dear Sarah,
>
> **Thank you for your letter!** I am so happy to hear from you. You asked me about my 20th birthday, so **let me tell you** about it.
>
> My birthday was **on March 15th** last month. I **held a small party** at my house and **invited** about 15 of my closest friends and my family. My mother cooked a lot of **delicious food**, and my father bought me a beautiful **birthday cake**.
>
> **During the party**, we ate, sang karaoke, and **took many photos** together. My friends **gave me** lovely gifts such as flowers, a teddy bear, and some books. **The most special moment** was when everyone sang "Happy Birthday" to me and I **blew out** the candles. I also received a new phone from my parents, which was a **big surprise**!
>
> I **felt so happy and grateful** on that day. **It was one of the most memorable days** in my life because I realized how much my family and friends **care about** me.
>
> I **hope you can come** to Vietnam and celebrate my next birthday with me! **Write back soon** and tell me about your birthday too.
>
> **Love,**
> Linh

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Thư thân mật]
**Mở thư:** *Dear + tên, Thank you for your letter! / I am happy to hear from you.*

**Thân thư:** Kể theo thời gian: chuẩn bị → diễn ra → cảm xúc

**Kết thư:** *I hope you can... / Write back soon. Love, + tên*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **Let me tell you about + N** | Để tôi kể cho bạn về... |
| **held a party** | Tổ chức tiệc |
| **The most special moment was when...** | Khoảnh khắc đặc biệt nhất là khi... |
| **It was one of the most + adj + N in my life** | Đó là một trong những... nhất trong đời tôi |
| **felt so + adj** | Cảm thấy rất... |
| **care about + sb** | Quan tâm đến ai |

**Lưu ý:** Thư thân mật được dùng **short forms** (I'm, It's, don't) và giọng văn **vui vẻ, gần gũi**. Đếm đủ **120+ từ**.
:::

## Writing Task 2: Essay — Discuss both views

> Nowadays, salaries of athletes are 10 times higher than those of teachers. Some think athletes deserve high salaries because of their hard training and injuries. Others think professions like teachers, doctors, and police also deserve higher pay. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

### Bài mẫu:

> **Nowadays**, it is common for athletes such as footballers to earn much more money than teachers or doctors. **Some people believe** that athletes deserve their high salaries, **while others think** that other professions should also be paid better. **In this essay**, I will discuss both views and give my opinion.
>
> **On the one hand**, there are good reasons why athletes earn a lot of money. **Firstly**, they have to **train very hard** from a very young age. They practice every day for many hours, and this takes a lot of **effort and dedication**. **Secondly**, athletes often **suffer from injuries** that can **affect their health** for the rest of their lives. **Moreover**, their careers are usually **very short** — most athletes retire before they are 35 years old. **Therefore**, they need high salaries **to prepare for** their future after retirement.
>
> **On the other hand**, many people believe that professions like teaching and medicine are **equally important** for society. Teachers **play a vital role in shaping** the future generation. Without good teachers, there would be no good doctors, engineers, or even athletes. **Similarly**, doctors **save people's lives** every day, and they also work long hours under **great pressure**. **However**, their salaries are much lower than those of athletes, which is **unfair**.
>
> **In my opinion**, I believe that **all professions have their own value**. Athletes work hard, but teachers and doctors **contribute greatly** to society too. **The government should** find a way to increase salaries for important professions **so that** everyone is paid more fairly.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Discuss Both Views]
**Mở bài:** *Some people believe that... while others think that... In this essay, I will discuss both views and give my opinion.*

**Thân bài 1:** *On the one hand, ... Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Moreover, ... Therefore, ...*

**Thân bài 2:** *On the other hand, ... Similarly, ... However, ...*

**Kết luận:** *In my opinion, I believe that...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **On the one hand, ... On the other hand, ...** | Một mặt... Mặt khác... |
| **play a vital role in + Ving** | Đóng vai trò quan trọng trong... |
| **suffer from + N** | Chịu đựng / mắc phải... |
| **Similarly, ...** | Tương tự... |
| **Therefore, ...** | Vì vậy... |
| **so that + S + V** | Để mà... |
| **contribute greatly to + N** | Đóng góp lớn cho... |
| **affect + N** | Ảnh hưởng đến... |

**Lưu ý:** Bài luận cần **ít nhất 150 từ**. Dùng **linking words** ở đầu mỗi câu để bài mạch lạc. **Không dùng "I think" quá nhiều** — thay bằng "I believe", "In my opinion".
:::
