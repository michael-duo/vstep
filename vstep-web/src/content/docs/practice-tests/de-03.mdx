## Speaking Part 1: Interview

### Topic A — Handicraft

**1. Do you like to collect things made by hand?**

> Yes, I **like to collect** handmade things. I have some **handmade bags** and **pottery** that I bought when I travelled to Bat Trang village. I think handmade products are very **unique** and have **cultural value**.

**2. Did you ever take handcraft lessons at school?**

> Yes, I took **handcraft lessons** in **primary school**. We learned how to make **paper flowers**, greeting cards, and small toys from recycled materials. I **enjoyed those lessons** very much because they were fun and **creative**.

**3. Are handcrafts popular in your country?**

> Yes, handcrafts are very **popular in Vietnam**. We have many **traditional craft villages** such as Bat Trang for pottery and Dong Ho for paintings. Many tourists like to visit these villages and buy **souvenirs**. **Moreover**, handcrafts help local people **earn a living**.

**4. Do you think children should learn more about handcrafts?**

> Yes, I think children **should learn more** about handcrafts. **Firstly**, it helps them become more **creative** and **patient**. **Secondly**, it teaches them to **appreciate** traditional culture. **In addition**, making things by hand is a good way for children to **relax** after studying.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **like to + V** | Thích làm gì | *I like to collect handmade things* |
| **have cultural value** | Có giá trị văn hoá | *Handmade products have cultural value* |
| **Moreover, ...** | Hơn nữa... | *Moreover, handcrafts help local people earn a living* |
| **earn a living** | Kiếm sống | *Handcrafts help local people earn a living* |
| **In addition, ...** | Ngoài ra... | *In addition, making things by hand is a good way to relax* |
:::

### Topic B — Food

**1. What's your favorite food?**

> My favorite food is **pho**. It is a **traditional Vietnamese soup** with rice noodles, beef or chicken, and fresh herbs. I love it because it has a **delicious taste** and it makes me feel **warm**, especially on cold days.

**2. Are there any foods you dislike?**

> Yes, I don't like **bitter melon** because it tastes **too bitter** for me. I also don't enjoy **durian** because the smell is very **strong**. **However**, I know these foods are good for health, so sometimes I try to eat a little.

**3. What are some traditional foods in your country?**

> Vietnam has many **traditional foods**. The most famous ones are **pho**, **banh mi**, and **spring rolls**. During Tet, we eat **banh chung** and **mut** (dried fruits). These foods are very **special** because they are part of our **culture and traditions**.

**4. Do you have a healthy diet?**

> I **try to have** a healthy diet. I eat **vegetables and fruits** every day, and I **try to avoid** eating too much **fast food**. **However**, sometimes I still eat fried chicken or pizza because they are delicious. I think **balance** is the most important thing.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **My favorite + N + is...** | ... yêu thích của tôi là... | *My favorite food is pho* |
| **too + adj + for sb** | Quá... đối với ai | *It tastes too bitter for me* |
| **try to + V** | Cố gắng làm gì | *I try to have a healthy diet* |
| **try to avoid + Ving** | Cố gắng tránh... | *I try to avoid eating fast food* |
| **part of our culture** | Một phần văn hoá của chúng tôi | *They are part of our culture* |
:::

## Speaking Part 2: Discussion

> Your English teacher is going to retire. Your class wants to hold a farewell party. Which place: **at school**, **at the teacher's house**, or **in a restaurant**?

### Bài mẫu:

> **I would choose to hold the farewell party in a restaurant.** I think this is the **best place** to show our respect and love for our teacher.
>
> **The first reason is that** we **don't need to prepare food** ourselves. The restaurant will **take care of everything** — the food, the drinks, and the decoration. This way, we can **focus on** enjoying the party and spending time with our teacher.
>
> **The second reason is that** a restaurant has a **nice and elegant atmosphere**. It is more special than a normal classroom. Our teacher will feel **happy and appreciated** when we take her to a beautiful restaurant. We can also **take nice photos** together as a memory.
>
> **I would not choose having the party at school** because it feels **too formal**, like a regular school day. There is no special feeling at all. **As for the teacher's house**, it would be **inconvenient** for her because she would have to cook and clean up. That is **not polite** because it is supposed to be a party for her.
>
> **In short**, a restaurant is the best choice because it is convenient, has a nice atmosphere, and shows our gratitude to our beloved teacher.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 2]
**Bước 1 — Chọn:** *I would choose + lựa chọn*

**Bước 2 — Lý do 1:** *The first reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 3 — Lý do 2:** *The second reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 4 — Loại bỏ:** *I would not choose... because...* / *As for..., it would be...*

**Bước 5 — Kết:** *In short, ...*

| Từ vựng quan trọng | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **take care of everything** | Lo liệu mọi thứ |
| **elegant atmosphere** | Không khí trang nhã |
| **feel appreciated** | Cảm thấy được trân trọng |
| **inconvenient** | Bất tiện |
| **gratitude** | Lòng biết ơn |
:::

## Speaking Part 3: Topic Development (nói 2 phút)

> **People should do yoga:** relax, good for health, build your body.

### Bài mẫu:

> Today, I would like to talk about **why people should do yoga**. I strongly agree with this idea, and I have three reasons.
>
> **Firstly**, yoga helps people **relax** and **reduce stress**. After a long working day, many people feel very **tired and anxious**. When they do yoga, they practice **deep breathing** and focus on their body. This helps their mind **become calm and peaceful**. Many studies show that yoga can also help people **sleep better** at night.
>
> **Secondly**, yoga is very **good for health**. It can **improve blood circulation** and reduce problems like **back pain** and headaches. People who do yoga regularly are **less likely to get sick**. **Moreover**, yoga can help prevent serious diseases such as **heart disease** and **high blood pressure**. So, it is a simple way to stay healthy.
>
> **Thirdly**, yoga helps **build a strong and flexible body**. Unlike other heavy exercises, yoga movements are **gentle** and suitable for **all ages** — from young children to old people. You don't need to go to a gym or buy expensive equipment. You can practice yoga **at home** with just a mat.
>
> **In conclusion**, I believe everyone should try yoga because it helps them relax, stay healthy, and keep their body strong and flexible.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 3]
**Mở bài:** *Today, I would like to talk about... I strongly agree with this idea.*

**Thân bài:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Thirdly, ...*

**Kết:** *In conclusion, I believe...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **reduce stress** | Giảm căng thẳng | *Yoga helps people reduce stress* |
| **improve blood circulation** | Cải thiện tuần hoàn máu | *Yoga can improve blood circulation* |
| **be less likely to + V** | Ít có khả năng... | *People are less likely to get sick* |
| **suitable for + N** | Phù hợp với... | *Yoga is suitable for all ages* |
| **It is beneficial for sb to V** | Việc... có lợi cho ai đó | *It is beneficial for everyone to practice yoga* |
| **Not only... but also...** | Không chỉ... mà còn... | *Yoga is not only relaxing but also good for health* |
:::

## Writing Task 1: Formal Letter

> You are going on a honeymoon with your wife in Hawaii. You have booked a room in a 5-star hotel. Write a letter to the hotel manager to ask about your reservation: time, room, and interesting activities/places.

### Bài mẫu:

> Dear Sir/Madam,
>
> **I am writing to inquire about** my reservation at your hotel. My name is Nguyen Van A, and I have booked a room for my **honeymoon** with my wife.
>
> **Firstly**, I would like to **confirm the dates** of our stay. We plan to **check in on July 10th** and **check out on July 17th**. Could you please tell me the **earliest check-in time**? We will arrive in the morning, so we hope to get our room as soon as possible.
>
> **Secondly**, I would like to ask about the **room**. We booked a double room, and **I would prefer** a room with a **sea view** if possible. Could you please let me know if the room includes **breakfast**? We would also like to know if there is a **swimming pool** and **spa** at the hotel.
>
> **Finally**, could you please **provide me with information** about **interesting activities and places** to visit near the hotel? We are interested in **water sports** such as snorkeling and surfing. We would also like to visit some **famous beaches** and **local restaurants**.
>
> **I would appreciate it** if you could reply at your earliest convenience. **Thank you** for your time and assistance.
>
> **Yours faithfully,**
> Nguyen Van A

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Thư trang trọng]
**Mở thư:** *Dear Sir/Madam, I am writing to inquire about...*

**Thân thư:** Hỏi 3 nội dung: thời gian + phòng + hoạt động

**Kết thư:** *I would appreciate it if you could reply. Yours faithfully, + tên*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **I am writing to inquire about + N** | Tôi viết thư để hỏi về... |
| **I would like to confirm + N** | Tôi muốn xác nhận... |
| **Could you please + V?** | Bạn có thể vui lòng...? (hỏi lịch sự) |
| **I would prefer + N** | Tôi muốn... hơn |
| **provide me with information about + N** | Cung cấp thông tin về... |
| **I would appreciate it if you could + V** | Tôi sẽ rất biết ơn nếu bạn có thể... |

**Lưu ý:** Thư trang trọng **KHÔNG dùng** short forms (không viết I'm, don't). Giọng văn **lịch sự, trang trọng**. Kết thư bằng **Yours faithfully** (khi không biết tên người nhận). Đếm đủ **120+ từ**.
:::

## Writing Task 2: Essay — Causes & Effects

> The rate of obesity is rising in many parts of the world. Why is this trend happening? What are its impacts?

### Bài mẫu:

> **Nowadays**, obesity is becoming a **serious problem** in many countries around the world. **In this essay**, I will discuss the main **causes** and **impacts** of this trend.
>
> **There are two main causes** of obesity. **Firstly**, people's **eating habits** have changed a lot. Many people eat too much **fast food** such as hamburgers, fried chicken, and pizza because they are cheap and convenient. They also drink a lot of **sugary drinks** like soda and bubble tea. These foods contain a lot of fat and sugar, which makes people **gain weight** quickly. **Secondly**, many people have a **sedentary lifestyle**. They spend long hours **sitting at their desks** at work or at school. They don't have enough time to **exercise**. When they go home, they just watch TV or play games instead of doing **physical activities**.
>
> **Obesity has many serious impacts.** **Firstly**, it causes many **health problems** such as **heart disease**, **diabetes**, and **high blood pressure**. Obese people are also more likely to suffer from **depression** and low self-confidence. **Secondly**, obesity puts **pressure on the healthcare system**. The government has to spend more money on **treating obesity-related diseases**. **As a result**, healthcare costs increase and affect the whole society.
>
> **In conclusion**, obesity is caused mainly by **unhealthy eating habits** and **lack of exercise**. It has serious impacts on both **individual health** and **society**. We should **raise awareness** about healthy lifestyles to solve this problem.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Causes & Effects]
**Mở bài:** *Obesity is becoming a serious problem. In this essay, I will discuss the causes and impacts.*

**Thân bài 1 — Nguyên nhân:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ...*

**Thân bài 2 — Tác động:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ...*

**Kết luận:** *In conclusion, ... is caused mainly by... It has serious impacts on...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **One of the main causes is + N** | Một trong những nguyên nhân chính là... |
| **sedentary lifestyle** | Lối sống ít vận động |
| **gain weight** | Tăng cân |
| **lead to + N** | Dẫn đến... |
| **put pressure on + N** | Gây áp lực lên... |
| **As a result, ...** | Kết quả là... |
| **raise awareness about + N** | Nâng cao nhận thức về... |
| **suffer from + N** | Chịu đựng / mắc phải... |

**Lưu ý:** Bài luận cần **ít nhất 150 từ**. Chia rõ phần **causes** và phần **effects/impacts**. Dùng **linking words** ở đầu mỗi câu để bài mạch lạc.
:::
