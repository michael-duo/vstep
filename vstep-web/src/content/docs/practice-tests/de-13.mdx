## Speaking Part 1: Interview

### Topic — Animals / Pets

**1. Do you keep a pet?**

> Yes, I **keep a dog** at home. His name is **Milo** and he is a small brown dog. I got him **two years ago**, and he has become a **part of my family**. I love playing with him every evening after school.

**2. What is your favorite animal?**

> My favorite animal is the **dog** because dogs are very **loyal and friendly**. They always **welcome you** when you come home and they never leave you. **Moreover**, dogs are very smart and you can **teach them** many tricks.

**3. Do you like watching animals in the zoo?**

> Yes, I enjoy going to the zoo. I like watching **elephants**, **monkeys**, and **pandas** because they are very **cute and interesting**. **However**, sometimes I feel a little **sad** because the animals don't have much **space to move**. I think animals should live in their **natural environment**.

**4. Is it important for children to know about animals?**

> Yes, I think it is **very important**. When children learn about animals, they understand more about **nature** and the world around them. **Besides**, it teaches them to be **kind** and have **respect for** all living things. This helps children become more **caring** people.

**5. Why do people have pets?**

> I think people have pets for many reasons. **Firstly**, pets help people **reduce stress** and feel **less lonely**. After a long day at work, playing with a pet **makes you feel** relaxed. **Secondly**, some people, especially old people, need a **companion**. A pet is like a **best friend** who is always there for them.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **has become a part of + N** | Đã trở thành một phần của... | *He has become a part of my family* |
| **Moreover, ...** | Hơn nữa... | *Moreover, dogs are very smart* |
| **have respect for + N** | Có sự tôn trọng đối với... | *Have respect for all living things* |
| **reduce stress** | Giảm căng thẳng | *Pets help people reduce stress* |
| **makes you feel + adj** | Khiến bạn cảm thấy... | *Playing with a pet makes you feel relaxed* |
:::

## Speaking Part 2: Discussion

> You are 35 years old. If you could choose your job again, would you choose your **current job** or **another job**? Why?

### Bài mẫu:

> **If I could choose again, I would still choose my current job.** I am a teacher, and I think it is the **best job** for me.
>
> **The first reason is that** my job **matches my interests** and skills. I have always loved **helping people learn** new things. When I see my students **improve** and succeed, I feel very **proud and happy**. This feeling is **priceless**.
>
> **The second reason is that** I have already gained a lot of **experience** in this field. After many years of working, I have built good **relationships** with my colleagues and students. If I started a new career, I would have to **learn everything from the beginning** again, which would be very **difficult and stressful**.
>
> **I would not choose another job** because changing careers at 35 is very **risky**. I would lose my **stable income** and have to spend a lot of time and money on **training**. **Besides**, there is no guarantee that I would enjoy the new job.
>
> **In short**, I would keep my current job because it matches my passion and I have valuable experience in it.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 2]
**Bước 1 — Chọn:** *If I could choose again, I would still choose...*

**Bước 2 — Lý do 1:** *The first reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 3 — Lý do 2:** *The second reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 4 — Loại bỏ:** *I would not choose... because...*

**Bước 5 — Kết:** *In short, ...*

| Từ vựng quan trọng | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **match my interests** | phù hợp với sở thích |
| **gain experience** | tích lũy kinh nghiệm |
| **learn everything from the beginning** | học lại từ đầu |
| **stable income** | thu nhập ổn định |
| **priceless** | vô giá |
:::

## Speaking Part 3: Topic Development (nói 2 phút)

> **Vietnam is a great destination for foreigners:** beauty spots, public security, comfortable accommodation.

### Bài mẫu:

> Today, I would like to talk about **why Vietnam is a great destination for foreign tourists**. I think there are three main reasons.
>
> **Firstly**, Vietnam has many **beautiful beauty spots**. We have famous places like **Ha Long Bay**, **Hoi An Ancient Town**, and **Phu Quoc Island**. These places have **stunning natural scenery** and rich **cultural heritage**. Every year, millions of tourists come to Vietnam to enjoy these **amazing** places.
>
> **Secondly**, Vietnam is a very **safe country** for visitors. The **crime rate** in Vietnam is quite **low** compared to many other countries. **Moreover**, Vietnamese people are very **friendly and helpful**. Tourists often say that local people are always ready to **help them** when they get lost or need information.
>
> **Thirdly**, Vietnam has **comfortable accommodation** for all budgets. From **cheap hostels** to **luxury resorts**, tourists can easily find a place to stay that **suits their budget**. The service quality has also **improved a lot** in recent years, and many hotels now meet **international standards**.
>
> **In conclusion**, with beautiful landscapes, good security, and comfortable places to stay, Vietnam is truly an **ideal destination** for foreign tourists.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 3]
**Mở bài:** *Today, I would like to talk about...*

**Thân bài:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Thirdly, ...*

**Kết:** *In conclusion, ...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **beauty spot** | Danh lam thắng cảnh | *Vietnam has many beautiful beauty spots* |
| **crime rate** | Tỉ lệ tội phạm | *The crime rate in Vietnam is quite low* |
| **suits their budget** | Phù hợp với ngân sách | *A place that suits their budget* |
| **improved a lot** | Cải thiện nhiều | *Service quality has improved a lot* |
| **international standards** | Tiêu chuẩn quốc tế | *Many hotels meet international standards* |
| **ideal destination** | Điểm đến lý tưởng | *Vietnam is an ideal destination* |
:::

## Writing Task 1: Informal Letter

> Your friend Jane asks about your party. Write a letter telling her about: **the guests**, **clothes requirements**, and **how to get** from her workplace to the party.

### Bài mẫu:

> Dear Jane,
>
> **Thanks for your letter!** I'm so happy that you're coming to my party. **Let me give you** some details about it.
>
> **About the guests**, I've invited about **20 people**. Most of them are my **university friends** and some **colleagues** from work. You already know some of them, like Mai and Tuan. Don't worry — everyone is very **friendly**, so you'll **have a great time**!
>
> **About the clothes**, the party has a **dress code**. Everyone should wear **something white**. It's a white theme party! You can wear a **white dress** or a **white top** with jeans. Just make sure you wear something **comfortable** because we will dance a lot.
>
> **About how to get there**, from your workplace, the easiest way is to **take bus number 5**. Get off at Nguyen Hue station, then **walk straight** for about 5 minutes. You will see my apartment building on the **left side**. **Alternatively**, you can **book a Grab** and it will take only about **15 minutes**. I'll send you the exact address on Zalo.
>
> **I can't wait to see you!** Let me know if you have any questions.
>
> **Love,**
> Linh

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Thư thân mật]
**Mở thư:** *Dear + tên, Thanks for your letter! / I'm so happy that...*

**Thân thư:** Trả lời lần lượt từng câu hỏi: khách → trang phục → đường đi

**Kết thư:** *I can't wait to see you! Love, + tên*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **Let me give you some details** | Để mình kể chi tiết cho bạn |
| **dress code** | Quy định về trang phục |
| **The easiest way is to + V** | Cách dễ nhất là... |
| **walk straight for + time** | Đi thẳng khoảng... |
| **Alternatively, ...** | Ngoài ra, bạn có thể... |
| **I can't wait to + V** | Mình rất nóng lòng muốn... |

**Lưu ý:** Thư thân mật dùng **short forms** (I'm, you'll, don't) và giọng văn **vui vẻ, gần gũi**. Đếm đủ **120+ từ**.
:::

## Writing Task 2: Essay — Benefits and Drawbacks

> Discuss the **benefits and drawbacks of studying abroad**.

### Bài mẫu:

> **Nowadays**, many young people dream of **studying abroad**. While studying in another country brings many benefits, it also has some drawbacks. **In this essay**, I will discuss both sides of this topic.
>
> **On the one hand**, studying abroad has many **benefits**. **Firstly**, students can **become more independent**. They have to cook, clean, and **manage their money** by themselves. This helps them **grow up** quickly. **Secondly**, students can have **new experiences** and learn about different cultures. They can make friends from many countries and **broaden their horizons**. **Finally**, studying abroad gives students access to **high-quality education** at **prestigious universities**, which can help them **get better jobs** in the future.
>
> **On the other hand**, there are also some **drawbacks**. **Firstly**, studying abroad is very **expensive**. Students have to pay high **tuition fees** and living costs, which is a big **financial burden** for their families. **Secondly**, many students **suffer from homesickness** because they are far away from their loved ones. This can make them feel **lonely and sad**. **Finally**, students may face **language barriers**, making it hard to understand lectures and **communicate** with local people.
>
> **In conclusion**, studying abroad has both advantages and disadvantages. I think students should **prepare carefully** before going abroad so that they can enjoy the benefits and **overcome** the challenges.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Benefits and Drawbacks]
**Mở bài:** *While... brings many benefits, it also has some drawbacks. In this essay, I will discuss both sides.*

**Thân bài 1 (Benefits):** *On the one hand, ... Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Finally, ...*

**Thân bài 2 (Drawbacks):** *On the other hand, ... Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Finally, ...*

**Kết luận:** *In conclusion, ... has both advantages and disadvantages.*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **become more independent** | Trở nên tự lập hơn |
| **broaden their horizons** | Mở rộng tầm nhìn |
| **prestigious university** | Trường đại học danh tiếng |
| **financial burden** | Gánh nặng tài chính |
| **suffer from homesickness** | Chịu nỗi nhớ nhà |
| **language barrier** | Rào cản ngôn ngữ |
| **prepare carefully** | Chuẩn bị kỹ lưỡng |

**Lưu ý:** Bài luận cần **ít nhất 150 từ**. Dạng Benefits and Drawbacks cần trình bày rõ **lợi ích** và **hạn chế**, sau đó đưa ra nhận xét ở kết luận.
:::
