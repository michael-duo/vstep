## Speaking Part 1: Interview

### Topic A — Daily Routine

**1. What is your daily routine?**

> Every day, I **wake up at 6 o'clock** in the morning. After that, I **wash my face**, brush my teeth, and have breakfast. I usually **go to school** at 7 a.m. After school, I **come home** around 5 p.m., have dinner with my family, and then **do my homework**. Before going to bed, I like to **watch TV** or read a book.

**2. What part of the day do you like most?**

> The part of the day I like most is **the evening**. After a long day at school, I can finally **relax** and do things I enjoy. I usually **spend time with my family**, watch movies, or chat with my friends online. **It makes me feel** happy and comfortable.

**3. Do you think it is important to have a daily routine?**

> Yes, I think having a daily routine is **very important**. **Firstly**, it helps me **manage my time** better, so I don't waste time. **Secondly**, when I have a routine, I feel more **organized** and less stressed. **Without a routine**, I would forget many things and feel confused.

**4. What is the busiest part of the day for you?**

> The busiest part of my day is **the morning**. I have to **get ready** very quickly because my class starts early. I need to eat breakfast, prepare my books, and **catch the bus** to school. Sometimes, I feel very **rushed** and don't have enough time for everything.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **Every day, I usually + V** | Hàng ngày, tôi thường... | *Every day, I wake up at 6 o'clock* |
| **The part of the day I like most is...** | Phần trong ngày tôi thích nhất là... | *The part of the day I like most is the evening* |
| **It makes me feel + adj** | Nó khiến tôi cảm thấy... | *It makes me feel happy and comfortable* |
| **manage my time** | Quản lý thời gian | *It helps me manage my time better* |
| **Without + N, I would + V** | Nếu không có... tôi sẽ... | *Without a routine, I would forget many things* |
:::

### Topic B — English Learning

**1. Do you like learning English?**

> Yes, I **really like** learning English. I think English is a **useful language** because many people around the world speak it. **Moreover**, learning English helps me **watch movies** and **listen to songs** without subtitles, which is very enjoyable.

**2. Why did you learn English and how did you learn it?**

> I **started learning English** when I was in **primary school** because it was a **required subject**. At first, I learned it at school with my teacher. Later, I also learned by **watching English videos** on YouTube and **reading simple stories**. I think **practicing every day** is the best way to improve.

**3. Will you study another language in the future?**

> Yes, **in the future**, I would like to learn **Korean**. I love watching Korean dramas, and I want to **understand** them without subtitles. **Besides**, knowing more languages can help me **get a better job** and **communicate** with more people.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **I really like + Ving** | Tôi thực sự thích... | *I really like learning English* |
| **started + Ving + when...** | Bắt đầu... khi... | *I started learning English when I was in primary school* |
| **Moreover, ...** | Hơn nữa... | *Moreover, learning English helps me watch movies* |
| **In the future, I would like to + V** | Trong tương lai, tôi muốn... | *In the future, I would like to learn Korean* |
| **Besides, ...** | Ngoài ra... | *Besides, knowing more languages can help me* |
:::

## Speaking Part 2: Discussion

> Your friend is going to study abroad. You want to give a gift. Which would you choose: **a book**, **a self-designed photo album**, or **a silk scarf**?

### Bài mẫu:

> **I would choose a self-designed photo album.** I think it is the **best gift** for my friend who is going to study abroad.
>
> **The first reason is that** the photo album is very **meaningful**. It contains pictures of our **happy memories** together, like trips, parties, and school days. When my friend feels **homesick** in a foreign country, she can look at the photos and **feel better**.
>
> **The second reason is that** this gift is very **unique and personal**. I designed it by myself, so it shows how much I **care about** my friend. No one can buy this gift in any store.
>
> **I would not choose a book** because I don't know what kind of book my friend likes, and she might **already have** it. **As for the silk scarf**, it is nice but it doesn't have any **personal meaning**. It is like a gift you buy in a hurry.
>
> **In short**, a self-designed photo album is the best choice because it is meaningful, unique, and shows my love for my friend.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 2]
**Bước 1 — Chọn:** *I would choose + lựa chọn*

**Bước 2 — Lý do 1:** *The first reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 3 — Lý do 2:** *The second reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 4 — Loại bỏ:** *I would not choose... because...* / *As for..., it is...*

**Bước 5 — Kết:** *In short, ...*

| Từ vựng quan trọng | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **meaningful** | có ý nghĩa |
| **happy memories** | những kỷ niệm vui |
| **homesick** | nhớ nhà |
| **unique and personal** | độc nhất và cá nhân |
| **care about + sb** | quan tâm đến ai |
:::

## Speaking Part 3: Topic Development (nói 2 phút)

> **Impacts of free wifi at university** (positive and negative).

### Bài mẫu:

> Today, I would like to talk about **the impacts of free wifi at university**. I think free wifi brings both **positive and negative** effects on students.
>
> **Firstly**, free wifi has many **positive impacts**. Students can easily **search for materials** and **download** documents for their studies. They don't need to go to the library every time they need information. **In addition**, free wifi helps students **save money** because they don't have to buy expensive **data packages** every month. This is especially helpful for students from **poor families**.
>
> **However**, free wifi also has some **negative impacts**. Many students use wifi to **play online games** or browse **social media** during class time. This is a big **distraction** and they cannot **concentrate on** their lessons. **As a result**, their grades may become worse. **Moreover**, some students **stay up late** at night because they can use the internet for free, which is **bad for their health**.
>
> **In conclusion**, free wifi at university is very **useful** for studying, but students need to **use it wisely** and not waste time on entertainment during class.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 3]
**Mở bài:** *Today, I would like to talk about...*

**Thân bài:** *Firstly, ... In addition, ... However, ... Moreover, ...*

**Kết:** *In conclusion, ...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **search for + N** | Tìm kiếm... | *Students can search for materials online* |
| **concentrate on + N** | Tập trung vào... | *They cannot concentrate on their lessons* |
| **As a result, ...** | Kết quả là... | *As a result, their grades may become worse* |
| **save money** | Tiết kiệm tiền | *Free wifi helps students save money* |
| **use it wisely** | Sử dụng khôn ngoan | *Students need to use it wisely* |
| **distraction** | Sự xao nhãng | *Social media is a big distraction* |
:::

## Writing Task 1: Formal Letter

> You are planning a honeymoon trip next month. Write a letter to an event organiser to ask about: **location** (beach, mountain, or island), **price**, and **activities**.

### Bài mẫu:

> Dear Sir/Madam,
>
> **I am writing to inquire about** honeymoon trip packages for next month. My husband and I are **planning our honeymoon** and we would like to get some information from your company.
>
> **Firstly**, I would like to know about the **locations** you offer. We are interested in going to a **beach**, a **mountain area**, or an **island**. **Could you please recommend** the best destination for a couple? We prefer a place that is **quiet and romantic**.
>
> **Secondly**, I would like to ask about the **price**. How much does a 5-day trip cost **per person**? Does the price **include** hotel, meals, and transportation? **In addition**, is there any **discount** if we book early?
>
> **Finally**, could you please tell me about the **activities** available at the destination? We enjoy **water sports** like swimming and snorkeling. We would also like to know if there are **romantic dinners** on the beach or **sightseeing tours**.
>
> I would **appreciate it** if you could reply **at your earliest convenience**. I **look forward to hearing from you**.
>
> **Yours faithfully,**
> Nguyen Thi Mai

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Thư trang trọng]
**Mở thư:** *Dear Sir/Madam, I am writing to inquire about...*

**Thân thư:** Hỏi lần lượt từng vấn đề: Firstly → Secondly → Finally

**Kết thư:** *I would appreciate it if you could reply at your earliest convenience. Yours faithfully, + tên*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **I am writing to inquire about + N** | Tôi viết thư để hỏi về... |
| **Could you please recommend + N** | Bạn có thể gợi ý... |
| **Does the price include + N** | Giá có bao gồm... không? |
| **I would appreciate it if you could + V** | Tôi sẽ rất biết ơn nếu bạn có thể... |
| **I look forward to hearing from you** | Tôi mong nhận được phản hồi |
| **at your earliest convenience** | Khi nào bạn thuận tiện nhất |

**Lưu ý:** Thư trang trọng dùng **full forms** (I am, I would, do not), giọng văn **lịch sự, chuyên nghiệp**. Đếm đủ **120+ từ**.
:::

## Writing Task 2: Essay — Agree or Disagree

> Computers play an important role in the classroom. Some people think that in the future, teachers will not be necessary anymore. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

### Bài mẫu:

> **Nowadays**, computers are becoming more and more popular in schools and universities. **Some people believe** that computers will **replace** teachers in the future. **However**, I **strongly disagree** with this idea, and I will explain my reasons below.
>
> **It is true that** computers are very **useful** in education. Students can use computers to **search for information** on the internet, watch **online lessons**, and do exercises on **learning apps**. **Moreover**, computers can help students study **anytime and anywhere**, which is very convenient. **Therefore**, some people think that teachers are no longer needed.
>
> **However**, I believe that teachers are still **very important** and computers **cannot replace** them. **Firstly**, teachers can **motivate and inspire** students to study harder. A good teacher knows how to **encourage** weak students and help them **believe in themselves**. A computer cannot do this. **Secondly**, teachers can understand students' **emotions and problems**. When a student is sad or stressed, a teacher can talk to them and give **advice**. A computer does not have **feelings**. **Finally**, young children need teachers to **guide** them and **manage** the classroom. Without teachers, students might play games on computers instead of studying.
>
> **In conclusion**, although computers are helpful tools in education, they **cannot replace** teachers. Teachers **play an irreplaceable role** in helping students learn and grow. **I believe that** in the future, teachers and computers will **work together** to make education better.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Agree or Disagree]
**Mở bài:** *Some people believe that... However, I strongly disagree with this idea.*

**Thân bài 1 (nhượng bộ):** *It is true that + ý kiến đối lập... Therefore, some people think...*

**Thân bài 2 (ý kiến chính):** *However, I believe that... Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Finally, ...*

**Kết luận:** *In conclusion, ... I believe that...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **It is true that... However, ...** | Đúng là... Tuy nhiên... |
| **cannot replace + N** | Không thể thay thế... |
| **motivate and inspire + sb** | Tạo động lực và truyền cảm hứng cho ai |
| **play an irreplaceable role in + N** | Đóng vai trò không thể thay thế trong... |
| **believe in themselves** | Tin vào bản thân mình |
| **work together** | Làm việc cùng nhau |

**Lưu ý:** Bài luận cần **ít nhất 150 từ**. Dùng **linking words** ở đầu mỗi câu. Với dạng Agree/Disagree, nên nhượng bộ ý kiến đối lập trước rồi nêu ý chính.
:::
