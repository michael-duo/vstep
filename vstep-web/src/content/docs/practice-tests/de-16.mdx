## Speaking Part 1: Interview

### Topic — Reading

**1. Do you like reading?**

> Yes, I really **enjoy reading** in my free time. I usually read **short stories** and novels because they help me **relax** after a long day. I think reading is a very good habit.

**2. Where do you usually read?**

> I usually read **at home**, especially **before going to bed**. I have a small bookshelf in my bedroom and I like to read on my bed. **Sometimes**, I also read at the **library** near my school because it is very quiet there.

**3. What is the importance of books in your life?**

> I think books are very **important** in my life. They help me **gain knowledge** and learn many new things. **Moreover**, reading books helps me **improve my vocabulary** and my writing skills. I believe that books are **good friends** that can teach us a lot.

**4. Have you ever given books as gifts?**

> Yes, I have. Last year, I **gave my best friend** a book on her birthday. It was a book about **how to learn English**. She really **appreciated** it because she was preparing for her English exam. I think books are **meaningful gifts** because they are useful.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **enjoy + Ving** | Thích làm gì | *I enjoy reading in my free time* |
| **Moreover, ...** | Hơn nữa... (thêm ý) | *Moreover, reading books helps me improve my vocabulary* |
| **I think + S + V** | Tôi nghĩ... | *I think books are very important* |
| **gain knowledge** | Thu nhận kiến thức | *They help me gain knowledge* |
| **appreciate + N** | Trân trọng, đánh giá cao | *She really appreciated it* |
:::

### Topic — Holidays

**1. What do you usually do on holidays?**

> On holidays, I usually **spend time with my family**. We often cook together and have a big meal at home. **Sometimes**, I **go out** with my friends to watch movies or go shopping.

**2. Where do you like to go on holidays?**

> I like to go to **the beach** on holidays because I love swimming and playing in the sand. My favorite beach is in Da Nang. **Besides**, I also enjoy going to the **countryside** to visit my grandparents.

**3. Do you think holidays are important?**

> Yes, I think holidays are very **important** for everyone. After working or studying hard, we need time to **rest and recharge**. **Having holidays** also helps us **spend quality time** with our family and friends.

**4. What is your favorite holiday?**

> My favorite holiday is **Tet** because my whole family **gets together**. We eat **traditional food**, visit our relatives, and children receive **lucky money**. **It makes me feel** very happy and warm every year.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **spend time with + người** | Dành thời gian với... | *I spend time with my family* |
| **Besides, ...** | Ngoài ra... | *Besides, I also enjoy going to the countryside* |
| **Having + N + helps sb + V** | Việc có... giúp ai đó... | *Having holidays helps us spend quality time* |
| **rest and recharge** | Nghỉ ngơi và nạp lại năng lượng | *We need time to rest and recharge* |
| **It makes me feel + adj** | Nó khiến tôi cảm thấy... | *It makes me feel very happy* |
:::

## Speaking Part 2: Discussion

> You are on a holiday. Which activity do you prefer: **hiking**, **going swimming**, or **visiting family**?

### Bài mẫu:

> **I would choose visiting family.** I think it is the **best activity** for a holiday.
>
> **The first reason is that** holidays are a great chance to **strengthen family bonds**. During the holiday, my whole family can eat together, talk, and share stories. We are all very busy on normal days, so this is a **precious time** to be with each other.
>
> **The second reason is that** being with my family makes me feel **relaxed and happy**. When I am with my parents and grandparents, I feel warm and loved. This helps me **recharge** after working hard.
>
> **I would not choose hiking** because it can be very **tiring** and sometimes **dangerous**, especially when the weather is bad. **As for going swimming**, it depends on the season. In winter, it is too cold to swim, and not everyone **knows how to swim**.
>
> **In short**, visiting family is the best choice because it helps me feel happy and brings my family closer together.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 2]
**Bước 1 — Chọn:** *I would choose + lựa chọn*

**Bước 2 — Lý do 1:** *The first reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 3 — Lý do 2:** *The second reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 4 — Loại bỏ:** *I would not choose... because...* / *As for..., it is...*

**Bước 5 — Kết:** *In short, ...*

| Từ vựng quan trọng | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **strengthen family bonds** | Gắn kết tình cảm gia đình |
| **precious time** | Thời gian quý giá |
| **recharge** | Nạp lại năng lượng |
| **tiring** | Mệt mỏi |
:::

## Speaking Part 3: Topic Development (nói 2 phút)

> **Important things in your life:** friends, family, career.

### Bài mẫu:

> Today, I would like to talk about **the important things in my life**. I think there are three things that matter the most: family, friends, and career.
>
> **Firstly**, I believe **family is the most important thing** in my life. My parents **raised and supported** me from when I was a child. They always **encourage** me when I face difficulties. **Whenever** I feel sad or stressed, my family is always there for me. I think family is the **foundation** of everything.
>
> **Secondly**, friends also **play an important role** in my life. My close friends **share my happiness and sadness** with me. They give me **useful advice** when I have problems. Good friends make my life more enjoyable and meaningful.
>
> **Thirdly**, having a good **career** is also very important. A good career helps me **earn money** and become **independent**. When I have a good job, I feel more **confident** and have a **sense of achievement**. I can also take better care of my family.
>
> **In conclusion**, family, friends, and career are all important parts of my life. I believe we should **treasure** them and try to keep a good balance among these three things.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 3]
**Mở bài:** *Today, I would like to talk about... I think there are three things...*

**Thân bài:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Thirdly, ...*

**Kết:** *In conclusion, I believe...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **play an important role in + N** | Đóng vai trò quan trọng trong... | *Friends play an important role in my life* |
| **Whenever + S + V, S + V** | Bất cứ khi nào... thì... | *Whenever I feel sad, my family is there for me* |
| **raise and support + sb** | Nuôi nấng và hỗ trợ ai | *My parents raised and supported me* |
| **earn money** | Kiếm tiền | *A good career helps me earn money* |
| **sense of achievement** | Cảm giác thành tựu | *I have a sense of achievement* |
| **treasure + N** | Trân trọng... | *We should treasure them* |
:::

## Writing Task 1: Formal Letter

> Your manager invited you to a workshop about improving soft skills. Write a letter asking about: the specific location, the time, the content of the workshop, and any other information you need.

### Bài mẫu:

> Dear Mr. Nguyen,
>
> **I am writing to ask for** more information about the upcoming workshop on improving soft skills that you mentioned last week. I am very **interested in attending**, but I need some details.
>
> **Firstly**, could you please tell me the **specific location** of the workshop? I would like to know whether it will be held at our office or at **an external venue** so that I can plan my transportation.
>
> **Secondly**, I would like to know the **exact date and time** of the workshop. Could you let me know what time it starts and **how long it will last**? I need to arrange my work schedule in advance.
>
> **Furthermore**, could you provide some details about the **content** of the workshop? I am curious about which **soft skills** will be covered, such as communication, teamwork, or time management.
>
> **Finally**, I would like to ask whether there is anything I need to **prepare in advance**. For example, should I bring a laptop? And will there be any **materials provided**?
>
> **Thank you for your time.** I look forward to hearing from you soon.
>
> **Yours sincerely,**
> Tran Van Minh

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Thư trang trọng]
**Mở thư:** *Dear Mr./Ms. + tên, I am writing to ask for / inquire about...*

**Thân thư:** Hỏi lần lượt từng nội dung: Firstly → Secondly → Furthermore → Finally

**Kết thư:** *Thank you for your time. I look forward to hearing from you soon. Yours sincerely, + tên*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **I am writing to ask for + N** | Tôi viết thư để hỏi về... |
| **Could you please tell me + N?** | Bạn có thể cho tôi biết...? |
| **I would like to know whether + S + V** | Tôi muốn biết liệu... |
| **I look forward to + Ving** | Tôi mong chờ... |

**Lưu ý:** Thư trang trọng **không dùng** short forms (I'm, don't). Dùng giọng văn **lịch sự, đầy đủ**. Đếm đủ **120+ từ**.
:::

## Writing Task 2: Essay — Causes and Effects

> What are the causes and effects of stress?

### Bài mẫu:

> **Nowadays**, stress has become a very common problem in modern life. Many people, from students to workers, **suffer from** stress every day. **In this essay**, I will discuss the main causes and effects of stress.
>
> **There are several causes** of stress. **Firstly**, many people feel stressed because of **tight deadlines** at work or school. They have too much work to do in a short time, so they feel **pressured**. **Secondly**, **social pressure** is another cause. Many young people compare themselves with others on social media, which makes them feel **anxious** and not good enough. **Finally**, working or studying for **long hours** without rest also **leads to** stress. When people do not have time to relax, they become tired and unhappy.
>
> Stress can cause many **negative effects**. **First**, it can lead to **mental health problems** such as anxiety and depression. Stressed people often feel sad and cannot enjoy life. **Second**, stress causes **sleeping problems** like insomnia. People who are stressed find it hard to fall asleep at night. **Moreover**, stress can also affect our **physical health**. For example, it can cause headaches, high blood pressure, and a weak immune system.
>
> **In conclusion**, stress is mainly caused by heavy workloads, social pressure, and lack of rest. It can lead to serious mental and physical problems. **Therefore**, people should learn to **manage their time** better and **take regular breaks** to stay healthy.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Causes and Effects]
**Mở bài:** *Nowadays, ... has become a common problem. In this essay, I will discuss the main causes and effects of...*

**Thân bài 1 — Causes:** *There are several causes. Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Finally, ...*

**Thân bài 2 — Effects:** *... can cause many negative effects. First, ... Second, ... Moreover, ...*

**Kết luận:** *In conclusion, ... is caused by... It can lead to... Therefore, people should...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **There are several causes of + N** | Có một số nguyên nhân của... |
| **lead to + N** | Dẫn đến... |
| **suffer from + N** | Chịu đựng, mắc phải... |
| **Therefore, S + should + V** | Vì vậy, ai đó nên... |
| **tight deadlines** | Hạn chót gấp gáp |
| **social pressure** | Áp lực xã hội |

**Lưu ý:** Bài luận cần **ít nhất 150 từ**. Tách rõ đoạn nguyên nhân và đoạn hậu quả. Dùng **linking words** để bài mạch lạc.
:::
