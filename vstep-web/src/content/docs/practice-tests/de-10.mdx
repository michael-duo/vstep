## Speaking Part 1: Interview

### Topic A — Walking

**1. Do you walk a lot?**

> Yes, I walk **quite a lot** every day. I usually walk to school in the morning, and after dinner, I **take a walk** around my neighborhood. I think walking is a **healthy habit** that helps me **stay fit**.

**2. Do you walk more now than in the past?**

> Yes, I walk **much more** now than in the past. Before, I used to take a motorbike everywhere. But now I try to walk more because I want to **stay healthy** and **save money** on gas. Walking also helps me **reduce stress**.

**3. Do you think people will walk more in the future?**

> Yes, I think people will walk more in the future because they are becoming more **health-conscious**. **Moreover**, many cities are building more **parks** and **walking paths**, so it is easier for people to walk. Walking is also good for the **environment** because it does not cause pollution.

**4. Where do you usually walk?**

> I usually take a walk in the **park near my house**. It has many **trees and flowers**, and the air is very fresh. Sometimes, I also walk along the **river bank** in the evening. It is very **peaceful and relaxing** there.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **take a walk** | Đi dạo | *I take a walk around my neighborhood* |
| **stay healthy / stay fit** | Giữ sức khoẻ | *Walking helps me stay fit* |
| **health-conscious** | Quan tâm đến sức khoẻ | *People are becoming more health-conscious* |
| **Moreover, ...** | Hơn nữa... | *Moreover, many cities are building parks* |
| **reduce stress** | Giảm căng thẳng | *Walking helps me reduce stress* |
:::

### Topic B — Shopping

**1. Do you enjoy shopping?**

> Yes, I **quite enjoy** shopping. I like going to **shopping malls** with my friends on weekends. We look at clothes, try on shoes, and sometimes buy things we like. It is a fun way to **spend time together**.

**2. How often do you go shopping?**

> I go shopping about **once or twice a month**. I usually buy clothes and **daily necessities**. However, I also do **online shopping** quite often because it is more **convenient** — I can buy things from home without going anywhere.

**3. What is the most expensive item you have purchased?**

> The most expensive thing I have ever bought is a **laptop** for my studies. It cost about **15 million VND**. I saved money for several months to buy it. **Although** it was expensive, I think it was **worth it** because I use it every day for studying and working.

**4. Is shopping a popular activity in your country?**

> Yes, shopping is very **popular** in Vietnam. Many people enjoy going to **shopping malls** and **markets** on weekends. **Especially during** sales events like **Black Friday** or **Tet**, the stores are always **crowded** with shoppers. **Online shopping** is also becoming **more and more popular**.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **once or twice a month** | Một hoặc hai lần một tháng | *I go shopping once or twice a month* |
| **Although + S + V, S + V** | Mặc dù... | *Although it was expensive, it was worth it* |
| **worth it** | Đáng giá | *I think it was worth it* |
| **more and more popular** | Ngày càng phổ biến | *Online shopping is more and more popular* |
| **Especially during + N** | Đặc biệt trong... | *Especially during sales events like Black Friday* |
:::

## Speaking Part 2: Discussion

> Your foreign friends want to learn about Vietnamese history. Which way is the best: **go to a museum**, **study through documentaries**, or **read books**?

### Bài mẫu:

> **I would choose going to a museum.** I think this is the **best way** for my foreign friends to learn about Vietnamese history.
>
> **The first reason is that** in a museum, my friends can see **real artifacts** and pictures from the past. They can look at old weapons, clothes, and documents with their own eyes. This is much more **interesting and memorable** than just reading about them in a book.
>
> **The second reason is that** many museums in Vietnam now have **English-speaking tour guides**. My friends can listen to the guide and **ask questions** directly. This helps them **understand** Vietnamese history more easily and clearly.
>
> **I would not choose reading books** because history books are usually very **long and difficult** to understand. My foreign friends might find them **boring**. **As for documentaries**, it is also not the best choice because there are **very few** English-language documentaries about Vietnamese history. My friends might not find good ones to watch.
>
> **In short**, visiting a museum is the best option because my friends can see real things and get clear explanations from a tour guide.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 2]
**Bước 1 — Chọn:** *I would choose + lựa chọn*

**Bước 2 — Lý do 1:** *The first reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 3 — Lý do 2:** *The second reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 4 — Loại bỏ:** *I would not choose... because...* / *As for..., it is...*

**Bước 5 — Kết:** *In short, ...*

| Từ vựng quan trọng | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **real artifacts** | hiện vật thật |
| **memorable** | đáng nhớ |
| **tour guide** | hướng dẫn viên |
| **ask questions directly** | hỏi trực tiếp |
:::

## Speaking Part 3: Topic Development (nói 2 phút)

> **Education is not the only way to succeed:** more independent, succeed earlier than others, run their own business.

### Bài mẫu:

> Today, I would like to talk about **why education is not the only way to succeed** in life. I agree with this idea, and I have three reasons.
>
> **Firstly**, people who do not go to university often become **more independent**. They have to find jobs and **solve problems** by themselves from a young age. They learn **practical skills** that cannot be taught in a classroom. **As a result**, they become **self-reliant** and can handle many situations in life.
>
> **Secondly**, these people can **succeed earlier** than others. While their friends spend four years at university, they start working and **earning money** right away. They gain **real work experience** and can build their career faster. **For example**, many successful people started working at the age of 18 and became managers before they were 25.
>
> **Thirdly**, some people choose to **run their own business** instead of going to university. **For instance**, famous entrepreneurs like **Bill Gates** and **Steve Jobs** dropped out of college but built **very successful companies**. This shows that **creativity, hard work, and determination** are sometimes more important than a university degree.
>
> **In conclusion**, I believe that education is valuable, but it is **not the only path** to success. Hard work, practical skills, and the right attitude can also lead to a successful life.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 3]
**Mở bài:** *Today, I would like to talk about... I agree with this idea.*

**Thân bài:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Thirdly, ...*

**Kết:** *In conclusion, I believe...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **self-reliant** | Tự lực | *They become self-reliant* |
| **As a result, ...** | Kết quả là... | *As a result, they become self-reliant* |
| **For instance, ...** | Ví dụ... | *For instance, Bill Gates dropped out* |
| **practical skills** | Kỹ năng thực tế | *They learn practical skills* |
| **run one's own business** | Kinh doanh riêng | *Some people choose to run their own business* |
| **not the only path to + N** | Không phải con đường duy nhất đến... | *Education is not the only path to success* |
:::

## Writing Task 1: Informal Letter

> Write a letter to congratulate your friend on his/her success in the class 12 board exam.

### Bài mẫu:

> Dear Huong,
>
> I just heard the **wonderful news** — **congratulations on** passing the class 12 board exam! I am so **happy and proud** of you.
>
> I know you **studied very hard** for this exam. You stayed up late many nights to review your lessons and **never gave up** even when it was difficult. I remember you told me that you were very worried, but look at you now — you **passed with excellent results**! You really **deserve it**.
>
> I think your parents must be very **proud** of you too. They always supported you and **believed in** you. This success is not only yours but also theirs.
>
> **By the way**, do you have any plans for the future? Which **university** do you want to go to? And what **major** will you choose? I hope you will **continue to work hard** and **achieve great things** in the future. I am sure you will do very well at university too.
>
> **Let's meet up** and **celebrate together** soon! I want to take you out for a nice meal. You've earned it!
>
> I am so proud of you, and I **wish you all the best** for the future!
>
> **Love,**
> Nam

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Thư thân mật]
**Mở thư:** *Dear + tên, Congratulations on...! / I am so happy and proud of you.*

**Thân thư:** Chúc mừng → khen ngợi nỗ lực → hỏi kế hoạch → hẹn gặp

**Kết thư:** *I wish you all the best. Love, + tên*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **Congratulations on + Ving/N** | Chúc mừng về... |
| **You really deserve it** | Bạn thật sự xứng đáng |
| **never gave up** | Không bao giờ bỏ cuộc |
| **continue to + V** | Tiếp tục làm gì |
| **achieve great things** | Đạt được những điều lớn lao |
| **wish you all the best** | Chúc bạn mọi điều tốt đẹp |

**Lưu ý:** Thư thân mật được dùng **short forms** (I'm, It's, don't) và giọng văn **vui vẻ, gần gũi**. Đếm đủ **120+ từ**.
:::

## Writing Task 2: Essay — Discuss both views

> Some people say we can see films on phones or tablets and there is no need to go to the cinema. Others say films need to be seen in a cinema to be fully enjoyed. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

### Bài mẫu:

> **Nowadays**, many people watch films on their phones or tablets instead of going to the cinema. **Some people believe** that this is enough, **while others think** that the cinema is still the best place to enjoy a film. **In this essay**, I will discuss both views and give my opinion.
>
> **On the one hand**, watching films on phones and tablets has many **advantages**. **Firstly**, it is very **convenient** — people can watch films **anytime and anywhere**, such as on the bus, before bed, or during lunch break. **Secondly**, there are many apps like **Netflix** that offer thousands of films at a **low price**. People do not need to buy expensive cinema tickets. **Moreover**, they can watch films **with their family** at home, which is more comfortable and relaxing.
>
> **On the other hand**, many people believe that films should be seen in a cinema. The cinema has a **large screen** and **surround sound** that give viewers an **immersive experience**. This is especially true for **action movies** and **horror films** — they are much more exciting on a big screen. **In addition**, going to the cinema is a **social activity**. People enjoy going with friends or family, and it creates **beautiful memories**.
>
> **In my opinion**, I believe that both ways of watching films have their value. **However**, for special films that I really love, I prefer to watch them in the cinema because the experience is **much better**. For everyday films, watching on my phone is **good enough**.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Discuss Both Views]
**Mở bài:** *Some people believe that... while others think that... In this essay, I will discuss both views and give my opinion.*

**Thân bài 1:** *On the one hand, ... Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Moreover, ...*

**Thân bài 2:** *On the other hand, ... In addition, ...*

**Kết luận:** *In my opinion, I believe that...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **On the one hand, ... On the other hand, ...** | Một mặt... Mặt khác... |
| **immersive experience** | Trải nghiệm sống động |
| **convenient** | Tiện lợi |
| **social activity** | Hoạt động xã hội |
| **Moreover, ...** | Hơn nữa... |
| **In addition, ...** | Ngoài ra... |
| **However, ...** | Tuy nhiên... |
| **create beautiful memories** | Tạo kỷ niệm đẹp |

**Lưu ý:** Bài luận cần **ít nhất 150 từ**. Dùng **linking words** ở đầu mỗi câu để bài mạch lạc. **Không dùng "I think" quá nhiều** — thay bằng "I believe", "In my opinion".
:::
