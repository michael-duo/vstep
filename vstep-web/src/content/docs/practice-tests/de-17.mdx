## Speaking Part 1: Interview

### Topic — Hobbies

**1. Do you have any hobbies?**

> Yes, my hobby is **listening to music**. I do it every day, especially **after studying**. I think music helps me **relax** and feel happy. I enjoy many kinds of music, but I like **pop music** the most.

**2. What hobbies are popular in your country?**

> In Vietnam, the most popular hobbies are **playing football** among boys and **shopping** among girls. **Moreover**, many young people enjoy **watching movies** and **playing online games** in their free time. Old people usually like **gardening** or **doing exercise** in the park.

**3. Is it important to have a hobby?**

> Yes, I think it is very important. **Having a hobby** helps us **reduce stress** and feel better after a long day of work or study. **Besides**, hobbies help us **make new friends** who share the same interests. A good hobby also keeps our mind **healthy and active**.

**4. What hobbies would you like to try?**

> **In the future**, I would like to try **painting** because I think it is very **creative** and relaxing. I see many people share beautiful paintings on social media, and I want to learn how to do that. I believe painting can help me **express my feelings** in a new way.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **I would like to try + N/Ving** | Tôi muốn thử... | *I would like to try painting* |
| **Having a hobby helps sb + V** | Có sở thích giúp ai đó... | *Having a hobby helps us reduce stress* |
| **reduce stress** | Giảm căng thẳng | *Hobbies help us reduce stress* |
| **Besides, ...** | Ngoài ra... | *Besides, hobbies help us make new friends* |
| **express my feelings** | Thể hiện cảm xúc | *Painting can help me express my feelings* |
:::

## Speaking Part 2: Discussion

> You want to go out with your friends on Sunday evening. Which activity do you prefer: **seeing a movie**, **going shopping**, or **singing karaoke**?

### Bài mẫu:

> **I would choose singing karaoke.** I think it is the **best activity** for a Sunday evening with friends.
>
> **The first reason is that** singing karaoke is very **fun and exciting**. Everyone can sing together, laugh, and have a great time. It is a wonderful way to **create happy memories** with friends.
>
> **The second reason is that** **everyone can participate** regardless of their singing ability. You do not need to sing well to enjoy karaoke. Even if we sing badly, we still **have a lot of fun**. It is a great way to **strengthen our friendship**.
>
> **I would not choose seeing a movie** because we have to **sit quietly** in the cinema for two hours. We cannot talk or interact with each other, so it is not a good way to enjoy time with friends. **As for going shopping**, it can be **expensive** and not everyone enjoys buying things. Some friends might feel bored.
>
> **In short**, singing karaoke is the best choice because it is fun, everyone can join, and it brings us closer together.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 2]
**Bước 1 — Chọn:** *I would choose + lựa chọn*

**Bước 2 — Lý do 1:** *The first reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 3 — Lý do 2:** *The second reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 4 — Loại bỏ:** *I would not choose... because...* / *As for..., it is...*

**Bước 5 — Kết:** *In short, ...*

| Từ vựng quan trọng | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **fun and exciting** | Vui và thú vị |
| **regardless of + N** | Bất kể... |
| **create happy memories** | Tạo kỷ niệm đẹp |
| **strengthen our friendship** | Củng cố tình bạn |
:::

## Speaking Part 3: Topic Development (nói 2 phút)

> **Benefits of the Internet.**

### Bài mẫu:

> Today, I would like to talk about **the benefits of the Internet**. I think the Internet is very important in our modern life, and it brings many advantages.
>
> **Firstly**, the Internet is a huge **source of information**. We can **search for** anything we need in just a few seconds using Google. For example, students can find study materials, read news, and learn new things online. **Thanks to** the Internet, learning has become much easier and faster.
>
> **Secondly**, the Internet helps us **communicate** with people easily. We can **keep in touch** with our friends and family who live far away through apps like Zalo and Facebook. We can also make **free video calls**, which saves a lot of money compared to phone calls.
>
> **Thirdly**, the Internet **provides** many forms of **entertainment**. We can watch movies, listen to music, and play games online. After a long day of work or study, these activities help us **relax and reduce stress**.
>
> **In conclusion**, the Internet is very useful for finding information, communicating with others, and entertaining ourselves. However, we should use it **wisely** and not spend too much time online.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 3]
**Mở bài:** *Today, I would like to talk about... I think... is very important...*

**Thân bài:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Thirdly, ...*

**Kết:** *In conclusion, ...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **source of information** | Nguồn thông tin | *The Internet is a huge source of information* |
| **Thanks to + N** | Nhờ có... | *Thanks to the Internet, learning has become easier* |
| **keep in touch with + sb** | Giữ liên lạc với ai | *We can keep in touch with friends* |
| **provide + N** | Cung cấp... | *The Internet provides many forms of entertainment* |
| **reduce stress** | Giảm căng thẳng | *These activities help us relax and reduce stress* |
| **wisely** | Một cách khôn ngoan | *We should use it wisely* |
:::

## Writing Task 1: Informal Letter

> Write a letter to your friend about one of the most important festivals in Vietnam. Include: when it takes place, who participates, what activities there are, and the importance to Vietnamese people.

### Bài mẫu:

> Dear Tom,
>
> **Thanks for your letter!** You asked me about an important festival in Vietnam, so **let me tell you** about **Tet** — our Lunar New Year. It is the **biggest and most important** festival in my country.
>
> Tet usually **takes place** in **late January or early February**, depending on the lunar calendar. We have about **a week off** from work and school, so everyone is very excited.
>
> **Everyone in Vietnam** celebrates Tet. People who work far from home **travel back** to their hometown to be with their families. It is a time for the **whole family** to get together.
>
> **During Tet**, there are many fun activities. We **clean and decorate** our houses with flowers before the holiday. My mother cooks **traditional food** like banh chung, which is a sticky rice cake. Children receive **lucky money** in red envelopes from adults. We also **visit our relatives** and wish each other good health and happiness.
>
> Tet is very important because it is a time for **family reunion**. It reminds us to **appreciate our family** and **start a new year** with hope and joy.
>
> I **hope you can visit** Vietnam during Tet someday! **Write back soon!**
>
> **Best wishes,**
> Linh

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Thư thân mật]
**Mở thư:** *Dear + tên, Thanks for your letter! Let me tell you about...*

**Thân thư:** Kể lần lượt: khi nào → ai tham gia → hoạt động → ý nghĩa

**Kết thư:** *I hope you can... / Write back soon! Best wishes, + tên*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **Let me tell you about + N** | Để tôi kể cho bạn về... |
| **take place in + time** | Diễn ra vào... |
| **travel back to + place** | Trở về... |
| **family reunion** | Đoàn tụ gia đình |
| **lucky money** | Tiền lì xì |
| **appreciate + N** | Trân trọng... |

**Lưu ý:** Thư thân mật dùng **short forms** (I'm, It's, don't) và giọng văn **vui vẻ, gần gũi**. Đếm đủ **120+ từ**.
:::

## Writing Task 2: Essay — Benefits and Drawbacks

> What are the benefits and drawbacks of social media for young people?

### Bài mẫu:

> **Nowadays**, social media has become very popular among young people. Almost every student in Vietnam uses Facebook, Instagram, or TikTok. **While** social media offers many benefits, it also has some drawbacks. **In this essay**, I will discuss both sides of this issue.
>
> **On the one hand**, social media brings several **benefits** to young people. **Firstly**, it helps them **connect with friends** easily. They can send messages, share photos, and make video calls for free. **Secondly**, young people can **learn new things** by following educational pages and watching useful videos. **Moreover**, social media allows them to **express their ideas** and creativity, which is good for their personal development.
>
> **On the other hand**, social media also has some **drawbacks**. **First**, many young people **waste a lot of time** scrolling through their phones instead of studying. This can **affect** their grades at school. **Second**, **cyberbullying** is a serious problem. Some young people are bullied online, which **seriously affects** their mental health. **Furthermore**, **fake news** spreads quickly on social media, and young people may believe **incorrect information**.
>
> **In conclusion**, social media has both positive and negative sides for young people. I think young people should use it **wisely** and **limit their screen time** so that they can enjoy its benefits without being harmed by its drawbacks.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Benefits and Drawbacks]
**Mở bài:** *While... offers many benefits, it also has some drawbacks. In this essay, I will discuss both sides.*

**Thân bài 1:** *On the one hand, ... Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Moreover, ...*

**Thân bài 2:** *On the other hand, ... First, ... Second, ... Furthermore, ...*

**Kết luận:** *In conclusion, ... has both positive and negative sides.*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **On the one hand, ... On the other hand, ...** | Một mặt... Mặt khác... |
| **It allows sb to + V** | Nó cho phép ai đó làm gì |
| **waste time + Ving** | Lãng phí thời gian làm gì |
| **affect + N** | Ảnh hưởng đến... |
| **cyberbullying** | Bắt nạt trực tuyến |
| **fake news** | Tin giả |
| **limit screen time** | Giới hạn thời gian dùng điện thoại/máy tính |

**Lưu ý:** Bài luận cần **ít nhất 150 từ**. Dùng **linking words** ở đầu mỗi câu để bài mạch lạc. **Không dùng "I think" quá nhiều** — thay bằng "I believe", "In my opinion".
:::
