## Speaking Part 1: Interview

### Topic — Marriage

**1. What do you think about marriage?**

> I think marriage is **one of the most important events** in a person's life. When two people love each other, they want to **build a family** together. Marriage helps people have a **stable life** and someone to **share happiness and difficulties** with.

**2. What age is good to get married?**

> **In my opinion**, people should get married **after 25 years old**. At that age, they usually have a **stable job** and enough **life experience**. If people get married too young, they may not be **mature enough** to handle problems in married life.

**3. What kind of clothes do people wear at weddings?**

> In Vietnam, the bride and groom usually wear **áo dài** at the engagement ceremony. At the wedding party, the bride often wears a **white wedding dress** and the groom wears a **suit**. Guests usually wear **formal clothes** like dresses or shirts.

**4. What presents do people bring to weddings?**

> In Vietnam, most guests give **red envelopes** with money inside. The money helps the new couple **start their life** together. **Besides**, some close friends also give gifts like **home appliances** or **decorations** for the new house.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **one of the most important + N** | Một trong những... quan trọng nhất | *Marriage is one of the most important events* |
| **In my opinion, ...** | Theo ý kiến của tôi... | *In my opinion, people should get married after 25* |
| **If + S + V (too young), S + may not + V** | Nếu... quá trẻ, có thể không... | *If people get married too young, they may not be mature enough* |
| **Besides, ...** | Ngoài ra... | *Besides, some close friends also give gifts* |
| **share happiness and difficulties** | Chia sẻ niềm vui và khó khăn | *Someone to share happiness and difficulties with* |
:::

## Speaking Part 2: Discussion

> Students use mobile phones during lessons. As a teacher, what would you do: **ask them to pay a fine**, **make them stand in the corner for 1 hour**, or **keep their phones until the end of the semester**?

### Bài mẫu:

> **I would choose to keep their phones until the end of the semester.** I think this is the **most effective** way to solve the problem.
>
> **The first reason is that** students will **learn a serious lesson**. When they lose their phones for a long time, they will understand that using phones in class has **consequences**. They will **not repeat** this mistake again.
>
> **The second reason is that** without phones, students can **focus on studying** during class time. They will not be **distracted** by social media or games. **As a result**, their **academic performance** will improve.
>
> **I would not choose asking them to pay a fine** because most students don't have their **own income**. It is **unfair** to ask them for money. **As for making them stand in the corner**, this is too **harsh and humiliating**. It can hurt their **self-esteem** in front of their classmates.
>
> **In short**, keeping phones until the end of the semester is the best punishment because it is effective and fair.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 2]
**Bước 1 — Chọn:** *I would choose + lựa chọn*

**Bước 2 — Lý do 1:** *The first reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 3 — Lý do 2:** *The second reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 4 — Loại bỏ:** *I would not choose... because...* / *As for..., this is...*

**Bước 5 — Kết:** *In short, ...*

| Từ vựng quan trọng | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **learn a serious lesson** | rút ra bài học nghiêm túc |
| **consequences** | hậu quả |
| **focus on studying** | tập trung vào học |
| **unfair** | không công bằng |
| **harsh and humiliating** | khắc nghiệt và nhục nhã |
| **self-esteem** | lòng tự trọng |
:::

## Speaking Part 3: Topic Development (nói 2 phút)

> **Advantages of learning how to ride a bicycle.**

### Bài mẫu:

> Today, I would like to talk about **the advantages of learning how to ride a bicycle**. I think riding a bicycle is a very **useful skill** and it brings many benefits.
>
> **Firstly**, riding a bicycle is **good for health**. It is a type of **exercise** that helps people **stay fit** and **lose weight**. When you ride a bicycle every day, your heart and lungs become **stronger**. It is also good for **reducing stress** after a busy day at work or school.
>
> **Secondly**, riding a bicycle helps people **save money**. You don't need to buy **gasoline** or pay **parking fees** like when you use a motorbike or a car. **Moreover**, a bicycle is very **cheap to maintain**. You only need to check the tires and keep it clean.
>
> **Thirdly**, riding a bicycle is good for **the environment**. Bicycles do not produce **air pollution** or **noise pollution** like cars and motorbikes. **If more people** ride bicycles, our cities will be **cleaner and quieter**.
>
> **In conclusion**, learning to ride a bicycle is very important because it is good for our health, helps us save money, and **protects the environment**. I think everyone should learn this skill.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 3]
**Mở bài:** *Today, I would like to talk about...*

**Thân bài:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Thirdly, ...*

**Kết:** *In conclusion, ...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **be good for + N** | Tốt cho... | *Riding a bicycle is good for health* |
| **stay fit** | Giữ dáng | *It helps people stay fit* |
| **save money** | Tiết kiệm tiền | *Riding a bicycle helps save money* |
| **cheap to maintain** | Rẻ để bảo dưỡng | *A bicycle is very cheap to maintain* |
| **If more people + V, ... will + V** | Nếu nhiều người hơn..., ... sẽ... | *If more people ride bicycles, our cities will be cleaner* |
| **protect the environment** | Bảo vệ môi trường | *Riding a bicycle protects the environment* |
:::

## Writing Task 1: Formal Letter

> You received an email from an online shop asking about your negative comments. Write a reply: **what products** did you order, **when** did you order, **what made you unhappy**, and **what they can do** to solve it.

### Bài mẫu:

> Dear Sir/Madam,
>
> **I am writing in response to** your email regarding my negative review on your website. I would like to explain the **reasons for my complaint**.
>
> I ordered a pair of **running shoes** from your online store **on March 10th**, and the order number is #56789. The shoes were supposed to arrive **within 3 days**, but I only received them **after 8 days**, which was very **late**.
>
> **Moreover**, when I opened the package, I was very **disappointed**. The shoes I received were a **different color** from what I ordered. I ordered **white shoes**, but I received **grey ones**. **In addition**, the right shoe had a small **damage** on the side, and the **size was wrong**. I ordered size 39, but the shoes were size 40.
>
> I **would like to request** a **full refund** for this order. **Alternatively**, you can send me a **replacement** — the correct shoes in the right color and size. I would also appreciate it if you could improve your **delivery service** so that this problem does not happen again.
>
> I **look forward to** your **prompt response**.
>
> **Yours faithfully,**
> Tran Van Hung

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Thư khiếu nại (Formal)]
**Mở thư:** *Dear Sir/Madam, I am writing in response to your email regarding...*

**Thân thư:** Nêu sản phẩm + thời gian → vấn đề gặp phải → yêu cầu giải quyết

**Kết thư:** *I look forward to your prompt response. Yours faithfully, + tên*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **I am writing in response to + N** | Tôi viết thư để phản hồi về... |
| **I was very disappointed** | Tôi rất thất vọng |
| **a different color from + N** | Màu khác với... |
| **I would like to request + N** | Tôi muốn yêu cầu... |
| **full refund** | Hoàn tiền toàn bộ |
| **replacement** | Sản phẩm thay thế |
| **prompt response** | Phản hồi nhanh chóng |

**Lưu ý:** Thư khiếu nại dùng giọng **lịch sự nhưng rõ ràng**. Nêu cụ thể vấn đề và yêu cầu giải quyết rõ ràng. Đếm đủ **120+ từ**.
:::

## Writing Task 2: Essay — Discuss both views

> Some people say universities should focus on **soft skills**, while others think **technical knowledge** is more important. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

### Bài mẫu:

> **Nowadays**, there is a debate about what universities should focus on. **Some people argue that** soft skills are more important, **while others believe that** technical knowledge should be the priority. **In this essay**, I will discuss both views and give my opinion.
>
> **On the one hand**, technical knowledge is very **important** for students. It is the **foundation** for every career. **For example**, a doctor needs to learn about medicine, and an engineer needs to understand mathematics and physics. Without **professional knowledge**, students cannot do their jobs well after graduation. **Therefore**, many people think universities should focus on teaching technical skills.
>
> **On the other hand**, soft skills are also **essential** in today's world. Skills like **communication**, **teamwork**, and **problem-solving** are needed in every job. **For instance**, even a good engineer needs to know how to **work in a team** and **present ideas** clearly. **Moreover**, many employers today look for people who have both knowledge and soft skills. A person with **good communication skills** can build better relationships with colleagues and clients.
>
> **In my opinion**, I believe that universities should teach **both** soft skills and technical knowledge. They are **equally important** and **complement each other**. Students who have both will be more **competitive** in the job market.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Discuss Both Views]
**Mở bài:** *Some people argue that... while others believe that... In this essay, I will discuss both views and give my opinion.*

**Thân bài 1:** *On the one hand, ... For example, ... Therefore, ...*

**Thân bài 2:** *On the other hand, ... For instance, ... Moreover, ...*

**Kết luận:** *In my opinion, I believe that...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **On the one hand, ... On the other hand, ...** | Một mặt... Mặt khác... |
| **foundation** for + N | Nền tảng cho... |
| **For example, ... / For instance, ...** | Ví dụ... |
| **equally important** | Quan trọng như nhau |
| **complement each other** | Bổ trợ lẫn nhau |
| **competitive in the job market** | Cạnh tranh trên thị trường việc làm |

**Lưu ý:** Bài luận cần **ít nhất 150 từ**. Dạng Discuss both views cần trình bày **công bằng** cả 2 phía, sau đó nêu ý kiến cá nhân ở kết luận.
:::
