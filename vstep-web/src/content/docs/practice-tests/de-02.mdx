## Speaking Part 1: Interview

### Topic A — Travelling

**1. Have you ever flown abroad?**

> Yes, I **have flown abroad** once. I went to **Thailand** last summer with my family. It was my **first time** on a plane, and I felt very excited. The trip was short, but I had a **wonderful experience**.

**2. When you travel, which type of vehicle do you prefer?**

> I **prefer travelling by plane** because it is **fast and comfortable**. For example, flying from Hanoi to Ho Chi Minh City takes only about 2 hours. **Moreover**, I can relax and enjoy the view from the sky.

**3. How do you prefer to travel on long journeys?**

> For long journeys, I prefer to **travel by plane** because it **saves a lot of time**. If the place is not too far, I also enjoy going by **train** because I can see **beautiful scenery** along the way. **However**, I don't like travelling by bus because it makes me feel **carsick**.

**4. What kind of places have you visited in your life?**

> I have visited many **beautiful places** in Vietnam such as **Da Lat**, **Ha Long Bay**, and **Nha Trang**. I also went to Thailand once. I enjoy visiting **beaches** and **mountains** the most because they help me **relax** and **forget about** my daily stress.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **have + V3 (past participle)** | Đã từng... (hiện tại hoàn thành) | *I have flown abroad once* |
| **prefer + Ving** | Thích... hơn | *I prefer travelling by plane* |
| **Moreover, ...** | Hơn nữa... | *Moreover, I can relax and enjoy the view* |
| **save a lot of time** | Tiết kiệm nhiều thời gian | *It saves a lot of time* |
| **forget about + N** | Quên đi... | *Forget about my daily stress* |
:::

### Topic B — Music

**1. Do you like listening to music?**

> Yes, I **love listening to music**. I listen to music **every day**, especially when I study or before I go to bed. Music helps me **feel relaxed** and happy.

**2. What types of music do you like?**

> I like **pop music** the most because it has a **nice melody** and the lyrics are easy to understand. I also enjoy **ballads** because they make me **feel emotional**. My favorite singer is **Son Tung MTP**.

**3. Did you learn to play a musical instrument when you were a child?**

> Yes, I learned to play **guitar** when I was about 12 years old. My father **taught me** how to play some simple songs. **However**, I didn't practice enough, so I am **not very good at it** now. I **wish** I had practiced more.

**4. How do you feel when you listen to this music?**

> When I listen to music, I feel very **relaxed and peaceful**. If I am sad, music **cheers me up**. If I am stressed after a long day of studying, music helps me **calm down**. I think music is like **a good friend** to me.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **love + Ving** | Rất thích làm gì | *I love listening to music* |
| **make sb feel + adj** | Khiến ai cảm thấy... | *They make me feel emotional* |
| **be good at + N/Ving** | Giỏi về... | *I am not very good at it* |
| **wish + S + had V3** | Ước gì đã... (tiếc nuối) | *I wish I had practiced more* |
| **cheer sb up** | Làm ai vui lên | *Music cheers me up* |
:::

## Speaking Part 2: Discussion

> Your English friend wants to improve English skills. Where should they take a class: **learning online**, **joining a class at a language center**, or **joining an English club**?

### Bài mẫu:

> **I would recommend joining a class at a language center.** I think this is the **best way** for my friend to improve his English.
>
> **The first reason is that** there is a **professional teacher** who can help my friend directly. When my friend makes a mistake, the teacher can **correct it right away**. He can also **ask questions** whenever he does not understand something.
>
> **The second reason is that** a language center has a **fixed schedule**. This helps my friend **study regularly** and not give up easily. He will also meet other students and **practice speaking** with them in class.
>
> **I would not choose learning online** because it is easy to get **distracted**. My friend might watch videos or check social media instead of studying. **As for joining an English club**, it is fun but there are no **structured lessons**. He can only chat with people, but he cannot learn grammar or writing skills.
>
> **In short**, joining a class at a language center is the best choice because my friend will have a good teacher and a clear study plan.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 2]
**Bước 1 — Chọn:** *I would recommend + lựa chọn*

**Bước 2 — Lý do 1:** *The first reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 3 — Lý do 2:** *The second reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 4 — Loại bỏ:** *I would not choose... because...* / *As for..., it is...*

**Bước 5 — Kết:** *In short, ...*

| Từ vựng quan trọng | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **correct mistakes** | Sửa lỗi sai |
| **fixed schedule** | Lịch học cố định |
| **get distracted** | Bị mất tập trung |
| **structured lessons** | Bài học có hệ thống |
:::

## Speaking Part 3: Topic Development (nói 2 phút)

> **People tend to choose planes for holidays:** convenient, fast, and good service.

### Bài mẫu:

> Today, I would like to talk about **why people tend to choose planes for holidays**. I agree with this idea, and I have three reasons.
>
> **Firstly**, flying is very **convenient**. Nowadays, people can **book tickets online** easily with just a few clicks. There are also many **low-cost airlines** such as VietJet and AirAsia, so flying is not expensive anymore. **In addition**, airports have many facilities like restaurants, shops, and free wifi, which make **waiting time** more comfortable.
>
> **Secondly**, planes are much **faster** than other vehicles. **For example**, a flight from Hanoi to Ho Chi Minh City **takes only 2 hours**, while going by train takes about 30 hours. This means people can **save time** and have more time to enjoy their holiday. **Especially** for international trips, planes are the only practical choice.
>
> **Thirdly**, airlines provide **good service** during the flight. Passengers can enjoy **free meals and drinks**. The seats are comfortable, and there are **entertainment programs** such as movies and music. Flight attendants are also very **friendly and helpful**.
>
> **In conclusion**, I believe travelling by plane is the **best choice** for holidays because it is convenient, fast, and offers excellent service.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 3]
**Mở bài:** *Today, I would like to talk about... I agree with this idea.*

**Thân bài:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Thirdly, ...*

**Kết:** *In conclusion, I believe...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **tend to + V** | Có xu hướng... | *People tend to choose planes* |
| **It takes + time + to V** | Mất bao lâu để... | *It takes only 2 hours to fly* |
| **Compared to + N** | So với... | *Compared to trains, planes are faster* |
| **In addition, ...** | Ngoài ra... | *In addition, airports have many facilities* |
| **Especially for + N** | Đặc biệt đối với... | *Especially for international trips* |
| **book tickets online** | Đặt vé trực tuyến | *People can book tickets online easily* |
:::

## Writing Task 1: Informal Letter

> Your sibling lives in the countryside and wrote to ask you about methods to improve their English score. Write a letter to tell them how to improve listening skills and how to calm down during the exam.

### Bài mẫu:

> Dear Minh,
>
> **Thank you for your letter!** I'm so happy to hear from you. You asked me about how to improve your English, so **let me give you** some useful tips.
>
> **First**, to improve your **listening skills**, you should **listen to English every day**. You can listen to **English songs** and try to understand the lyrics. You should also watch **English movies with subtitles** — this helps you get used to the speed of English speaking. **Another good way** is to listen to **BBC Learning English** or simple podcasts. When you listen, try to focus on the **keywords** — you don't need to understand every single word.
>
> **Second**, about how to **stay calm during the exam**, here are my tips. The night before the exam, you should **sleep early** and eat a good breakfast in the morning. When you enter the exam room, **take a deep breath** and tell yourself "I can do it." **Before the listening part starts**, read the questions carefully so you know **what to listen for**. This will make you feel **more confident**. If you miss an answer, **don't worry** — just move on to the next question.
>
> I **believe you can do it**! Study hard and **stay positive**. Write to me if you need more help.
>
> **Love,**
> Lan

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Thư thân mật]
**Mở thư:** *Dear + tên, Thank you for your letter! / I'm happy to hear from you.*

**Thân thư:** Chia 2 phần rõ ràng: cải thiện Listening + bình tĩnh khi thi

**Kết thư:** *I believe you can do it! / Write to me if you need more help. Love, + tên*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **Let me give you some tips** | Để tôi cho bạn vài mẹo |
| **get used to + N/Ving** | Quen với... |
| **Another good way is to + V** | Một cách tốt khác là... |
| **take a deep breath** | Hít thở sâu |
| **stay calm / stay positive** | Giữ bình tĩnh / giữ tinh thần tích cực |
| **Don't worry** | Đừng lo lắng |

**Lưu ý:** Thư thân mật dùng **short forms** (I'm, don't, you're) và giọng văn **vui vẻ, động viên**. Đếm đủ **120+ từ**.
:::

## Writing Task 2: Essay — Problems & Solutions

> The world is experiencing a dramatic increase in population. Describe the problems of overpopulation and suggest solutions.

### Bài mẫu:

> **Nowadays**, the world's population is **increasing rapidly**, and this is causing many serious problems. **In this essay**, I will describe the main problems of overpopulation and **suggest some solutions**.
>
> **Firstly**, overpopulation leads to a **shortage of resources**. When there are too many people, there is **not enough** food, clean water, and housing for everyone. This is especially serious in **poor countries** where many people live in **poverty** and **hunger**. Children cannot go to school, and families do not have enough money to buy food.
>
> **Secondly**, more people means more **pollution**. People need more cars, more factories, and more houses. This produces a lot of **waste and emissions**, which **damages the environment**. **Moreover**, people cut down **forests** to build houses and farms, which destroys the **ecosystem** and makes **climate change** worse.
>
> **To solve these problems**, I think the government should **educate people** about **family planning**. When people understand the importance of having fewer children, the population will grow more slowly. **In addition**, the government should **create more jobs** in rural areas so that people do not have to move to big cities. **Finally**, investing in **technology** can help produce more food and use resources more **efficiently**.
>
> **In conclusion**, overpopulation causes many problems such as resource shortage and environmental pollution. **However**, with proper education and good policies, we can **find effective solutions** to this problem.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Problems & Solutions]
**Mở bài:** *The world's population is increasing rapidly. In this essay, I will describe the problems and suggest solutions.*

**Thân bài 1 — Vấn đề:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ...*

**Thân bài 2 — Giải pháp:** *To solve these problems, ... In addition, ... Finally, ...*

**Kết luận:** *In conclusion, ... However, with + N, we can...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **lead to + N** | Dẫn đến... |
| **shortage of + N** | Thiếu hụt... |
| **damage the environment** | Gây hại cho môi trường |
| **To solve these problems, ...** | Để giải quyết những vấn đề này... |
| **family planning** | Kế hoạch hoá gia đình |
| **In addition, ...** | Ngoài ra... |
| **find effective solutions** | Tìm ra giải pháp hiệu quả |
| **Moreover, ...** | Hơn nữa... |

**Lưu ý:** Bài luận cần **ít nhất 150 từ**. Chia rõ phần **problems** và phần **solutions**. Dùng **linking words** ở đầu mỗi câu để bài mạch lạc.
:::
