## Speaking Part 1: Interview

### Topic A — Uniform

**1. Have you ever worn a uniform?**

> Yes, I **used to wear** a uniform when I was in **high school**. It was a white shirt and dark blue trousers. I wore it every day for three years, and I think it looked **neat and tidy**.

**2. Are you willing to wear a uniform at work?**

> Yes, I am **willing to** wear a uniform at work. I think wearing a uniform is **convenient** because I don't need to think about what to wear every morning. **Moreover**, it helps me look more **professional**.

**3. Why should students wear uniforms?**

> I think students should wear uniforms because uniforms create **equality** among students. When everyone wears the same clothes, **no one feels** bad about being rich or poor. **In addition**, uniforms help students **focus on** their studies instead of fashion.

**4. On what occasion should people wear uniforms?**

> People should wear uniforms on **formal occasions** such as going to work, attending school, or joining **official events**. **For example**, doctors wear white coats at hospitals, and soldiers wear uniforms at **military ceremonies**. It shows that they are **professional**.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **used to + V** | Đã từng làm gì (quá khứ) | *I used to wear a uniform in high school* |
| **be willing to + V** | Sẵn lòng làm gì | *I am willing to wear a uniform at work* |
| **Moreover, ...** | Hơn nữa... | *Moreover, it helps me look more professional* |
| **In addition, ...** | Ngoài ra... | *In addition, uniforms help students focus* |
| **For example, ...** | Ví dụ... | *For example, doctors wear white coats* |
:::

### Topic B — Parental Roles

**1. Who are you closest to in your family?**

> The person I am **closest to** in my family is my **mother**. She always **takes care of** me and listens to my problems. Whenever I feel sad, she **comforts me** and gives me good advice.

**2. Do you get along well with your family?**

> Yes, I **get along well** with my family. We often **spend time together** on weekends. We cook dinner, watch TV, and **talk about** our day. I think we have a **close relationship**.

**3. Are your parents your role model in life?**

> Yes, my parents are definitely my **role models**. My father works very hard to **support our family**, and my mother is always **patient and kind**. I want to be like them when I grow up. **They have taught me** many important things in life.

**4. Should parents monitor what their young children do online?**

> Yes, I think parents **should monitor** their children's online activities. The internet has a lot of **harmful content** that is not good for young children. **If parents don't pay attention**, children may visit **dangerous websites** or talk to strangers. **Therefore**, parents need to **protect** their children online.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng]
| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ trong bài |
|---|---|---|
| **be closest to + người** | Thân thiết nhất với... | *The person I am closest to is my mother* |
| **get along well with + người** | Hoà thuận với... | *I get along well with my family* |
| **spend time together** | Dành thời gian cùng nhau | *We spend time together on weekends* |
| **role model** | Hình mẫu, tấm gương | *My parents are my role models* |
| **Therefore, ...** | Vì vậy... | *Therefore, parents need to protect their children* |
:::

## Speaking Part 2: Discussion

> You have found out that your younger brother has a girlfriend. What would you do? Options: **don't intervene**, **deal with it by yourself**, or **tell your parents**.

### Bài mẫu:

> **I would choose to deal with it by myself.** I think this is the **best way** to help my younger brother.
>
> **The first reason is that** my brother would **trust me** more than our parents. If I talk to him **gently**, he will listen to me and **share his feelings**. I can give him **useful advice** about how to balance his study and his relationship. This is very important for a young student.
>
> **The second reason is that** I am his older sibling, so I **understand** him well. I was also young once, so I know how he feels. I can help him **make good decisions** without putting too much pressure on him.
>
> **I would not choose to tell our parents** because they might **overreact** and punish my brother. Then he would **lose trust** in the family and not share anything anymore. **As for not intervening**, it is also not a good choice because my brother is still young. He needs someone to **guide him** so that his studies are not affected.
>
> **In short**, I will talk to my brother myself because this is the most caring and effective way.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 2]
**Bước 1 — Chọn:** *I would choose + lựa chọn*

**Bước 2 — Lý do 1:** *The first reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 3 — Lý do 2:** *The second reason is that + S + V*

**Bước 4 — Loại bỏ:** *I would not choose... because...* / *As for..., it is...*

**Bước 5 — Kết:** *In short, ...*

| Từ vựng quan trọng | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **deal with** | giải quyết, xử lý |
| **overreact** | phản ứng thái quá |
| **lose trust** | mất lòng tin |
| **guide sb** | hướng dẫn ai |
:::

## Speaking Part 3: Topic Development (nói 2 phút)

> **Keeping a pet teaches children many interesting things:** love for animals, learn responsibility, care for others, learn about biological features.

### Bài mẫu:

> Today, I would like to talk about **why keeping a pet teaches children many interesting things**. I strongly agree with this idea, and I have several reasons.
>
> **Firstly**, keeping a pet helps children develop **love for animals**. When children feed and play with their pets every day, they learn to **be kind** to animals. They understand that animals also have feelings and need love. This helps children become more **caring people**.
>
> **Secondly**, having a pet teaches children **responsibility**. Children have to feed the pet, give it water, and clean its place **every day**. If they forget, the pet will be hungry or sick. **Thanks to** this, children learn that they must **take care of** someone who depends on them.
>
> **Thirdly**, caring for a pet helps children learn to **care for others**. When the pet is sick, children feel worried and want to help. This feeling of **empathy** can also be used with their friends and family in real life.
>
> **Finally**, children can learn about **biological features** by watching their pets. They can see how animals eat, sleep, and grow. This is a **natural way** to learn about science and nature.
>
> **In conclusion**, I believe that keeping a pet is a **wonderful way** for children to learn many important life lessons.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Part 3]
**Mở bài:** *Today, I would like to talk about... I strongly agree with this idea.*

**Thân bài:** *Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Thirdly, ... Finally, ...*

**Kết:** *In conclusion, I believe...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **Thanks to + N/Ving** | Nhờ có... | *Thanks to this, children learn responsibility* |
| **take care of + N** | Chăm sóc... | *They must take care of someone who depends on them* |
| **It teaches children to + V** | Nó dạy trẻ em... | *It teaches children to be kind* |
| **learn about + N** | Học về... | *Children learn about biological features* |
| **a wonderful way to + V** | Cách tuyệt vời để... | *A wonderful way for children to learn* |
| **develop + N** | Phát triển... | *Develop love for animals* |
:::

## Writing Task 1: Informal Letter

> Your foreign friend is going to Nha Trang. Write a letter about: how to travel there, what to do, and why you recommend Nha Trang.

### Bài mẫu:

> Dear Tom,
>
> **Thank you for your letter!** I'm so glad to hear that you are coming to Vietnam. You asked me about Nha Trang, so **let me tell you** about this wonderful city.
>
> **First of all**, the best way to **get to** Nha Trang is **by plane**. You can fly from Ho Chi Minh City to **Cam Ranh Airport**, and it only takes about **one hour**. From the airport, you can take a taxi to the city center. It is about **30 minutes** and very easy.
>
> **When you are in Nha Trang**, there are many things to do. You should **go swimming** at the beautiful beach. The water is very **clean and blue**. I also **recommend visiting** Vinpearl Land — it is a big amusement park on an island. **Don't forget to try** the local **seafood** — it is very **fresh and cheap**!
>
> **The reason I recommend** Nha Trang is that it has **beautiful beaches**, **warm weather** all year, and the people are very **friendly**. **Moreover**, it is **not expensive**, so you can enjoy many things without spending too much money.
>
> I'm sure you will **have a great time** there! **Write back soon** and tell me your travel plan.
>
> **Love,**
> Minh

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Thư thân mật]
**Mở thư:** *Dear + tên, Thank you for your letter! / I'm glad to hear...*

**Thân thư:** Trả lời theo 3 ý: cách đi → hoạt động → lý do chọn

**Kết thư:** *I'm sure you will... / Write back soon. Love, + tên*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **Let me tell you about + N** | Để tôi kể cho bạn về... |
| **The best way to get to... is by + N** | Cách tốt nhất để đến... là bằng... |
| **I recommend + Ving** | Tôi khuyên nên... |
| **Don't forget to try + N** | Đừng quên thử... |
| **have a great time** | Có khoảng thời gian vui vẻ |

**Lưu ý:** Thư thân mật được dùng **short forms** (I'm, It's, don't) và giọng văn **vui vẻ, gần gũi**. Đếm đủ **120+ từ**.
:::

## Writing Task 2: Essay — Give your opinion

> Some people say computers have made life easier and more convenient. Others say computers have made life more complex and stressful. Give your opinion.

### Bài mẫu:

> **Nowadays**, computers are used in almost every part of our lives. **Some people believe** that computers make life easier, **while others think** they make life more stressful. **In this essay**, I will discuss both views and give my opinion.
>
> **On the one hand**, computers have made life much **more convenient**. **Firstly**, we can use computers to **communicate** with people around the world through email and video calls. We don't need to travel far just to talk to someone. **Secondly**, computers help us **find information** very quickly. **For example**, students can use Google to search for anything in just a few seconds. **Moreover**, we can do **online shopping** from home, which **saves a lot of time**.
>
> **On the other hand**, some people think computers make life **more complex and stressful**. They have to learn new **software** and technology all the time, which is **difficult** for older people. **In addition**, many people spend too much time on social media, which **reduces their productivity**. Sitting in front of a computer for many hours also causes **health problems** like eye strain and back pain.
>
> **In my opinion**, I believe that computers have **more advantages than disadvantages**. Computers have made our lives much easier, but we need to **use them wisely**. If we **control our screen time** and use computers for useful purposes, they will **continue to benefit** our lives.

:::note[Cấu trúc đã dùng — Công thức Give Opinion]
**Mở bài:** *Some people believe that... while others think that... In this essay, I will discuss both views and give my opinion.*

**Thân bài 1:** *On the one hand, ... Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Moreover, ...*

**Thân bài 2:** *On the other hand, ... In addition, ...*

**Kết luận:** *In my opinion, I believe that...*

| Cấu trúc | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **On the one hand, ... On the other hand, ...** | Một mặt... Mặt khác... |
| **save a lot of time** | Tiết kiệm nhiều thời gian |
| **reduce productivity** | Giảm năng suất |
| **use sth wisely** | Sử dụng cái gì khôn ngoan |
| **more advantages than disadvantages** | Nhiều lợi ích hơn bất lợi |
| **For example, ...** | Ví dụ... |
| **In addition, ...** | Ngoài ra... |
| **control screen time** | Kiểm soát thời gian dùng màn hình |

**Lưu ý:** Bài luận cần **ít nhất 150 từ**. Dùng **linking words** ở đầu mỗi câu để bài mạch lạc. **Không dùng "I think" quá nhiều** — thay bằng "I believe", "In my opinion".
:::
