Thư informal viết cho **bạn bè, người thân, người quen**. **Phổ biến hơn thư formal trong đề thi thật.**

:::tip[Cấu trúc chung thư informal]
**Dear [Tên],** hoặc **Hi [Tên],**

Thân thư: **2–3 đoạn**, mỗi đoạn 1 ý chính

**Best wishes, / Take care, / Lots of love,**
**[Tên bạn]**
:::

---

## Scenarios hay gặp trong đề thật

| Độ phổ biến | Tình huống | Mục đích thư |
|-------------|------------|-------------|
| Rất phổ biến | **Kể về chuyến du lịch / kỳ nghỉ** | Chia sẻ trải nghiệm |
| Rất phổ biến | **Cho lời khuyên** | Gợi ý, đề xuất giải pháp |
| Phổ biến | **Mời bạn tham dự sự kiện** | Mô tả sự kiện, khuyến khích đến |
| Phổ biến | **Kể về sự kiện đặc biệt** (sinh nhật, lễ) | Mô tả và chia sẻ cảm xúc |
| Ít gặp | Chúc mừng bạn | Thể hiện sự vui mừng |
| Ít gặp | Xin lỗi và giải thích | Thừa nhận lỗi, hứa cải thiện |

## Template đầy đủ

<TemplateBox title="Template thư Informal — Cấu trúc chung">

**Mở đầu thân thiện:**
- ***How are you? I hope you're doing well.***
- ***I'm so excited to tell you about...***
- *It was great to hear from you!*

**Triển khai ý 1:**
- ***First of all, I'd like to tell you that...***
- ***The best part was...***
- *I really enjoyed... because...*

**Triển khai ý 2:**
- ***Another thing I'd like to share is...***
- *What's more,...*

**Kết thư:**
- ***I hope to hear from you soon.***
- ***Write back when you get a chance!***
- *Please give my regards to your family.*

</TemplateBox>

## Ví dụ bài mẫu B1

**Đề bài:** *Write a letter to your friend telling them about a trip you recently took. Mention: where you went, what you did, and what you enjoyed most.*

> "Dear Minh,
>
> **How are you?** I hope everything is going well with you! **I'm so excited to tell you about** my trip to Hoi An last weekend.
>
> My family and I went there by train, **which** took about three hours from Da Nang. When we arrived, we visited the Ancient Town and walked along the famous Hoi An streets decorated with colourful lanterns. We also tried many local dishes, including white rose dumplings and cao lau noodles, **which** were absolutely delicious.
>
> **The thing I enjoyed most** about the trip was the atmosphere — it was so peaceful and charming. In the evening, we released paper lanterns on the river, which was a truly magical experience.
>
> I think you would love Hoi An — we should plan a trip together sometime! **I look forward to hearing** about your news too.
>
> Best wishes,
> Lan"

## Cụm từ hữu ích theo tình huống

### Cho lời khuyên
- ***If I were you, I would...***
- ***I think you should / could try...***
- *Have you considered...?*
- *Why don't you...?*

### Mời tham dự
- ***I'd love it if you could come to...***
- ***It would be great to see you at...***
- *Please let me know if you can make it.*

### Chúc mừng
- ***Congratulations on your...***
- *I'm so proud of you for...*
- *What wonderful news! I'm so happy for you.*

### Xin lỗi
- ***I'm really sorry for...***
- *I feel terrible about...*
- *I promise it won't happen again.*

### Dùng từ thân mật hơn
- *amazing / fantastic / awesome* (thay vì *good/nice*)
- *I'm over the moon about...* (rất vui mừng)
- *It blew my mind!* (thật ấn tượng)

### Dùng câu hỏi để tạo kết nối
- *Don't you think that...?*
- *Can you believe it?*

### Kết thư ấn tượng hơn
- *I miss you loads and can't wait to see you!*
- *Drop me a line when you get a chance!*
