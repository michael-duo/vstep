Thư formal dùng khi viết cho: **công ty, khách sạn, trường học, tổ chức**. Ngôn ngữ **lịch sự, trang trọng**, không dùng từ viết tắt.

:::tip[Cấu trúc chung mọi thư formal]
**Dear Sir/Madam,** (khi không biết tên) hoặc **Dear Mr./Ms. [Tên],**

Thân thư: chia thành **2–3 đoạn** rõ ràng

**Yours faithfully,** (khi dùng Dear Sir/Madam) hoặc **Yours sincerely,** (khi dùng tên)
:::

---

## ★★★ Dạng 1: Letter of Request (Thư yêu cầu) — Phổ biến nhất

**Rất hay gặp trong đề thi.** Có 2 loại: yêu cầu thông tin và yêu cầu hàng hóa/dịch vụ.

<TemplateBox title="Template — Letter of Request">

**Opening:**
- ***I am writing to ask for further information about...***
- ***I am writing because I am interested in...***
- *With reference to your advertisement, I would like to enquire about...*

**Making requests:**
- ***I would be grateful if you could...***
- ***Could you please send me / provide me with...?***
- *Would it be possible for you to...?*

**Second request (if needed):**
- ***I would also like to know / ask about...***
- *Another matter I need information on is...*

**Closing:**
- ***I look forward to hearing from you at your earliest convenience.***
- *Please do not hesitate to contact me if you need further information.*

</TemplateBox>

**Ví dụ mở đầu:**
> "Dear Sir/Madam,
> **I am writing to ask for further information about** the English language course advertised on your website. **I would be grateful if you could** provide me with details about the course fees and the schedule. **I would also like to know** whether accommodation is available for students from other cities.
> **I look forward to hearing from you** at your earliest convenience.
> Yours faithfully,
> [Your name]"

## ★★★ Dạng 2: Letter of Complaint (Thư khiếu nại) — Rất phổ biến

**Rất hay gặp trong đề thi.**

<TemplateBox title="Template — Letter of Complaint">

**Opening:**
- ***I am writing to complain about...***
- ***I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with...***

**Describing the problem** (dùng quá khứ đơn):
- ***On [date], I purchased / visited / received...***
- ***However, I was disappointed to find that...***
- *The problem is that...*

**Requesting action:**
- ***I would appreciate it if you could...***
- ***I must ask you to either replace... or refund...***
- *I demand that you refund my money immediately.*

**Warning (optional):**
- ***If I do not hear from you within [time], I will have to take further action.***

**Closing:**
- ***I look forward to your prompt response.***

</TemplateBox>

## ★★ Dạng 3: Letter of Giving Information (Thư cung cấp thông tin)

<TemplateBox title="Template — Letter of Giving Information">

**Opening — Báo tin tốt:**
- ***I am writing to inform you that... / I feel delighted to inform you...***

**Opening — Báo tin xấu:**
- ***We regret to inform you that...***

**Opening — Phản hồi:**
- ***I am writing in response to your letter requesting information about...***

**Giving information:**
- ***Firstly, I would like to inform you that...***
- ***I would also like to let you know that...***
- *You might also find it useful to know that...*

**Closing:**
- ***I hope that I have answered all of your questions.***
- ***Please do not hesitate to contact me if you need further assistance.***

</TemplateBox>

### ★ Dạng 4: Letter of Giving Opinion (Thư đưa ra ý kiến/lời khuyên)

**Opening:**
- ***I am writing in response to your letter asking for advice about...***

**Giving advice:**
- ***I strongly recommend that you...***
- ***I would advise you to...***
- *May I suggest that...?*

**Closing:**
- *I hope that these suggestions will be of some help.*
- *Please do not hesitate to contact me should you have any problems.*

---

### ★ Dạng 5: Letter of Apology (Thư xin lỗi)

**Opening:**
- ***I am writing to apologise for... / I must apologize for...***
- ***I am extremely sorry for...***

**Explanation:**
- ***This was due to... / This happened because...***

**Offering solution:**
- *I am pleased to inform you that we are prepared to replace...*

**Closing:**
- *Please accept our apologies for...*

---

### ★ Dạng 6: Letter of Application (Thư xin việc/xin học bổng)

**Opening:**
- ***I am writing to apply for the position of... advertised in...***

**Qualifications:**
- ***I feel that my qualifications match your requirements...***
- *I have [X] years of experience in...*

**Closing:**
- ***I am available for an interview at your convenience.***

</TierContent>
