## Khi nào viết thư Formal?

:::tip[Nhận diện trong 5 giây]
Đề bài có **công ty / khách sạn / trường học / tổ chức / quản lý** → Formal

Đề bài có **bạn bè / người thân / người quen** → [Thư Informal](/writing/task-1/informal-letter/)
:::

---

## 4 câu cốt lõi — thuộc lòng ngay

<TemplateBox title="Chỉ cần nhớ 4 thứ này — không được sai">

**1. Lời xưng hô:**
- Không biết tên: `Dear Sir/Madam,`
- Biết tên: `Dear Mr./Ms. [Tên],`

**2. Câu mở đầu thân thư:**
`I am writing to [ask for / complain about / inform you that / apply for]...`

**3. Câu yêu cầu / đề nghị:**
`I would be grateful if you could...`

**4. Câu kết + lời kết:**
- `I look forward to hearing from you.`
- Dùng *Dear Sir/Madam* → kết bằng `Yours faithfully,`
- Dùng tên người → kết bằng `Yours sincerely,`

</TemplateBox>

---

## 3 dạng hay gặp nhất — nhớ 3 câu mở này

| Dạng | Khi nào dùng | Câu mở |
|---|---|---|
| **Request** | Hỏi thông tin, yêu cầu hàng hóa/dịch vụ | *I am writing to ask for...* |
| **Complaint** | Phàn nàn, không hài lòng | *I am writing to complain about...* |
| **Giving Information** | Cung cấp thông tin, phản hồi | *I am writing to inform you that...* |

## Dạng 1: Letter of Request (Thư yêu cầu)

**Phổ biến nhất trong đề thi.** Có 2 loại — template gần giống nhau, chỉ khác phần yêu cầu.

### 1a — Yêu cầu hàng hóa / dịch vụ

<TemplateBox title="Template — Yêu cầu hàng hóa / dịch vụ">

**Mở đầu:**
- ***I am writing because I am interested in [sản phẩm/dịch vụ] at your company.***
- ***With reference to your advertisement, I would like to enquire about...***
- *I am writing on account of [vấn đề].*

**Yêu cầu chính:**
- ***I would be grateful if you could send me... as soon as possible.***
- ***Could you possibly send me [hàng hóa]?***
- ***I would like to order... from your company.***
- *Would it be possible for you to send/provide...?*
- *We would appreciate if you could... immediately.*

**Yêu cầu thêm:**
- ***I would also like to know whether...***
- *Another matter I would like to ask about is...*

**Kết — thể hiện sẵn sàng liên hệ:**
- ***Please do not hesitate to contact me if you need further information.***
- *If you have any questions, please give me a call at...*
- ***I look forward to hearing from you at your earliest convenience.***

</TemplateBox>

### 1b — Yêu cầu thông tin

<TemplateBox title="Template — Yêu cầu thông tin">

**Mở đầu:**
- ***With reference to your advertisement for [khóa học/chương trình], I am writing to ask for further information about...***
- ***I am writing to ask for further information about [chủ đề].***

**Yêu cầu thông tin:**
- ***Could you please explain...?***
- ***I would be very grateful if you could send me information / directions / instructions about...***
- *I would appreciate information about...*
- *Would it be possible for you to clarify...?*
- *I would like instruction about...*

**Hỏi thêm:**
- ***I would also like to know whether...***
- *Another matter I need information on is...*
- *I am not certain about... Could you clarify this?*

**Kết:**
- ***I look forward to receiving your reply.***
- ***I look forward to hearing from you at your earliest convenience.***

</TemplateBox>

## Dạng 2: Letter of Complaint (Thư khiếu nại)

**Rất hay gặp trong đề thi thật.**

<TemplateBox title="Template — Letter of Complaint">

**Mở đầu:**
- ***I am writing to complain about [sản phẩm/dịch vụ] which I [purchased / received] at your [shop/hotel] on [ngày].***
- ***I am writing to express my concern about...***
- *I am writing because I am not at all satisfied with the quality of...*

**Mô tả vấn đề** (dùng quá khứ đơn):
- *Making a complaint:* thì hiện tại đơn
- *Describing the matter:* thì quá khứ đơn

- ***On [ngày], I [purchased / visited / received]...***
- ***However, I was disappointed to find that...***
- *The problem is that...*

**Yêu cầu giải quyết:**
- ***I would be grateful if you could look into this matter.***
- ***I would appreciate either a replacement or a full refund.***
- *I demand that you refund my money immediately.*
- *I must ask you to either replace [sản phẩm] or refund the price I paid.*
- *I feel that at the very least you owe me an apology.*

**Cảnh báo:**
- ***If I do not hear from you within [thời gian], I will have to take further action.***
- *Unless I hear from you within ten days, I shall have to take legal advice on this matter.*
- *If this matter is not resolved, I intend to contact the relevant authorities.*

**Kết:**
- ***I look forward to your prompt response.***

</TemplateBox>

### Bài mẫu — Letter of Complaint

**Đề bài:** *Write a letter to a hotel manager to complain about problems during your stay. Mention: the problems you experienced and what action you expect.*

<SampleAnswer client:load title="Formal Letter of Complaint — B1" score="B1">

Dear Sir/Madam,

**I am writing to complain about** the service at your hotel during my stay from 15th to 17th March.

**Firstly,** the room was not clean **when** I arrived. The bathroom was dirty, **and** there were no clean towels. I told the front desk about this problem, **but** it was not fixed until the next morning.

**Secondly,** the Wi-Fi was very slow during my stay. I was travelling for work, **so** I could not do my job properly. **As a result,** I had to go to a café to use the Internet.

**I would be grateful if you could** look into these problems and give me a partial refund for the trouble. **If I do not hear from you within two weeks, I will have to take further action.**

**I look forward to your prompt response.**

Yours faithfully,
Nguyen Thi Mai

</SampleAnswer>

**Phân tích bài mẫu:**
- **4 đoạn** — mở / vấn đề 1 / vấn đề 2 / yêu cầu + cảnh báo + kết
- **In đậm** = câu template dùng trực tiếp từ bảng trên
- **Từ nối**: *Firstly, Secondly, but, so, As a result* — đa dạng
- **Số từ**: ~145 từ — trên mức tối thiểu 120 từ

## Dạng 3: Letter of Giving Information (Thư cung cấp thông tin)

<TemplateBox title="Template — Letter of Giving Information">

**Mở đầu — Báo tin tốt:**
- ***I am writing to inform you that / let you know that...***
- ***I feel delighted to inform you that...***
- *You will be pleased to hear that...*

**Mở đầu — Báo tin xấu:**
- ***We regret to inform you that...***
- *I am afraid it would not be possible to...*
- *I feel obliged to inform you that...*

**Mở đầu — Phản hồi thư:**
- ***I am writing in response to your letter requesting information about...***
- *I am writing to provide you with information about...*
- *I am writing to reply to your enquiry about...*

**Vấn đề đầu tiên:**
- ***Firstly, I would like to inform you that...***
- *The first thing I would like to tell you is...*
- *I would like to begin by informing you that...*

**Mở rộng thêm thông tin:**
- ***I would also like to let you know that...***
- ***I would also like to inform you that...***
- *You might also find the following information useful...*
- *Another piece of information that might be useful is...*

**Kết:**
- ***I hope that I have been able to answer all of your questions.***
- ***Please do not hesitate to contact me if you have any further questions.***
- *I look forward to being able to help you again in the future.*

</TemplateBox>

### Thư đưa ý kiến / lời khuyên (Letter of Giving Opinion)

<TemplateBox title="Template — Letter of Giving Opinion">

**Mở đầu:**
- ***I am writing in response to your letter asking for advice about...***
- *I hope the following suggestions will be of some help to you.*
- *I would like to give my views on...*

**Đưa lời khuyên trực tiếp:**
- ***I strongly recommend that you...***
- ***I would advise you to...***
- *I believe that the best course of action is...*
- *You ought to...*

**Gợi ý thêm:**
- ***I would suggest that...***
- *May I suggest that...?*
- *Another suggestion would be to...*
- *I would like to offer one or two suggestions concerning...*

**Đưa ra quan điểm:**
- *In my view / It seems to me that...*
- *I agree/disagree with... because...*
- *I would like to point out that...*

**Kết:**
- ***I hope that these suggestions will be of some assistance.***
- *I hope you will accept this advice.*
- *Please do not hesitate to contact me should you have any problems.*

</TemplateBox>

### Thư xin lỗi (Letter of Apology)

<TemplateBox title="Template — Letter of Apology">

**Mô tả sự việc (ngắn gọn):**
- ***I am afraid I [đã làm gì]...***
- ***I regret to say that...***
- *We are very sorry that you received a defective [sản phẩm].*
- *Thank you for your letter informing me that...*

**Xin lỗi:**
- ***I apologise for...***
- ***I am extremely sorry for...***
- *I must apologise for not [doing something].*

**Giải thích lý do:**
- ***This was due to...***
- ***This happened because...***
- *The reason for this has been that I forgot / misunderstood...*

**Hứa khắc phục:**
- ***I am pleased to inform you that we are prepared to replace your [sản phẩm] with a new one.***
- *I will ensure this does not happen again.*
- *I am coming to [địa điểm] next week, so I will [giải pháp].*

**Kết:**
- ***Please accept our sincere apologies for the inconvenience caused.***
- *We hope that this will restore your confidence in our service.*
- *We very much regret for any inconvenience this has caused you.*

</TemplateBox>

### Thư xin việc / học bổng (Letter of Application)

<TemplateBox title="Template — Letter of Application">

**Mở đầu:**
- ***With reference to your advertisement in [báo] on [ngày], I would like to apply for the post of [vị trí].***
- *I wish to apply for the post of... as I saw advertised in [báo] on [ngày].*
- *I am writing in reply to your advertisement in [báo] for the post of...*

**Trình bày trình độ:**
- ***I feel that my qualifications match your requirements.***
- ***I feel I am the right person for this post because my qualifications and experience are extremely appropriate.***
- *My qualifications include...*
- *I feel I would be suitable for this post because...*

**Trình bày kinh nghiệm:**
- ***I have been working as [vị trí] for the last [X] years.***
- *Having already worked as [vị trí] for [thời gian], I wish to extend my experience.*
- *I have had experience of [lĩnh vực].*
- *I have [X] years of experience in [lĩnh vực].*

**Lý do nộp đơn:**
- *One of the main reasons I am applying is that...*
- *I am applying because...*

**Đính kèm hồ sơ:**
- *Please find enclosed my curriculum vitae and two references as requested.*
- *I enclose my qualifications and the contact details of two referees.*

**Kết:**
- ***I am available for an interview at any time convenient to you.***
- *I could come for an interview at any time which would suit you.*
- *I hope my application will receive your favourable consideration.*

</TemplateBox>
