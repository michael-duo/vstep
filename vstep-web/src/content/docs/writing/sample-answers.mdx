## Task 1 — Bài mẫu thư Informal

**Đề bài:** *Write a letter to your friend telling them about a recent birthday celebration. Mention: who celebrated, what you did, and your favourite moment.*

<SampleAnswer client:load title="Task 1 — Informal Letter (Birthday)" score="B1">

Dear Linh,

How are you? I hope you're doing well! I'm writing to tell you about my sister's birthday party last weekend — it was such a wonderful occasion!

My sister turned 20 on Saturday, **so** our family organised a small surprise party at home. We decorated the living room with balloons and flowers, **and** my mother cooked all of her favourite dishes. **When** my sister walked in and saw everyone, she was completely surprised and started crying with happiness!

The moment I enjoyed most was **when** we all sang "Happy Birthday" together and she blew out the candles on her cake. She looked so happy, **and** it made all of us feel really emotional. After that, we shared stories and looked at old family photos, **which** was a lot of fun.

I really wish you could have been there! Let's plan to celebrate your birthday together soon. I can't wait to hear from you!

Best wishes,
Hoa

</SampleAnswer>

**Cấu trúc được dùng:**
- **Từ nối ghép câu**: *so, and, when*
- **Mệnh đề quan hệ**: *which was a lot of fun*
- **Mệnh đề thời gian**: *When my sister walked in...*

**Phân tích:**
- **Task Fulfillment**: Đề yêu cầu 3 ý (ai, làm gì, khoảnh khắc yêu thích) — bài viết **đủ cả 3**
- **Organization**: **4 đoạn** rõ ràng
- **Vocabulary**: *organised, decorated, surprised, emotional, occasion* — đa dạng
- **Grammar**: câu ghép với *when, so, and*; mệnh đề quan hệ *which*
- **Số từ**: khoảng **175 từ** — trên mức tối thiểu 120 từ

## Task 1 — Bài mẫu thư Formal

**Đề bài:** *Write a letter of complaint to a hotel manager about problems during your stay. Mention: the problems you experienced and what action you expect.*

<SampleAnswer client:load title="Task 1 — Formal Letter of Complaint" score="B1">

Dear Sir/Madam,

**I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with** the service I received during my stay at your hotel from 15th to 17th March.

First of all, the room I was given was not clean **when** I checked in. The bathroom **had not been cleaned** properly, **and** there were no fresh towels provided. **Despite** reporting this to the front desk, the problem was not resolved until the following morning, **which** caused significant inconvenience.

**Furthermore,** the Wi-Fi connection in the room was extremely poor throughout my stay. **As** I was travelling for business, this was a serious problem **as** I was unable to work effectively.

**I would appreciate it if you could** investigate these matters and provide a partial refund as compensation for the inconvenience I experienced.

**I look forward to your prompt response.**

Yours faithfully,
Nguyen Thi Mai

</SampleAnswer>

**Cấu trúc được dùng:**
- **Câu bị động**: *had not been cleaned, was not resolved*
- **Mệnh đề quan hệ**: *which caused significant inconvenience*
- **Từ nối**: *Furthermore, Despite, As*
- **Template complaint**: *I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with... / I would appreciate it if you could...*

**Phân tích:**
- **Task Fulfillment**: Đề yêu cầu 2 ý (vấn đề + hành động mong muốn) — bài **đủ cả 2**
- **Tone**: Lịch sự, trang trọng — *Dear Sir/Madam* + *Yours faithfully*
- **Organization**: **4 đoạn** rõ ràng
- **Vocabulary**: *dissatisfaction, inconvenience, compensation* — từ formal

## Task 2 — Bài mẫu Essay

**Đề bài:** *Some people think that university education is very important for success in life. Others believe that practical work experience is more valuable. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.*

*Suggested: job opportunities / personal development / real-world skills*

<SampleAnswer client:load title="Task 2 — Discuss Both Views Essay" score="B1">

**Nowadays, there is a growing debate about** whether university education or practical work experience is more important for success in life. **While** both have their own advantages, **I personally believe that** a combination of both is the most effective path.

**On the one hand,** university education provides students with many benefits. **First,** it opens up better job opportunities, **as** many employers require a degree **when** hiring candidates. **Moreover,** university life helps students develop important personal skills **such as** critical thinking, time management, and communication. **For example,** group projects and presentations prepare students for real working environments.

**On the other hand,** practical work experience is also highly valuable. People **who** start working early develop real-world skills **that** cannot always be learned in a classroom. **For instance,** working part-time or doing internships allows students to understand how businesses operate. **In addition,** work experience can sometimes lead directly to full-time employment.

**In conclusion,** both university education and work experience have their own merits. **However, I personally believe that** combining academic study with practical experience is the best approach, **as** it prepares young people for the challenges of the modern workplace.

</SampleAnswer>

**Cấu trúc được dùng:**
- **Từ nối**: *On the one hand / On the other hand / Moreover / In addition / However*
- **Mệnh đề quan hệ**: *People who start working... / skills that cannot be learned...*
- **Câu bị động**: *cannot always be learned in a classroom*
- **Câu điều kiện ngầm**: *as it prepares young people...*
- **Template Discuss Both Views**: đúng cấu trúc Intro → View A → View B → Conclusion

**Phân tích:**
- **Task Fulfillment**: Thảo luận **đủ 2 quan điểm** + ý kiến cá nhân **rõ ràng**
- **Organization**: Intro → Body 1 → Body 2 → Conclusion
- **Vocabulary**: *critical thinking, real-world skills, internships, merits*
- **Số từ**: khoảng **260 từ** — trên mức tối thiểu 250 từ

### Bài B1 vs B2 — Task 2

**B1 Introduction:**
> "Nowadays, there is a growing debate about whether university education or practical work experience is more important."

**B2 Introduction (nâng cao hơn):**
> "In an increasingly competitive job market, the question of whether formal academic qualifications or hands-on professional experience holds greater value for career success has sparked considerable debate."

**Điểm khác biệt:**
- B2 dùng từ học thuật phức tạp hơn: *proponents, contend, ultimately, foundations*
- B2 paraphrase đề hoàn toàn, không lặp từ trong đề
- B2 câu dài và phức tạp hơn — nhưng **B1 không cần đến mức này**
