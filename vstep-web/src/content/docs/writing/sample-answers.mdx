## Task 1 — Bài mẫu thư Informal

**Đề bài:** *Write a letter to your friend telling them about a recent birthday celebration. Mention: who celebrated, what you did, and your favourite moment.*

<SampleAnswer client:load title="Task 1 — Informal Letter (Birthday)" score="B1">

Dear Linh,

How are you? I hope you are doing well! I am writing to tell you about my sister's birthday party last weekend.

My sister turned 20 on Saturday, **so** our family had a small party at home. We put up balloons **and** my mother cooked her favourite dishes. **When** my sister came home and saw everyone, she was very surprised and happy!

The moment I enjoyed most was **when** we all sang "Happy Birthday" together. She looked so happy, **and** it made all of us feel really good. **After that,** we talked and looked at old family photos, **which** was a lot of fun.

I really wish you could have been there! Let me know if you want to meet up soon. I hope to hear from you!

Best wishes,
Hoa

</SampleAnswer>

**Kiến thức nền tảng được dùng:**
- **Từ nối**: *so, and, when, after that, which* — từ [cụm từ kết nối](/foundation/universal-phrases/#cụm-từ-kết-nối)
- **Template**: *How are you?... I am writing to tell you about...* — từ [thư không trang trọng](/writing/task-1/informal-letter/)

**Phân tích:**
- **Nội dung**: Đề yêu cầu 3 ý (ai, làm gì, khoảnh khắc yêu thích) — bài viết **đủ cả 3**
- **Bố cục**: **4 đoạn** rõ ràng — mở, 2 thân, kết
- **Từ vựng**: *surprised, enjoyed, put up* — đơn giản, đúng B1
- **Số từ**: khoảng **155 từ** — trên mức tối thiểu 120 từ

## Task 1 — Bài mẫu thư Formal

**Đề bài:** *Write a letter to a hotel manager to complain about problems during your stay. Mention: the problems you experienced and what action you expect.*

<SampleAnswer client:load title="Task 1 — Formal Letter of Complaint" score="B1">

Dear Sir/Madam,

**I am writing to complain about** the service at your hotel during my stay from 15th to 17th March.

**Firstly,** the room was not clean **when** I arrived. The bathroom was dirty, **and** there were no clean towels. I told the front desk about this problem, **but** it was not fixed until the next morning.

**Secondly,** the Wi-Fi was very slow during my stay. I was travelling for work, **so** I could not do my job properly. **As a result,** I had to go to a café to use the Internet.

**I would be grateful if you could** look into these problems and give me a partial refund for the trouble.

**I look forward to hearing from you.**

Yours faithfully,
Nguyen Thi Mai

</SampleAnswer>

**Kiến thức nền tảng được dùng:**
- **Từ nối**: *Firstly, Secondly, but, so, As a result* — từ [cụm từ kết nối](/foundation/universal-phrases/#cụm-từ-kết-nối)
- **Template complaint**: *I am writing to complain about... / I would be grateful if you could...* — từ [thư trang trọng](/writing/task-1/formal-letter/)

**Phân tích:**
- **Nội dung**: Đề yêu cầu 2 ý (vấn đề + hành động mong muốn) — bài **đủ cả 2**
- **Tone**: Lịch sự, trang trọng — *Dear Sir/Madam* + *Yours faithfully*
- **Bố cục**: **4 đoạn** — mở, 2 vấn đề, yêu cầu + kết
- **Từ vựng**: *complain, dirty, refund, trouble* — đơn giản, đúng B1
- **Số từ**: khoảng **140 từ** — trên mức tối thiểu 120 từ

## Task 2 — Bài mẫu Essay

**Đề bài:** *Some people think that university education is very important for success in life. Others believe that practical work experience is more valuable. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.*

*Suggested: job opportunities / personal development / real-world skills*

<SampleAnswer client:load title="Task 2 — Discuss Both Views Essay" score="B1">

**Nowadays,** there is a growing debate about whether university education or work experience is more important for success. **This essay will discuss both perspectives.**

**On the one hand, some people believe that** university education is very important. **Firstly,** it helps people **get more job opportunities** because many companies want workers with a degree. **For example,** jobs like doctors, teachers, and engineers all need a university degree. Without it, people cannot even apply for these positions. **Secondly,** university helps students **improve** important skills like communication and teamwork. **This is because** students often have to do group projects and presentations during their studies.

**On the other hand, others argue that** work experience is more valuable. **Firstly,** people who start working early can learn real skills that they cannot learn in a classroom. **For example,** working part-time helps students understand how a business works and how to deal with customers. **Secondly,** work experience can sometimes **lead to** a full-time job directly. **As a result,** they can **save time and money** compared to studying at university for many years.

**In conclusion,** both university education and work experience have their own merits. **However,** I personally believe that going to university first and then getting work experience is the best way **because** it gives people both knowledge and practical skills.

</SampleAnswer>

**Kiến thức nền tảng được dùng:**
- **Cấu trúc**: [Khung A — Discuss Both Views](/writing/task-2/3-khung/#khung-a--hai-mặt)
- **Từ nối**: *Firstly, Secondly, For example, As a result, However* — [bộ từ nối chung](/writing/task-2/3-khung/#từ-nối-dùng-chung-cho-cả-3-khung)
- **Cụm từ vạn năng**: *get more job opportunities, improve, lead to, save time and money* — từ [10 ý vạn năng](/foundation/universal-phrases/#10-ý-vạn-năng) và [cụm từ hành động](/foundation/universal-phrases/#cụm-từ-hành-động)

**Phân tích:**
- **Nội dung**: Thảo luận **đủ 2 quan điểm** + ý kiến cá nhân **rõ ràng**
- **Bố cục**: Intro → Body 1 → Body 2 → Conclusion — đúng template
- **Từ vựng**: *degree, teamwork, part-time, practical* — đơn giản, đúng B1
- **Số từ**: khoảng **250 từ** — đủ yêu cầu tối thiểu

<TierContent client:load title="🔼 Nâng cao — Bài B1 vs B2 khác nhau ở đâu?">

### So sánh Introduction

**B1:**
> "Nowadays, there is a growing debate about whether university education or work experience is more important for success."

**B2:**
> "In an increasingly competitive job market, the question of whether formal academic qualifications or hands-on professional experience holds greater value has sparked considerable debate."

### Điểm khác biệt chính

| | B1 | B2 |
|---|---|---|
| **Từ vựng** | Đơn giản: *important, learn, help* | Học thuật: *qualifications, hands-on, considerable* |
| **Câu** | Ngắn, rõ ràng | Dài, nhiều mệnh đề |
| **Paraphrase** | Lặp từ trong đề | Diễn đạt lại hoàn toàn |

**Lưu ý:** B1 **không cần** viết như B2. Viết đơn giản, đúng cấu trúc, đủ ý là đạt điểm.
