Dạng **Discuss Both Views** chiếm khoảng **40% đề thi thật** — phổ biến nhất trong các dạng essay.

**Nhận diện:** Đề hỏi hai quan điểm trái chiều: *"Some people think... while others believe..."*

:::note[Xem thêm]
Template đầy đủ ở trang [3 khung cho mọi dạng đề → Khung A](/writing/task-2/3-khung/#khung-a--hai-mặt). Trang này tập trung vào **mẹo nghĩ ý + bài mẫu**.
:::

---

## Cấu trúc chung

| Phần | Nội dung | Số từ |
|------|---------|-------|
| **Introduction** | Giới thiệu chủ đề + nêu 2 quan điểm | 30–40 từ |
| **Body 1** | Quan điểm A + 2 ý + giải thích + ví dụ | 80–110 từ |
| **Body 2** | Quan điểm B + 2 ý + giải thích + ví dụ | 80–110 từ |
| **Conclusion** | Tóm tắt + **quan điểm cá nhân rõ ràng** | 25–35 từ |

Tổng bài: **≥ 250 từ**.

---

## Mẹo nghĩ ý trong 2 phút

Đề luôn cho sẵn **2 quan điểm** → không cần tự nghĩ hướng, chỉ cần nghĩ **ý hỗ trợ** cho mỗi bên.

Với **mỗi quan điểm**, tự hỏi: **"Tại sao người ta nghĩ vậy?"** → chọn 2 ý từ bảng dưới (xem thêm [kho ý tưởng vạn năng](/foundation/universal-phrases/#kho-ý-tưởng--nguyên-nhân--ảnh-hưởng)):

| Nhóm ý | Tiếng Anh | Ví dụ dùng cho |
|--------|-----------|----------------|
| **Tiết kiệm thời gian / tiền** | save time, save money, cheaper | online learning, fast food, technology |
| **Tiện lợi** | convenient, easier | city life, online shopping |
| **Tốt cho sức khỏe** | good for health, reduce stress | countryside, exercise, cooking at home |
| **Kết nối con người** | connect with people, make friends | school, countryside, team sports |
| **Cơ hội việc làm** | job opportunities, higher salary | city life, university education |
| **Tập trung / hiệu quả** | focus better, more effective | school, books, no distractions |

## Ví dụ 1: City vs Countryside

**Đề:** *Some people prefer living in the city, while others think that living in the countryside is better. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.*

### Dàn ý

| Body 1 — City | |
|---|---|
| **Ý 1:** More job opportunities *(nhóm: công việc)* | Many companies, higher salary |
| **Ý 2:** Convenient — save time *(nhóm: tiện lợi)* | Hospitals, shops, schools nearby |

| Body 2 — Countryside | |
|---|---|
| **Ý 1:** Good for health *(nhóm: sức khỏe)* | Fresh air, less pollution, less stress |
| **Ý 2:** Connect with people *(nhóm: quan hệ)* | Neighbours are friendly, people help each other |

### Bài mẫu hoàn chỉnh

<TemplateBox title="Bài mẫu — City vs Countryside">

**Nowadays,** there is a growing debate about where to live. **Some people believe that** living in the city is better, **while others argue that** the countryside is a better place to live. **This essay will discuss both perspectives.**

**On the one hand, some people believe that** living in the city has many advantages. **Firstly,** there are more **job opportunities** in the city because there are many companies and factories. **For example,** young people can easily find a job with a higher salary, **so** they can support their family better. **Secondly,** city life is very **convenient** because hospitals, shops, and schools are all nearby. **This means that** people can **save time** on travelling and have more time for their work or study.

**On the other hand, others argue that** living in the countryside is better. **Firstly,** it is **good for health** because the air is fresh and there is less pollution. **For example,** people in the countryside often feel less stressed than people in the city because they live in a quiet environment. **Secondly,** people in the countryside can **connect with** each other more easily. Neighbours are friendly **and** they often help each other in daily life. **As a result,** people feel happier and less lonely than those who live in big cities.

**In conclusion,** both the city and the countryside have their own advantages. **However,** I personally believe that living in the city is better for young people **because** it offers more job opportunities and is more convenient for daily life.

</TemplateBox>

**Phần in đậm** = cấu trúc cố định (dùng lại cho mọi đề). Phần thường = nội dung thay đổi theo đề.

## Ví dụ 2: Online vs School (chỉ dàn ý)

**Đề:** *Some people think that students should study online, while others believe that studying at school is better. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.*

| Body 1 — Study online | |
|---|---|
| **Ý 1:** Save time *(nhóm: tiện lợi)* | No need to travel → use that time to review lessons |
| **Ý 2:** Cheaper *(nhóm: tiền)* | Lower cost → more students can study |

| Body 2 — Study at school | |
|---|---|
| **Ý 1:** Connect with teachers *(nhóm: quan hệ)* | Ask questions → get answers immediately |
| **Ý 2:** Focus better *(nhóm: học tập)* | Less distracted by social media and games |

→ Lắp vào template [Khung A](/writing/task-2/3-khung/#khung-a--hai-mặt) là xong.

## Cụm từ hữu ích

### Mở đoạn Body

| Cụm từ | Ví dụ câu hoàn chỉnh |
|--------|----------------------|
| *On the one hand,* | On the one hand, some people believe that online learning is better. |
| *On the other hand,* | On the other hand, others argue that schools are more effective. |
| *Firstly, / Secondly,* | Firstly, it helps students save time. |

### Giải thích và ví dụ

| Cụm từ | Ví dụ câu hoàn chỉnh |
|--------|----------------------|
| *This is because...* | This is because students can study at home. |
| *This means that...* | This means that more students can afford it. |
| *For example,* | For example, they can ask questions directly. |

### Kết luận

| Cụm từ | Ví dụ câu hoàn chỉnh |
|--------|----------------------|
| *In conclusion,* | In conclusion, both viewpoints have their own merits. |
| *However, I personally believe that...* | However, I personally believe that schools are better. |

<TierContent client:load title="🔼 Nâng cao — Viết Body chặt chẽ hơn (P-E-E)">

### Cấu trúc P-E-E (Point – Explanation – Example)

1. **Point** (ý chính): *One advantage of exercise is that it improves mental health.*
2. **Explanation** (giải thích): *This is because physical activity releases chemicals that reduce stress.*
3. **Example** (ví dụ): *For instance, studies show that people who exercise regularly have lower rates of depression.*

### Paraphrase mở bài

Không chép lại đề — diễn đạt lại:
- Đề: *"Some people think that technology is harmful."*
- Mở bài: *"In today's digital age, there is growing debate about whether modern technology has a negative impact on society."*

</TierContent>
