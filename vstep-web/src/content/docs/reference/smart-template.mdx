## 6 cụm học 1 lần — dùng cho MỌI part

Trang này **cố ý dùng lại cụm từ** ở nhiều part khác nhau. Bạn chỉ cần thuộc **6 cụm** này là đủ dùng cho cả Speaking P1/P2/P3 và Writing.

| Cụm từ | Dùng ở đâu |
|--------|-----------|
| **It helps me [V]** *(relax / save money / learn new things...)* | P1, P2, P3, Informal |
| **First, ... In addition, ... Finally,** | P3, Informal (3 ý) |
| **This is because...** | P3, Essay — giải thích |
| **For example,** | Tất cả — đưa ví dụ |
| **In conclusion,** | P2, P3, Essay — kết |
| **However,** | Essay — đối lập |

:::tip[Chiến lược học]
Thuộc 6 cụm + 7 lý do ở Part 1 = **đủ dùng cho cả bài thi**. Các part sau chỉ là lắp ráp lại.
:::

## 🗣️ Speaking

### Part 1 — Trả lời câu hỏi

**Nhớ 1 công thức:**

<TemplateBox title="Smart P1 — Chỉ cần nhớ cái này">

**[Trả lời] + [Lý do] + [Ví dụ/Chi tiết]**

*"I like [X]. It helps me [ý tốt — chọn 1 trong bảng dưới]. For example, I [hành động cụ thể]. That's why I enjoy [X]."*

**Ví dụ:**
*"I like reading books. It helps me **relax** after a long day. For example, I usually read before going to sleep. That's why I enjoy reading very much."*

</TemplateBox>

**7 lý do dùng được cho MỌI câu hỏi — chọn 1:**

| Lý do | Cụm từ |
|-------|--------|
| Thư giãn | **it helps me relax** |
| Sức khỏe | **it is good for my health** |
| Kết bạn | **it helps me make new friends** |
| Tiết kiệm tiền | **it helps me save money** |
| Học thêm kiến thức | **it helps me learn new things** |
| Cảm thấy vui | **it makes me feel happy** |
| Cải thiện kỹ năng | **it helps me improve my skills** |

### Part 2 — Chọn 1 trong 3

<TemplateBox title="Smart P2 — Công thức 5 bước">

**Bước 1 — Chọn:** *"I choose [A]."*

**Bước 2 — Lý do 1:** *"It helps me [lý do 1]."* ← **dùng lại cụm P1**

**Bước 3 — Lý do 2:** *"Also, it helps me [lý do 2]."*

**Bước 4 — Loại B và C:**
*"[B] is not a good choice because [nhược điểm 1 câu]."*
*"[C] is also not good because [nhược điểm 1 câu]."*

**Bước 5 — Kết:** *"So I think [A] is the best choice."*

**Ví dụ (chọn học ở nhà):**
*"I choose studying at home. **It helps me** focus easily. Also, **it helps me** save money on transport. The library is not a good choice because it is far from my house. A coffee shop is also not good because it is noisy. So I think studying at home is the best choice."*

</TemplateBox>

---

### Part 3 — Trình bày 3 ý

<TemplateBox title="Smart P3 — Công thức 5 phần">

**Mở:** *"[Topic] is important for many reasons."*

**Ý 1:** *"First, [gợi ý 1]. This is because [lý do đơn giản]."*

**Ý 2:** *"In addition, [gợi ý 2]. For example, [ví dụ ngắn]."*

**Ý 3:** *"Finally, [gợi ý 3]. This helps people to [kết quả tốt]."*

**Kết:** *"In conclusion, [topic] is very important for everyone."*

**Ví dụ (Physical exercise — dùng lại 3 cụm từ bảng P1):**

*"Physical exercise is important for many reasons.*

*First, **it is good for my health**. This is because a healthy body helps us study and work better.*

*In addition, **it helps me relax**. For example, after a long day, playing sports helps me feel happy and comfortable.*

*Finally, **it helps me make new friends**. This is because I can meet many people when I play team sports.*

*In conclusion, physical exercise is very important for everyone."*

</TemplateBox>

**Khi giám khảo hỏi thêm — 3 mẫu trả lời:**

| Câu hỏi | Trả lời |
|--------|---------|
| Đồng ý không? | *"Yes, I agree. I think it is very [important/useful] for [ai đó]."* |
| Ví dụ thêm? | *"Another example is [ví dụ]. It also helps people [lý do]."* |
| Tại Việt Nam? | *"In Vietnam, many people [hành động]. For example, [ví dụ ngắn]."* |

## ✍️ Writing

### Task 1: Thư Informal

<TemplateBox title="Smart Informal — Khung 5 phần">

```
Dear [Tên],

How are you? I hope you are well.
I am writing to [mục đích — 1 câu].

[Nội dung 1 — 2 câu].
[Nội dung 2 — 2 câu].
[Nội dung 3 — 2 câu].

I hope to hear from you soon.

[Tên bạn]
```

**Ví dụ (kể chuyến đi Da Nang — dùng lại cụm P1):**

*Dear Lan,*

*How are you? I hope you are well. I am writing to tell you about my trip to Da Nang last week.*

*First, I visited the beach. **It helps me relax** and feel happy.*
*In addition, I tried many local foods. My favourite was Mi Quang — it was delicious and cheap.*
*Finally, I visited the Marble Mountains. **It helps me learn new things** about history.*

*I hope to hear from you soon.*

*Linh*

</TemplateBox>

**Câu mở theo mục đích — chọn 1:**

| Mục đích | Câu mở |
|---------|--------|
| Kể chuyện | *I am writing to tell you about [sự kiện].* |
| Mời bạn | *I am writing to invite you to [sự kiện].* |
| Lời khuyên | *I am writing to give you some advice about [vấn đề].* |
| Xin lỗi | *I am writing to say sorry for [lỗi].* |

### Task 1: Thư Formal

<TemplateBox title="Smart Formal — Khung 4 phần">

```
Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to [mục đích — 1 câu].

[Nội dung 1 — 2 câu].
[Nội dung 2 — 2 câu].

I would be grateful if you could [yêu cầu].
I look forward to hearing from you.

Yours faithfully,
[Tên]
```

**Ví dụ (phàn nàn phòng khách sạn):**

*Dear Sir/Madam,*

*I am writing to complain about the room I stayed in at your hotel last weekend.*

*First, the room was not clean. There was dust on the furniture and the bathroom was dirty.*
*In addition, the Wi-Fi did not work. I needed it for my work, but I could not connect at all.*

*I would be grateful if you could give me a refund for my stay. I look forward to hearing from you.*

*Yours faithfully,*
*Minh*

</TemplateBox>

**Câu mở theo dạng — chọn 1:**

| Dạng | Câu mở |
|------|--------|
| Yêu cầu | *I am writing to ask for...* |
| Phàn nàn | *I am writing to complain about...* |
| Thông báo | *I am writing to inform you that...* |
| Xin việc | *I am writing to apply for...* |

### Task 2: Essay

<TemplateBox title="Smart Essay — Khung 4 đoạn (≥ 250 từ)">

**Intro (2 câu):**
*"Nowadays, [chủ đề] is something many people talk about. In my opinion, [quan điểm của bạn]."*

**Body 1 — Lý do 1 (3-4 câu):**
*"First, [lý do 1]. This is because [giải thích]. For example, [ví dụ]. As a result, [kết quả]."*

**Body 2 — Lý do 2 (3-4 câu):**
*"In addition, [lý do 2]. This is because [giải thích]. For example, [ví dụ]."*

**Conclusion (2 câu):**
*"In conclusion, I think [nhắc lại quan điểm]. [Chủ đề] is [tính từ] for everyone."*

</TemplateBox>

**Ví dụ thực tế (đề: Online learning vs classroom learning):**

> *Nowadays, online learning is something many people talk about. In my opinion, studying in a classroom is better than studying online.*
>
> *First, studying in a classroom helps students focus better. This is because there are fewer distractions at school. For example, at home, students may use their phones or watch TV. As a result, they cannot focus on their lessons.*
>
> *In addition, face-to-face learning **helps students improve** their communication skills. This is because students can practise speaking with their classmates and teachers. For example, group activities **help students make new friends** and improve their English.*
>
> *In conclusion, I think studying in a classroom is more effective. Traditional education is still very important for everyone.*

:::tip[Mẹo đủ 250 từ]
Mỗi Body viết **3-4 câu** — nếu thiếu từ, thêm 1 câu ví dụ nữa vào Body 2. Không cần Body 3.
:::

## Từ nối tối thiểu cần nhớ

| Chức năng | Dùng |
|----------|------|
| Ý 1 | **First,** |
| Ý 2 | **In addition,** |
| Ý 3 | **Finally,** |
| Bổ sung (khi nói) | **Also,** |
| Giải thích | **This is because...** |
| Ví dụ | **For example,** |
| Kết | **In conclusion,** |
| Đối lập | **However,** |
