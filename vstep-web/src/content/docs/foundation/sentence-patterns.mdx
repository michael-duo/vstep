## Cơ bản — 6 cấu trúc phải biết

### 1. It makes me / helps me + V ★★★

**Nghĩa:** Nó khiến tôi / giúp tôi làm gì

> *Do you like watching TV?*
> Yes, of course. I really enjoy watching TV because **it makes me relax** after a long working day.

> *What type of transportation do you usually use?*
> My favorite vehicle is a motorbike because **it helps me travel** to work actively.

---

### 2. It is a good / effective way to + V ★★★

**Nghĩa:** Đây là một cách tốt để làm gì

> *Do you like reading books?*
> Definitely, **reading books is an effective way** for me to entertain as well as learn more knowledge.

> *What is a useful item you usually use every day?*
> I cannot live without my smartphone because **it is an effective way to** contact everybody and update information.

---

### 3. I find it + adj + to + V ★★★

**Nghĩa:** Tôi thấy thật (như thế nào) để làm gì

> *Do you like to live in your hometown?*
> Yes, **I find it comfortable to live** in Dalat where I can enjoy the fresh air.

> *What is your favorite food?*
> Salad is my cup of tea. **I find it good for** my health and my shape.

---

### 4. It is + adj + for sb + to + V ★★★

**Nghĩa:** Thật (như thế nào) cho ai đó để làm gì

> *Do you like shopping on the Internet?*
> Yes. **It is convenient and flexible for me to** stay at home and surf the Internet.

> *Do you usually travel by public transportation?*
> **It is too tiring for me to** wait for the bus every morning, so I prefer using my motorbike.

---

### 5. Thanks to A, I can... ★★★

**Nghĩa:** Nhờ có A, tôi có thể...

> **Thanks to** my smartphone, **I can** connect with people all over the world.

> **Thanks to** my parents' support, **I can** focus on my studies.

---

### 6. The thing I like most about... is... ★★★

**Nghĩa:** Điều tôi thích nhất về ... là ...

> **The thing I like most about** travelling **is that** I can discover new cultures.

> **The thing I like most about** my university **is** its dynamic environment.

### 7. S + allows me/sb + to + V ★★

**Nghĩa:** Cho phép tôi/ai đó làm gì

> Learning English **allows me to** connect with people around the world.

> Working from home **allows me to** save commuting time.

---

### 8. It takes + time/effort/money + to + V ★★

**Nghĩa:** Tốn thời gian / công sức / tiền để làm gì

> **It takes time** for people to think about protecting the environment.

> **It takes a lot of effort** to stay healthy.

---

### 9. S + encourage + sb + to do st ★★

**Nghĩa:** Khuyến khích ai đó làm gì

> My parents always **encourage me to** follow my dreams.

> The teacher **encouraged us to** speak English in class.

---

### 10. S + enable + sb + to do st ★★

**Nghĩa:** Làm cho việc gì có thể diễn ra

> The new technology **enables faster** data processing.

> Social networking **enables users to** broaden their relationships.

---

### 11. Provide sb with sth / Provide sth for sb ★

**Nghĩa:** Cung cấp cho ai cái gì

> The hotel **provides guests with** free Wi-Fi.

> The company **provides health insurance for** employees.

---

### 12. Prevent sb/sth from V-ing ★

**Nghĩa:** Ngăn ai/cái gì làm việc gì

> The heavy rain **prevented us from** going out.

> Nothing can **prevent her from** achieving her goals.

---

### 13. Equip sb with sth ★

**Nghĩa:** Trang bị cho ai cái gì (dụng cụ, kỹ năng, kiến thức)

> The school **equips students with** modern computers.

> The training course will **equip you with** the skills needed for the job.

---

### 14. Play a crucial/important role in + N/V-ing ★

**Nghĩa:** Đóng vai trò quan trọng trong một quá trình/hoạt động

> Education **plays a crucial part in** shaping a child's future.

> Technology **plays an important role in** modern communication.
