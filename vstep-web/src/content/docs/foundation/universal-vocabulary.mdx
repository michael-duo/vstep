Những từ này **xuất hiện trong mọi bài thi** — dùng được cho bất kỳ chủ đề nào trong Speaking và Writing.

:::tip[Cách dùng]
Ghép với [cấu trúc vạn năng](/foundation/universal-structures/) + [cụm từ vạn năng](/foundation/universal-phrases/):

*The main **advantage** of travelling is that it helps me **reduce** stress.*
*I think education plays an **important** role in **society**.*
:::

---

## Danh từ — nói về lợi / hại / giải pháp

| Từ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|----|-------|-------|
| **advantage** | ưu điểm, lợi thế | One **advantage** of living in a city is convenience. |
| **disadvantage** | nhược điểm, bất lợi | The main **disadvantage** is the high cost of living. |
| **benefit** | lợi ích | There are many **benefits** of regular exercise. |
| **drawback** | hạn chế, mặt trái | One **drawback** of online learning is lack of interaction. |
| **solution** | giải pháp | Education is the best **solution** for reducing poverty. |
| **consequence** | hậu quả | Pollution has serious **consequences** for our health. |
| **reason** | lý do | There are several **reasons** why I prefer living in a city. |
| **opinion** | ý kiến | In my **opinion**, family is the most important thing. |
| **environment** | môi trường | We should protect the **environment** for future generations. |
| **society** | xã hội | Education contributes to the development of **society**. |

---

## Tính từ — mô tả mức độ quan trọng

| Từ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|----|-------|-------|
| **important** | quan trọng | English is very **important** for my future career. |
| **necessary** | cần thiết | Exercise is **necessary** for a healthy lifestyle. |
| **convenient** | tiện lợi | Shopping online is very **convenient**. |
| **effective** | hiệu quả | This is an **effective** way to improve your English. |
| **serious** | nghiêm trọng | Air pollution is a **serious** problem in big cities. |
| **harmful** | có hại | Smoking is **harmful** to your health. |
| **useful** | hữu ích | Smartphones are very **useful** for students. |
| **popular** | phổ biến | Social media is very **popular** among young people. |
| **complicated** | phức tạp | The process is too **complicated** for most people. |
| **time-consuming** | tốn thời gian | Cooking from scratch can be very **time-consuming**. |

### Danh từ nâng cao

| Từ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|----|-------|-------|
| **awareness** | nhận thức | We need to raise **awareness** about pollution. |
| **responsibility** | trách nhiệm | It is our **responsibility** to protect the environment. |
| **opportunity** | cơ hội | Travelling gives us the **opportunity** to learn new things. |
| **influence** | ảnh hưởng | Social media has a strong **influence** on young people. |
| **development** | sự phát triển | Technology contributes to the **development** of society. |

### Tính từ nâng cao

| Từ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|----|-------|-------|
| **significant** | đáng kể | Technology has made **significant** changes to our lives. |
| **essential** | thiết yếu | Communication skills are **essential** for any job. |
| **affordable** | giá phải chăng | Public transport is more **affordable** than driving. |
| **sustainable** | bền vững | We need **sustainable** solutions for energy. |
| **efficient** | hiệu quả (về hiệu suất) | Online learning is more time-**efficient**. |

### Trạng từ nâng cao

| Từ | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|----|-------|-------|
| **significantly** | đáng kể | Technology has **significantly** changed our lives. |
| **effectively** | hiệu quả | Exercise helps **effectively** reduce stress. |
| **increasingly** | ngày càng | People are **increasingly** aware of environmental issues. |
