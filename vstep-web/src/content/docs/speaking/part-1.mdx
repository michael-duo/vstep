## Cách mở ý — 3 kỹ thuật

### Cách 1: Ngũ cung (dễ nhất)

Trả lời bằng cách thêm **What / Who / Where / When / Why**:

> *Do you read books?*
> Yes, I do. I often read **comic books** *(what)* **with my sister** *(who)* **at home** *(where)* **every evening** *(when)* **because it helps me relax** *(why)*.

### Cách 2: Tập trung WHY — đưa lý do

Dùng các cụm nối lý do:

| Cụm nối | Nghĩa |
|---------|-------|
| **Simply because...** | Đơn giản vì... |
| **Thanks to (chủ đề), ...** | Nhờ có... |
| **One advantage of ... is...** | Một lợi ích của... là... |
| **...is a great way to V** | ...là cách tuyệt vời để... |

> *What do you do in your free time?*
> Well, when it comes to my free time, I would say that watching YouTube is my favourite activity. **Simply because** I can learn many interesting things. **Thanks to** that, my English has improved a lot.

### Cách 3: Đối lập — While / Although

> **While** some people prefer big cities, I still think the countryside is better because the air is fresher and life is more peaceful.

> **Although** cooking takes time, I find it very enjoyable because it helps me relax.

Mở rộng câu bằng **who / which / where**:

- I live in Da Nang, **which** is a beautiful coastal city.
- My mother, **who** is a teacher, always encourages me to study hard.
- I study at a university **where** there are many good teachers.

## 3 Template trả lời nhanh

<TemplateBox title="T1: Ý chính + Lý do + Ai/Đâu/Khi nào (dễ nhất)">

*Well, I'd say that... This is mainly because... I usually do it with... at... and I often...*

> "Well, I'd say that my favourite hobby is reading. This is mainly because it helps me relax after a long day. I usually read alone, at home in my bedroom, and I often do it at night before going to sleep."

</TemplateBox>

<TemplateBox title="T2: Ý chính + Lý do + Ví dụ">

*Well, I'd say that... This is mainly because... For example,...*

> "Well, I'd say that I rely on my smartphone a lot. Mainly because it helps me stay organised. For example, I use it to manage my schedule and look up information quickly."

</TemplateBox>

<TemplateBox title="T3: Ý chính + Cảm xúc + Tương lai">

*Well,... It makes me feel... In the future, I'd like to...*

> "Well, I prefer to travel with my family. It makes me feel comfortable and safe. In the future, I'd like to travel to Japan because I'm fascinated by its culture."

</TemplateBox>

<TierContent client:load title="🔼 Nâng cao — T4 và T5">

<TemplateBox title="T4: Hiện tại vs Quá khứ + Lý do">

*Right now, I... But in the past, I used to... The change happened because...*

> "Right now, I wake up at 6 every morning, but in the past, I used to get up at 8. The change happened because my new job starts early."

</TemplateBox>

<TemplateBox title="T5: Ý chính + Tần suất + Kết quả">

*Well,... I do it (tần suất)... As a result,...*

> "Well, I've started learning English. I do it for at least 30 minutes every day. As a result, I can feel my speaking skills improving gradually."

</TemplateBox>

</TierContent>

## Chủ đề hay gặp nhất (từ đề thi thật)

**Nhóm Cuộc sống hàng ngày:** Daily routine, Hobbies, Favourite food, Cooking, Walking, Shopping habits

**Nhóm Học tập & Phát triển:** Favourite subject, English learning, Reading habits

**Nhóm Xã hội & Văn hóa:** Festivals & holidays, Music, Travelling, Hometown, Pets

**Nhóm Khác:** Birthday celebrations, Celebrities, Marriage
