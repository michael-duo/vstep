Mỗi bài mẫu được phân tích theo **cấu trúc vạn năng đã dùng** để bạn thấy cách áp dụng vào bài nói thật.

---

## Part 1 — Bài mẫu

**Câu hỏi:** *"Do you enjoy travelling? Why or why not?"*

<SampleAnswer client:load title="Part 1 — Chủ đề Travelling" score="B1">

"Yes, I really enjoy travelling. This is mainly because **it helps me relax** and escape from my daily routine. For example, last year I went on a trip to Da Nang with my family. **Thanks to** that trip, I could discover a new city, try local seafood, and take many beautiful photos. In the future, I'd like to travel to other countries, especially Japan, because I'm fascinated by its culture and food."

**Cấu trúc được dùng:**
- ✓ **It helps me** + V *(helps me relax)*
- ✓ **Thanks to** A, I can... *(Thanks to that trip, I could...)*

</SampleAnswer>

---

**Câu hỏi:** *"What kind of food do you like?"*

<SampleAnswer client:load title="Part 1 — Chủ đề Food" score="B1">

"Well, I'd say that my favourite food is pho. Mainly because it's both delicious and healthy — it has plenty of vegetables and a flavourful broth. I usually eat it with my family on weekends at a small local restaurant near my house. **I find it** very comforting, especially on cold mornings. In my opinion, pho is the best representation of Vietnamese cuisine."

**Cấu trúc được dùng:**
- ✓ **I find it** + adj + to V *(I find it very comforting)*
- ✓ Template 1: Ý chính + Lý do + Ai/Đâu/Khi nào

</SampleAnswer>

## Part 2 — Bài mẫu

**Đề bài:** *Your group is deciding where to study for the upcoming exam. Options: A. At home | B. At the library | C. At a café*

<SampleAnswer client:load title="Part 2 — Địa điểm ôn thi" score="B1">

"I have thought about the three options and I would choose Option B — studying at the library.

The first reason is that the library is very quiet and focused. This means that I can concentrate better on my studies without distractions. For example, whenever I study at the library, I can finish my work much faster than at home.

Moreover, the library **provides students with** all the resources they need — books, computers, and Wi-Fi. This **saves** a lot of time.

As for Option A — studying at home, I don't think it's the best choice because it's easy to get distracted by family members or my phone.

Similarly, Option C — a café is not ideal for studying because it can be noisy and expensive.

Therefore, I strongly believe that the library is the most suitable place to study for the exam."

**Cấu trúc được dùng:**
- ✓ **Provide sb with sth** *(provides students with all the resources)*
- ✓ **Save** time/money/effort *(saves a lot of time)*

</SampleAnswer>

## Part 3 — Bài mẫu

**Đề bài:** *Talk about the importance of physical exercise. Ideas: health benefits / mental well-being / social connections*

<SampleAnswer client:load title="Part 3 — Lợi ích của tập thể dục" score="B1">

"Today I'd like to talk about the importance of physical exercise. I believe that exercise **plays an important role** in our daily lives.

First of all, regular exercise brings many health benefits. This is because it strengthens our immune system and helps prevent diseases. For example, people who exercise regularly are less likely to suffer from heart disease or obesity.

In addition, exercise **is good for** our mental well-being. **It helps me relax** and reduce stress after a long day of studying. For instance, I always feel much better and more energetic after a 30-minute run.

Finally, exercising can help us build social connections. Playing team sports or joining a fitness class **allows us to** meet new people and make friends. **Thanks to** this, we can **improve** our teamwork and communication **skills** at the same time.

In conclusion, I strongly believe that physical exercise is essential not just for our body, but also for our mind and social life."

**Cấu trúc được dùng:**
- ✓ **Play an important role in** + N *(plays an important role in our daily lives)*
- ✓ **Is good for** my health *(is good for our mental well-being)*
- ✓ **It helps me** + V *(helps me relax)*
- ✓ **Allows sb to** V *(allows us to meet new people)*
- ✓ **Thanks to** A, I can... *(Thanks to this, we can improve...)*
- ✓ **Improve my skills** *(improve our teamwork and communication skills)*

</SampleAnswer>

### Cùng câu hỏi, điểm khác nhau

**Câu hỏi:** "Do you enjoy cooking?"

**Bài B1 (đạt):**
> "Yes, I enjoy cooking. **It helps me relax** after work. For example, I cook dinner for my family every weekend. In the future, I'd like to learn more recipes."
>
> Cấu trúc: ✓ It helps me + V

**Bài B2 (cao hơn):**
> "Absolutely — cooking is one of my favourite ways to unwind. What I particularly enjoy is the creative process of combining ingredients and experimenting with different flavours. Not only does it relax me, but it also gives me a sense of achievement when the dish turns out well."
>
> Cấu trúc: ✓ Not only... but also | ✓ Từ vựng nâng cao: *unwind, experimenting, ingredients*
