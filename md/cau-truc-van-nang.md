# **CÁC CẤU TRÚC “VẠN NĂNG” CHO SPEAKING/ WRITING VSTEP**

*Áp dụng cho mọi đề \- Không lo bí*

## **1\. IT MAKES ME / HELPS ME \+ V**

Nó khiến tôi/ giúp tôi làm gì

* Do you like watching TV?

Yes, of course. I really enjoy watching TV because **it makes me relax after a long working day.**

* What type of transportation do you usually use?

My favorite vehicle is a motorbike because **it helps me travel to work actively** without waiting for a bus or train.

## **2\. IT IS A GOOD/ AN EFFECTIVE WAY TO V**

Nó là 1 cách tốt để làm gì

* Do you like reading books?

Definitely, **reading books is an effective way for me to entertain as well as learn more knowledge.**

* What is a useful item you usually use every day?

Well, I cannot live without my smartphone daily because **it is an effective way to contact everybody and update the latest information**.

## **3\. I find it \+ adj \+ to V**

Tôi thấy nó như thế nào để làm gì

* Do you like to live in your hometown?

Yes, **I find it comfortable to live in Dalat** where I can enjoy the fresh air and wonderful weather.

* What is your favorite food?

Well, salad is my cup of tea. I can eat salad every single day cause **I find it good for my health and my shape.**

## **4\. The thing I like/ love/ dislike most about…is…**

Điều tôi thích/ k thích nhất về cái gì đó là…

* How often do you travel?

I always try my best to travel at least once a year in order to recharge my battery. **The thing I like most about travelling is that** I can discover new cultures of different destinations.

* What do you like about your university?

**The thing I like most about my university** is its dynamic environment with many activities and projects.

## **5\. IT is \+ adj \+ for sb \+ to V**

Nó như thế nào cho ai để làm gì

* Do you like shopping on the Internet?

Yes, of course. I prefer buying items online because **it is convenient and flexible for me to stay at home and surf the Internet**.

* Do you usually travel by public transportation?

To be honest, **it is too tiring for me to wait for the bus every morning**, so I prefer using my motorbike.

## **6\. It/ S \+ allows me/ sb \+ to V**

Cho phép tôi/ ai đó làm gì

* Why do you learn English?

English brings me many benefits. First, **learning English allows me to connect with people around the world.**

* What type of jobs do you like to work?

I really want to work remotely because **working from home allows me to save commuting time.**

## **7\. Thanks to A, I can…**

Nhờ có A, tôi có thể…

* What item do you usually use for your study?

Well, the thing I always bring with me is my smartphone. **Thanks to it, I can connect with people all over the world.**

* Who do you love the most in your family?

My mother is the most important person in my life. **Thanks to her, I can find my life easier and happier.**

## **8\. IT takes \+ time/ effort/ money \+ to V**

Nó tốn thời gian/ công sức/ tiền bạc để làm gì

* Do you care about environmental protection?

Yes, of course. I know **it takes time for people to think about protecting the environment** in their daily activities, but…

* What do you do to improve your health?

Well, now I’m trying to quit my bad habit of staying up late at night. It’s difficult because **it takes a lot of effort to stay healthy.**

## **9\. S \+ encourage \+ sb \+ to do st**

Khuyến khích ai đó làm gì

* The teacher **encouraged us to speak English in class**.

* My parents always **encourage me** **to follow my dreams**.

## **10\. S \+ enable \+ sb \+ to do st**

Làm cho việc gì/cái gì có thể diễn ra.

* The new technology **enables faster data processing**.

* Social networking **enables users to broaden their relationships** easily.

## **11\.  Provide sb with sth/ Provide sth for sb** 

Cung cấp cho ai cái gì/ Cung cấp cái gì cho ai

* The hotel **provides guests** **with free Wi-Fi**.  
* The company **provides** **health insurance for employees**.

## **12\.  Prevent sb/ sth from Ving**

Ngăn ai/cái gì làm việc gì

* The heavy rain prevented us **from going out**.

* Nothing can prevent her **from achieving her goals**.


## **13\.  Equip sb with sth**

Trang bị cho ai cái gì (dụng cụ, kỹ năng, kiến thức…)

* The school equips students **with modern computers**.

* The training course will equip you **with the skills needed for the job**.

## **14\.  Play a crucial part in \+ N/V-ing hoặc Play an important role in \+ N/V-ing**

Đóng vai trò quan trọng trong một quá trình/hoạt động.

* Education plays a crucial part in **shaping a child’s future**.

* Technology plays an important role in **modern communication**.

## **Speaking Part 1 Tips**

* Bỏ túi các từ / cụm từ theo chủ đề (10–15 words/topic)  
* Bỏ túi những cấu trúc “vạn năng”  
* Lắp ghép những từ vựng phù hợp vào các cấu trúc vạn năng, cố gắng nói trôi chảy
