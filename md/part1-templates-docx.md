**Sample:           What do you do in your free time?**

Well, when it comes to my leisure time, I would say that watching videos on Youtube, especially beauty and fashion videos, is right up my street, as I’m always keen on learning more about those topics. Plus, only by trying to understand and imitate famous Youtubers can I practice my English listening and speaking skills. Thanks to that, my English score at school has been improved. So I guess it would be more exact to say that watching YouTube videos is really helpful to my study, not just my spare time activity.
