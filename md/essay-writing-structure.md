# Essay Writing

## Types of Essays

1. Advantages and Disadvantages (Benefits and Drawbacks) (Upsides and Downsides) (Pros and cons)
2. Causes and Effects/ Problems
3. Problems (Causes) - Solutions
4. Agree and Disagree
5. Discuss both views and give your own opinion
