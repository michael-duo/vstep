**Templates triển khai Speaking Part 1**

1. **Template 1: Idea \+ Reason \+ (Who \- Where \- When)** (Ý chính \+ lý do \+ với ai ở đâu khi nào)  
   *Well, I’d say that… This is mainly because… I usually do it with… at ..(place)...(time)*

**Ví dụ 1 \- Topic: Hobbies**

**Answer:**  
 “Well, I’d say that my favorite hobby is reading. Mainly because it helps me relax after a long day. I usually read alone, at home in my bedroom, and I often do it at night before going to sleep.”

**Ví dụ 2 \- Topic: Eating habits/ Food** 

**Answer:**

“Well, I’d say that I love eating Vietnamese noodles. Mainly because they're tasty and convenient. I usually eat them with my colleagues, at a small restaurant near my workplace, and I often have them for lunch.”

2. **Template 2: Basic Idea \+ Reason \+ Example** (Ý chính \+ lý do \+ ví dụ)  
   *Well, I’d say that …This is mainly because…For example,...*  
   

**Ví dụ 1 – Topic: Hobbies:**  
“Well, I’d say that my favorite hobby is reading. Mainly because it helps me relax after work. For example, I usually read around 20 minutes before going to bed.”

**Ví dụ 2- Topic: Technology:**  
 “Well, I’d say that I rely on my smartphone a lot. Mainly because it helps me stay organized. For example, I use it to manage my schedule and notes.”

3. **Template 3: Basic idea \+ Feeling \+ Future intention** (Ý chính \+ Cảm xúc \+ Tương lai)  
   *Well,...It makes me feel…In the future, I’d like to…*

**Ví dụ 1 – Topic: Reading**  
“Well, I am a big fan of reading. It makes me feel relaxed. In the future, I’d like to read more self-help books.”

**Ví dụ 2 – Topic: Travelling**  
“Well, I prefer to travel with others. It makes me feel comfortable and safe. In the future, I’d like to travel to other countries with my family.”

4. **Template 4: Now vs. Past \+ Reason** (Hiện tại với quá khứ \+ Lý do)  
   *Right now, I… But in the past, I used to… The change happened because…*

**Ví dụ 1- Topic: Daily routine:**

“Right now, I wake up early, but in the past, I used to get up late. The change happened because my work requires an early start.”

**Ví dụ 2 \- Topic: Eating habits**

“Right now, I eat healthy meals, but in the past, I used to eat fast food a lot. The change happened because I wanted to have better health.”

5. **Template 5: Basic Idea \+ Frequency \+ Result** (Ý chính \+ Tần suất \+ Kết quả)  
   *Well,...I do it (tần suất/ thời gian)...As a result,...*

**Ví dụ 1 \- Topic: Learning English**

“Well, I started a new habit of learning English. I do it for at least 30 minutes a day. As a result, I can make progress in my English skills, especially speaking.”
